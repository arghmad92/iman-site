**What I want in a concealer?**

1\. Able to conceal my dark circles

2\. Easy to blend, easy to apply

3\. Minimal creasing - Yes I know, susah nak cari concealer yang tak crease untuk under eyes, (tak crease - tak berkedut kedut ikut lines kat bawah mata tu)

4\. Not drying

The swatches

  

  

So today, I am going to compare some drugstore concealer that I've tried, what I love about it, what I don't like about it.

  

**Maybelline Pure Concealer Mineral**

  

This is one of the first concealer that I've tried and loved. There are only two shades available. I bought the lightest shade. Easy to blend, enough to cover my dark circles. Tapi rasanya yang ni tak jual dah sebab dah tak nampak.

  

I don't have the full review but you can read it here: [MY BASIC DRUGSTORE MAKEUP ESSENTIALS FOR BEGINNERS](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2016/04/my-basic-makeup-essentials-for-beginners.html)

  

_**Price**: RM 18.90 for 5.5mL (RM3.44/mL)_

  

  

**Maybelline Fit Me Concealer**

  

I love this, easy to blend, good coverage, minimal creasing. Tapi the shade macam a bit orange-ish for me. Not brightening enough, sebab I suka dia brightening, nampak la berseri sikit. Tak nampak penat sangat mata tu.

  

Full review: [MAYBELLINE FIT ME CONCEALER REVIEW](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/09/maybelline-fit-me-concealer-review.html)

  

_**Price**: RM27.90 for 6.8 mL (RM4.1/mL)_

  

**Catrice Liquid Camouflage High Coverage Concealer**

  

Gosh, I hate the smell. I don't know why. Dia wangi but I don't like it. Depends on hidung masing masing la. Ada yang suka. But not for me. Try la bau dulu.

  

But the performance is great. Coverage is good, easy to blend, minimal creasing, brightening effect and cheap. Cuma kalau I pakai, kena tahan nafas sikit :(

  

Full review: [CATRICE LIQUID CAMOUFLAGE HIGH COVERAGE CONCEALER REVIEW](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/09/catrice-camouflage-concealer.html)

  

_**Price**: RM19.90 for 5 mL (RM3.98/mL)_

  

**Maybelline Instant Age Rewind**

  

I love this at first, then I hate it. Sebab dah rosak hahaha. Packaging dia OMG I tak suka. Again, this one depends on people. Certain people memang suka gilaaaaaa this concealer. I menyampah sebab penutup dia kecik gila and senang gila, sponge dia cepat rosak and susah nak apply. Performance, great. Coverage ok, shade ok, minimal creasing, I suka formula dia.

  

Tapi packaging dia, hmmmmm.

  

Full review: [MAYBELLINE INSTANT AGE REWIND ERASER DARK CIRCLES TREATMENT CONCEALER REVIEW](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/06/maybelline-instant-age-rewind-eraser.html)

  

_**Price**: RM34.60 for 6 mL (RM5.77/mL)_

  

**Wet n Wild Photofocus Concealer**

  

Good coverage, shade that matches my skin tone and brightening, easy to blend, not drying. Tapi, cepat crease. Pakai kejap je ok. Kalau lama lama, kemain tau crease dia.

  

Full review: [WET N WILD PHOTOFOCUS CONCEALER REVIEW](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/09/wet-n-wild-photofocus-concealer-review.html)

_**Price**: RM24.90 for 8.5mL (RM2.93/mL)_

  

**Summary:**

  

**Performance-wise**: I suka Maybelline Instant Age Rewind, Maybelline Pure Mineral, Catrice Liquid Camouflage High Coverage Concealer

  

**Packaging-wise**: All, except Maybelline Instant Age Rewind

  

**Price-wise**: Wet n Wild Photofocus is the cheapest (RM2.93/mL) and yang paling mahal is Maybelline Instant Age Rewind  (RM5.77/mL) kalau compared to price and quantity.

  

**Overall**: I'll go for Wet n Wild Photofocus despite dia cepat crease kalau nak keluar kejap kejap je. I'll go for Catrice Liquid Camouflage High Coverage Concealer tapi kena tahan nafas bila pakai. I pakai Maybelline Fit Me Concealer kalau setakat light no-makeup makeup je. Yang paling perfect? Tak ada lagi haha.

  

  

Hope this helps! Thanks for reading.

  

(Disclaimer: I bought both of this products using my own money. This is not a paid review. Every skin is different. What works for me, might not work for you. Not everything works the same for everyone. This is just my personal experience)
