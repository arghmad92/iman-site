This is actually not a new product. Dah release lama dah and I bought this like 4 months ago rasanya. This is all because I saw Vivy Yusof used this in one of her youtube video untuk cover dark circles so I macam yes I need this haha

So the main reason I bought this is because my dark circles are getting worst, and I malas nak pakai eye cream, and I need an instant fix for my dark circles. Concealer biasa pun dah tak mampu nak tutup aib ini.....

  

  

  

[![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjzAqoEWiqEkcBP9yiMrFlX4mjHTgGazGSwzoM3LS_q-Z_eF6dhe8kEYudEhTno8slmeNz-yM4OfwjG80-x4gyo4cOfnKoEUuXFvR6AkSnxTPKLZYXJBYwIU-JrJWtoWm4AGUFB-62ufMxK/s640/Photo+13-05-2018%252C+4+56+27+PM.jpg)

  

I bought this at Sephora KLCC for RM110 tapi dapat discount sebab I redeem my membership discount amenda tah I pun lupa. It claims to brighten dark circles to look wide awake even if you're running on empty. Lightweight formula illuminates as it blurs flaws for a correcting effect. Anything yang boleh tutup dark circles, yes please.

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhXAhu6ylyHmyJ51u0_-kcgalukeTSHHIpbyEtjn1Z-Hg2waRidHC3CCsOZjku6O8U2fwr6DqtXJpVwM5B8CpgrkJ0CupMfMGR0Tp2bwlaKYloKy2HHtSGAzFqLztwtnj9RoMysXbZNTWrd/s640/Screen+Shot+2018-05-13+at+5.01.01+PM.png)

  

It comes with five different shades:

  

**Green** is for counteract spot redness, including blemishes and broken blood vessels. 

**Lavender** is for balance out sallowness and helps neutralize skin with a yellow undertone. 

**Pink** disguises dark circles, particularly on fair skin tones. Also works well as a highlighter and creates a lifted appearance. 

**Yellow** is perfect for concealing purple-hued dark circles or brightening lackluster complexions.

**Peach** works great for dark circles, dark spots, veins and deep discoloration. It’s ideal for blue-hued dark circles, typically seen in deeper skin tones.

**Deep peach** is a more intense version of Peach, and masks dark discoloration like circles and sun spots. Tip: Great for deeper or bronzier skin tones!

  

 I was contemplating between pink and peach, tak tahu nak pilih shade yang mana for dark circles so I picked pink sebab risau peach is too dark for me. And nasib baik I did a great choice cause the pink shade works really well for my skin tone.

  

  

[![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhDuYSAXhtrZqq_NaQ_hm9GY6OZGZJ7JEvgTdPlx-tQvamwLizJnWoVD0Z0TaDcyCVcsbk3l120Eso056ND5oY9sZ1KakkdI7s3fn4ibsZDMQNh1TYTWx9iyo5O6cnq1syI8g9Nqw46G8bb/s640/Photo+13-05-2018%252C+4+56+49+PM.jpg)

  

It has a creamy texture that blends really well and beautiful. Tak patchy, tak bercapuk. I just apply it under my eyes, blend it out using a sponge lepastu tambah my normal concealer. This is not a concealer, yang ni just a colour correcting supaya under eyes nampak lagi bright and boleh conceal the dark circles. Just an extra step supaya nampak muka tu tak penat sangat, fresh and awake.

  

The packaging is like a normal concealer, a transparent glass tube with a wand and doe-foot applicator. I ada dengan a few people yang dia punya applicator tercabut and patah since it's quite flexible. So far I guna ok lagi. We'll see nanti macam mana. Kalau patah, I'll update here haha.

  

So far, I really love this corrector thingy, one of the best investment ever, and always nampak muka bermaya sikit walaupun dark circles teruk, still boleh cover dengan jayanya hehehe. It did a very good job in canceling my dark circles out And yes, I would repurchase kalau habis, unless I jumpa option lain yang lagi best. Would really love to try NYX or LA Girl to compare with this Urban Decay. Maybe soon.

  

  

  

The coverage is quite good, tapi not enough to use it alone. I pernah try pakai this correcting fluid only, without concealer and buat kulit nampak pelik sikit, depends on your skin lah. So it's better to use a concealer on top, for me. Again, this is just a colour corrector, the texture is lighter than a normal concealer and doesn't have a full coverage as a concealer.

  

(Disclaimer: I bought both of this products using my own money. This is not a paid review. Every skin is different. What works for me, might not work for you. Not everything works the same for everyone. This is just my personal experience)
