**What is SGShop?**

SGshop is a cross border shopping and shipping e-commerce, for Malaysians to buy products from _China and ship to Malaysia_ with our website and mobile apps. SGshop’s services are widely used by consumers across cities and kampung, as well as business owners in Malaysia.

## **How does Buy-For-Me works:** 

Step 1: Search product with photo, keyword or Taobao.com URL. 

  

Step 2: Add product to cart and checkout. This is the first payment for Buy-For-Me, which the payment will be used to purchase product from China Seller.
