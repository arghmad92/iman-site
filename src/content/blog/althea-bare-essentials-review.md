The Contour Cleanser helps to promote blood circulation, and reduces bloating and swelling for a defined, enviably slim face shape.

**My thoughts:**

It has a refreshing scent and the texture is cream which is good for my dry skin. The ingredients does not contains SLS and alcohol which I would normally avoid, however it does have menthol and fragrance in it, which may not be suitable for sensitive skin. Luckily, my skin didn't experience any irritation or discomfort. I also didn't feel any tightness or dryness after using this cleanser.

  

[!

](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEj_1_zB-DikqTh5FNLCZnbo2pVPQZv4c6vtZ3y9am7Xeey9vpbzexqfhVbyl88DpzGcdSl-qGVS5geLvzUCUZuhRpyaOoD10Ogi_ozadnVOsyUP0ejVFhfjMT6Utj6fKVhHE71ujzkOJsBS/s1600/Photo+12-04-2018%252C+11+08+42+AM.jpg)

  

I also tested the pH of this cleanser since I believe that a good cleanser should have a low pH so it will not disrupt our skin barrier. The average pH of human skin is pH4-6 so it is best to make sure we use a pH balanced skincare. Based on my test paper, this cleanser turns out to be around pH8 which is quite high. This cleanser also contains micro beads to exfoliate our skin, which makes me feel scared to use this daily as I'm afraid my skin will be over-exfoliate since I have dry skin. For oily skin, I think it's okay to use this daily.

  

**Who will love this?**

People with not so sensitive skin and love refreshing scent

**My thought:**

As usual, the scent is really refreshing which I really love. The texture is quite thick, not like your regular watery toner. I like the texture. It absorbs beautifully on my skin and doesn't leave a sticky or greasy feeling. I feel my skin is well hydrated enough and soft.

  

However, I do notice that alcohol is the forth ingredient on the list. Surprisingly, it didn't cause me any irritation or redness. I guess there's a lot of moisturizing ingredients so macam tak rasa sangat the drying effect of alcohol which is good because normally I'll avoid any toner with high level of alcohol. If you have very sensitive skin, you might want to consider this.

I tested the pH value and the pH value is around pH6 which is suitable for our skin. I also love the ingredients. It contains centella which is good for soothing, especially if you have redness or red acne scars. Chia Seeds (Salvia Hispanica Seed Extract) is a rich source of fatty acids and a good antioxidant. Sometimes I enjoy using this toner for the 3 skin methods, which means you apply this toner for 3 layers. I love the hydrating effect!

**My thought?**

I love the ingredient lists, there's a lot of emollients which is good for drier skin. The texture is quite thick since it's a cream. If you have oily skin, you may want to apply this very thinly to avoid any greasy feeling. I love how this product absorbs into my skin, and it makes my skin feels really soft and smooth. Although it contains fragrance, (all three products contains fragrance but it's very low), it didn't cause any irritation or discomfort.

  

However, I don't really fancy jar packaging. All the antioxidants will be unstable once exposed to light and air. So macam, sayanglah moisturizer ada banyak antioxidants tapi dah tak powerful sangat. Plus. it is unhygienic to dip your fingers inside the product. Please use a spatula, okay? I don't remember whether they provide a spatula or not. Kalau ada, maybe I dah hilangkan kot haha so kena cari spatula baru. This is just my personal preference.

  

**Who will love this?**

People with drier skin type.

  

  

  

**Overall**

I did enjoy using this Bare Essentials because the scent is very refreshing, the design is very pretty and minimal. I like looking at it and it looks nice on my vanity hehehe. However, I do hope that Althea will come out with a sunscreen as well since I believe sunscreen is also a very important step in our skincare routine. Overall, worth to try! My fav is the Priming Water <3
