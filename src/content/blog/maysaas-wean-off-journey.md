But I decided to share it on my blog too :)  
  
 1️⃣ Kurangkan sikit sikit. Sebelum ni every 2jam, kurangkan jadi 2x je siang. Do it naturally.  
  
 2️⃣ Jangan offer, tapi jangan tak bagi bila dia nak. Dulu I selalu offer Maysaa. Pastu dah masuk 2y, I kurangkan sikit.  
  
 3️⃣ Kalau siang, distract dia bila dia minta. “Tak payah la susu, kita main toys ok? Kita minum susu princess ok?” (Susu Dutch Lady yang ada Elsa Anna tu)  
  

[![](https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-kjPZFkPLffA/Xa7ZACB8tdI/AAAAAAAAI2o/7JLFrEREFMs7hW7pgNtQIMQRGbllP2YmACLcBGAsYHQ/s320/ShotType1_540x540.jpg)

](https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-kjPZFkPLffA/Xa7ZACB8tdI/AAAAAAAAI2o/7JLFrEREFMs7hW7pgNtQIMQRGbllP2YmACLcBGAsYHQ/s1600/ShotType1_540x540.jpg)

  
  
 4️⃣ Untuk malam pulak, cakap nicely. “Maysaa, no no susu okay? Susu baby punya. Maysaa dah big girl”. But sometimes dia mintak jugak nak pinjam. Bagitahu boleh kejap je, then lepaskan.  
  
5️⃣ Biasanya dia susu sampai lelap. Tapi bagi susu kejap je and pandai pandai lelap sendiri without susu. Taknak bagi mengempeng dah.  
  
6️⃣ Always pesan bila dia mintak. “Bukan susu Maysaa kan?” Tapi kalau mengamuk and mintak, “Please Mummy, Assa nak pinjam je susu....” I bagi je. Kesian sampai mintak nak pinjam sebab tahu bukan dia punya :( #MudahTersentuh  
  
7️⃣ Makesure kenyang, minum susu, snack, minum air kosong. Mmmm tengok la sekarang Maysaa makin berisi lepas tak bf....  
  
8️⃣ Massage! A few times Maysaa tertidur lepas urut or lali and terus lelap.Settle satu malam tanpa drama.  
  
9️⃣ Have sleepovers. Kena ada support from family. Bila dia sleepover, dia tidur without susu. This one memang a big thankyou to my family 💕  
  
🔟 Have a bedtime routine. Salin comfy nappy, brush your teeth, baca doa, peluk and kiss. Make sure bilik gelap and sejuk. Pastu dia lelap sendiri.  
  
1️⃣1️⃣ Support from husband. Daddy pun mainkan peranan tidurkan Maysaa supaya Maysaa lupakan susu.
