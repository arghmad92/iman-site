I saw these masks at Watsons and I rasa cute, so I beli je lah haha. So this is my thoughts on them.

  

![](https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-PneGEmJyzxw/WxzpiOXw4xI/AAAAAAAAGnY/62wCiq2trO4UsO7bub8C4qPP-cY2hy7RgCLcBGAs/s640/Photo%2B22-05-2018%252C%2B8%2B10%2B31%2BPM.jpg)

  

  
  

 I bought three types,  
1) Pomegranate Repairing & Hydrating Facial Mask  
2) Sakura Hydrating & Refreshing Facial Mask  
3) Vitamin C Whitening & Moisturizing  
  
  
  
  
**Name:**   
Pomegranate Repairing & Hydrating Facial Mask  
  
**Star ingredients:**   
\- Niacinamide  
\- Punica Gratum Fruit Extract (Pomegranate) which is one of the best anti-oxidant properties and can reduce the appearance of wrinkles by soothing the skin  
  

![](https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-7XxUXgGiSgc/WxzpjHx8qpI/AAAAAAAAGnc/35YgTdlCUHcLBCm1MQNjmBaQUpVB14KbgCLcBGAs/s640/Photo%2B22-05-2018%252C%2B8%2B10%2B35%2BPM.jpg)

  

![](https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-uwIZtqZqk_E/WxzpjX91eqI/AAAAAAAAGng/bswMzdRQ81AR1GU4mNnirZ411q1A-isygCLcBGAs/s640/Photo%2B22-05-2018%252C%2B8%2B10%2B40%2BPM.jpg)

  
  

  

  

Material: Normal transparent sheet mask

Essence: 26ml clear thick essence

Scent: Slightly scented

Fit: Fit nicely for my face, even the eyes and mouth area.

Adherence: Quite good

  

**My thoughts:**

\- Hydrating

\- Quite sticky. Took around 20 mins for the essence to fully absorb into my skin

\- Not irritating although contains fragrance

\- No alcohol

\- Comfortable

\- Minimal brightening and soothing effect

**My thoughts:**

\- Uses Magnesium Ascorbyl Phosphate and Ethyl Ascorbic Acid for Vitamin C

\- Minimal brightening effect

\- Hydrating

\- Not so sticky as the Pomegranate and Sakura

\- Not irritating although contains fragrance

\- No alcohol

**Would I repurchase?** 

Maybe not since there's a lot of other sheet mask options. But I'll buy if there's an emergency haha. Worth to try.
