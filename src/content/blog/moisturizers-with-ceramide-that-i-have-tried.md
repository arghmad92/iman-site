Moisturizers with ceramide that I have tried

  

  
  

**What is ceramide?**

One of the best ingredient to repair your skin barrier.

  

Bayangkan ceramide tu simen and kulit kita batu bata. Simen tu akan membantu pegang batu bata tu supaya kukuh, and ada lapisan perlindungan (skin barrier) daripada hilang moisture (kelembapan), kotoran and environment luaran. Kulit kita memang dah ada natural ceramide tapi bila kita makin berusia, tak pakai sunscreen and kena cahaya matahari, kita punya skin barrier boleh jadi makin lemah. 

  

Also read: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/08/skincare-101-skin-barrier.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/08/skincare-101-skin-barrier.html)

  

Bila kita product yang ada ceramide, lagi lagi yang well formulated, lapisan kulit kita boleh terjaga, baiki our skin barrier and increase hydration. 

  

Baca kat sini kenapa hydration penting: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/08/why-hydration-is-important-and-how-to.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/08/why-hydration-is-important-and-how-to.html)

  

That is why I love moisturizer that contains ceramides and other antioxidants, and paling penting, dalam bentuk tube. Taknak dalam jar sebab anti-oxidant yang tak stable, bila terdedah dengan udara and cahaya, boleh buat dia hilang effectiveness. So I prefer my moisturizer dalam bentuk tube.
