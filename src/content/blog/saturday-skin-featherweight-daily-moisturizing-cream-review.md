My review on SATURDAY SKIN Featherweight Daily Moisturizing Cream  
  
  
  
  
I bought this moisturizer during Sephora sale 20% sebab kalau normal price, it's quite expensive. Tapi sebab nampak best and warna pink, I try je lah. The current price dekat Sephora Malaysia sekarang is RM190 for 50ml.  
  
  
**Ingredients**

Water, Butylene Glycol, Ethylhexyl Palmitate, Glycerin, Cyclopentasiloxane, Dipropylene Glyco,l Octyldodecyl Myristate, Silica, Cetearyl Alcohol, Cyclohexasiloxane, Polysorbate 60, 1,2-Hexanediol, Citrus Limon (Lemon) Peel Oil, Juniperus Mexicana Oil, Hydrolyzed Avocado Protein, Citrus Aurantium Dulcis (Orange) Peel Oil, Citrus Grandis (Grapefruit) Peel Oil, Eucalyptus Globulus Leaf Oil, Lavandula Angustifolia (Lavender) Oil, Rosmarinus Officinalis (Rosemary) Leaf Oil, Ananas Sativus (Pineapple) Fruit Extract, Pyrus Malus (Apple) Fruit Extract, Actinidia Chinensis (Kiwi) Fruit Extract, Fragaria Vesca (Strawberry) Fruit Extract, Prunus Persica (Peach) Fruit Extract, Vitis Vinifera (Grape) Fruit Extract, Ficus Carica (Fig) Fruit Extract, Ginkgo Biloba Nut Extract, Morus Alba Fruit Extract, Punica Granatum Fruit Extract, Phoenix Dactylifera (Date) Seed, Glyceryl Stearate, Ammonium Acryloyldimethyltaurate/VP Copolymer, Sorbitan Sesquioleate, Tromethamine, PEG-100 Stearate, Caprylyl Glycol, Cetearyl Olivate, Sorbitan Olivate, Stearic Acid, Dimethicone Crosspolymer, Dimethiconol, Hydrogenated Lecithin, Acrylates/C10-30 Alkyl Acrylate Crosspolymer, Ceteareth-20, Maltodextrin, Saccharide Hydrolysate, Cholesterol, PEG-800, Ascorbic Acid, Polysorbate 20, sh-Oligopeptide-1, sh-Polypeptide-1, sh-Oligopeptide-2, sh-Polypeptide-22, sh-Polypeptide-45, sh-Polypeptide-8, sh-Polypeptide-9, Carbomer, Xanthan Gum, Disodium EDTA, Phenoxyethanol

  

**Suggested Usage**  
Pump twice to release cream and use fingers to tap cream into your skin in an outward motion.  
  
**Precautions**  
The jar is an airless container should not be twisted open. If the body of the jar is opened, the airless pump mechanics will no longer function.

To be honest, I jakun sikit sebab first time jumpa product yang nampak macam jar packaging tapi tak payah celup. Jump tekan je pump dia. I tak suka moisturizer yang dalam jar. At first, I tak rasa nak beli sebab dia dalam jar tapi bila tahu dia sebenarnya pump, terus rasa nak beli. I love the pump and supaya produk terjaga. It's super cute. However, it is only 50ml. Sikit sangat sebenarnya. Sebulan je boleh habis. 

  
**Scent**  
Very refreshing citrus scent.  
  
**Texture**

Although dia kata featherweight, it't not. It's like a thick gel texture but not greasy and tacky. Just enough for my dry skin.  
  

  

  
**My thoughts**  
  
I like this moisturizer since it's texture is just right for my dry skin. Quite thick but not too greasy, absorbs beautifully and buat kulit rasa lembut. Pakai sunscreen on top pun rasa okay, tak terlalu thick.

  

It contains essential oils, so kalau kulit jenis sensitive, this product maybe tak sesuai. My skin okay je with essential oils, tak buat rasa pedih or irritated. Overall, I like this.

  
  
**Where to get?**  
Sephora Malaysia

  

**Recommended for?**

If you have dry skin and okay with essential oils, you might like this. 

  

**Repurchase?** 

I don't think I'll repurchase this since the price is quite high and there's a lot of other good affordable moisturizer out there. Tapi kalau setakat nak try, okay je.
