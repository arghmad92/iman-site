### Micellar Water tu apa?

  

Basically, micellar cleansing water ni cara nak cepat untuk remove light makeups & kotoran dekat muka. Just tuang je micellar water atas cotton pad and swipe dekat muka sampai kotoran hilang. Senang. Lepastu tak perlu bilas.

  

**About the product:**

Product Name: SIMPLE Micellar Cleansing Water 

Product Brand: Simple Skincare

Product Type: Micellar Water

Size: 200 mL 

Price: RM 22.90  
Availability: Guardian, Watsons, Caring Pharmacy  
  
**Ingredients**  
Aqua, Hexylene Glycol, Glycerin, Niacinamide, Panthenol, Chamomilla Recutita Flower Extract, PEG-6 Capryllc/Capric Glycerides, Butylene Glycol, Pantolactone, Cetrimonium Chloride, Tetrasodium EDTA, Citric Acid, Potassium Chloride, Sodium Ascorbyl Phosphate, Sodium Chloride, DMDM Hydantoin, Iodopropynyl Butylcarbamate  
  

**Ingredients analysis**

## SIMPLE Micellar Cleansing Water Review

  

**The packaging:**

The product comes in a 200ml transparent plastic bottle.  
  

**The colour/consistency/texture:**

Just like a normal micellar water. Colourless and liquid-like texture.

  

**The scent:**

There is no distinct scent for this product. Biasa biasa je, tak ada bau.  
  

  

**How to use?**

Tuang atas cotton pad lepastu swipe dekat muka lembut lembut, jangan kasar kasar sangat. Jangan tonyoh!

  

So I tried to remove makeups. Waterproof lipsticks etc.

It can only remove light makeups je tau. Heavy and waterproof makeups, susah. Kena tonyoh. Kesian kulit. No no.

  

  
**Pros**  
\- Affordable and senang nak beli  
\- Really works to remove dirts and light makeups  
\- No irritation, suitable for sensitive skin  
\- No weird pahit feeling bila remove lipsticks  
\- No need to rinse afterwards  
\- Hydrating and no tight feeling, feels refreshing  
  
  
**Cons**  
None, actually.  
  
  

**Recommended?**

YES.
