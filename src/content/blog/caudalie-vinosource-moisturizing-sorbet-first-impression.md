My first impression on CAUDALIE Vinosource Moisturizing Sorbet

  

  
  

I remember when I really wanted to buy this moisturizer from Sephora, because it's pink. I just can't help it, when the packaging is pink. RASA MACAM KENA BELI.

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEj20NeeKXX4VDrG8XboKny0d7wxsxYwxsajRSROD_JnItKNoVS510JN1-Wi5JkPp4qKl7mdAVPLYTMuA-DopUK0sg7TQvm7oIb2gBvzwa9uWy33b-kFPsqj0QUkdA7rWj-2z1UY2eC9caOv/s1600/IMG_0514.jpg)

  

Tapi sabar dulu. Harga pun RM135 for only 40ml. That's quite expensive for my budget (padahal beli  Saturday Skin 50ml RM190 okay pulak tanpa berfikir panjang?! Sebab pink jugak! Review here: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/11/saturday-skin-featherweight-daily.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/11/saturday-skin-featherweight-daily.html) )

  

So anyways, when I received these samples (tak ingat pula beli apa ) tapi happy sebab dah lama nak try.

  

**What it is** (Based on Sephora's website):

A cream that intensely moisturizes dehydrated and sensitive skin. 

  

 **What it does**:

 This natural formula deeply hydrates all skin types, even the hypersensitive ones. It instantly moisturizes, softens and soothes.

CAUDALIE Vinosource Moisturizing Sorbet Ingredients

  

 It has a buttery-oily scent. Macam bau minyak pun ada but it's not so strong and okay je for me. Although it contains fragrance, but there is no weird scent, sometimes ada skincare yang bau floral kuat sangat which I don't really like.

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhjcTTJdAb7YKwvYN5v2Kx_n5MuLFio0cDyB-2InWbnpqEYy6BDK_dBvGVnE66MO73oN2gDYsF80IPobx7zf9AAkFW1bwJWu2PRLInZRY2c8kFlXICEVi1F-aLCs-DOhyphenhyphen2ab2iJEJlZzglK/s1600/7E2DD19E-69F8-4C0F-91A2-6B572B99ACC6.jpg)

  

The texture is lightweight cream, which absorbs nicely into skin. At first I pakai semua 2ml and cause pilling (bila gosok lepas pakai, dia keluar daki daki gitu, macam habuk pemadam). So it's better to use sikit je, dah cukup. It leaves a smooth feeling, hydrating but not moisturizing enough for my dry skin. I prefer a heavier feeling yang buat kulit rasa pakai moisturizer tu especially because I have dry skin.

  

Overall, I think this is a good moisturizer for combination or oily skin. As for me, I will not buy the full size because I prefer a thicker moisturizer for my skin. If the ingredients sesuai dengan kulit, it should be good for you :)
