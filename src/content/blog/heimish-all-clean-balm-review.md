**How to use Heimish All Clean Balm**

Just like the normal oil cleanser. 

1\. You scoop it out

2\. Letak dekat muka, gosok slow slow atas muka yang kering. KERING ok. Biar makeup semua cair.

3\. Ambil air sikit, gosok gosok in circular motion bagi dia jadi macam milky gitu.

4\. Continue bilas sampai semua hilang.

Done.

**How I feel:**

I started to use this cleanser bila I tertinggal my Hada Labo Oil Cleanser somewhere.  
  
Also read: [HADA LABO CLEANSING OIL REVIEW](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/07/hada-labo-cleansing-oil.html)  
  
This is the dupe / almost similar to the Banila Co Clean It Zero. Yang tu memang the most famous product ever, it's like a cult. I wanted to buy that one, tapi mahal nya laaaaaa. So, Heimish All Clean Balm ni macam a cheaper version of that. Yang tu harga around RM70-80, yang ni harga RM59. Okay lah.  
  
This product really works if you want to remove heavy makeups, waterproof lipsticks and mascara. Yes! Senang gila nak remove waterproof mascara, just pakai this balm, gosok slow slow, tak perlu pun tonyoh beriya, and semua hilang. Amazing!  
  
Paling penting, it doesn't irritates my skin, smells nice, not drying, at really works! And it is suitable for all skin type. I feel like I love this cleansing balm more than my oil cleanser.... Sebab tak messy. I suka benda yang tak leceh and senang. Oil cleanser, kena picit atas tangan, pastu minyak dia meleleh leleh. Well, it's not that bad sebenarnya. Tapi kalau ada product yang lagi senang and berkesan, why not?  
  
**Pros**  
\- Really works in removing waterproof makeups. Yes.  
\- Smells really nice  
\- Doesnt irritates my skin  
\- Easy to use  
\- Packaging cantik  
\- Not drying  
  
**Cons**  
\- Price is quite expensive (apa apa yang atas RM50, I selalu rasa mahal)  
\- Susah nak beli. Yang I jumpa, dekat Hermo je  
  
  

**Recommended?**

YES!
