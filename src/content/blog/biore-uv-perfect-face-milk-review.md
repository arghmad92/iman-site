## Biore UV Perfect Face Milk Review

  
Product Name: Biore UV Biore UV Perfect Face Milk

Product Brand: Biore

Product Type: Sunscreen

Size: 30 mL 

Price: RM 27.80

Availability: Guardian, Watson, SASA, Tesco, AEON Wellness

  

  

  

**What it claims?**

Biore UV Perfect Face Milk SPF 50+ PA+++ Superior UV block with long-lasting protection against harmful UVA & UVB sunrays. Formulated with sebum absorbing powder, prevents skin from becoming sticky and oily and is effective as a make-up base. Does not clog pores, and its light texture spreads smoothly on the face.

  

**Ingredients**

Cyclopentasiloxane, Zinc Oxide, Alcohol, Water (Aqua), Dimethicone, Lauryl Methacrylate/Sodium Methacrylate Crosspolymer, Ethylhexyl Methoxycinnamate, Talc, Styrene/Stearyl Methacrylate Crosspolymer, PEG-32, Glycerin, Methicone, Polysilicone-9, PEG-12 Dimethicone, PEG-3 Dimethicone, Titanium Dioxide, Dimethicone/Vinyl Dimethicone Crosspolymer, Aluminum Hydroxide, Silica, Phenoxyethanol

  

**How to apply?**

The last step for skincare. Maksudnya lepas cleanser, toner, moisturizer, baru lah apply sunscreen ni. Lepastu continue makeup pulak (primer, foundation etc)

**The packaging:**

The product comes in a 30 ml white plastic squeezable tube with a white twisted cap. Leceh sebab dia tak attached. Senang nak picit sebab kecik je muncung dia tu tapi sebab dia tak transparent, so tak nampak dia banyak mana yang tinggal.

**The colour/consistency/texture:**

White milky texture.

  

**The scent:**

Normal sunscreen smell. Macam chemical gitu tapi tak kuat sangat pun.  
  

**How I feel:**

Although this product contains alcohol and the alcohol is the third ingredient, which means the alcohol percentage is quite high. Tapi, I don't have any problem with this.   
  
So the first time I applied, it's like a milky texture, tak thick macam lotion, more like essence and it's white. Absorbs quickly and leave my skin feels smooth and matte. However, I don't fancy any matte feeling products, since I have dry skin.

**Overall**  
It's good, very affordable, no weird smell, not greasy, dries matte.  
  
**Who will love this?**  
People with oily, combination and normal skin yang nak kulit nampak matte and tak melekit.  
  

**Recommended?**

It depends on your skin!
