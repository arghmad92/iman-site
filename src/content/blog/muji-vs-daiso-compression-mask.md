**Overall experience with Daiso Compression Mask**

It's quite thick so macam membazir jugak la essence kalau nak bagi dia absorb and dia kering pun cepat around 20 mins macam tu dah kering dah. 

  

**Overall experience with MUJI Compression Mask**

The sheet macam a bit irritating? Gatal gatal sikit. I think it's because of the material of the sheet mask. And dia nipis, senang sangat nak terkoyak. Tapi best sebab rasa product soaked in kedalam kulit.

  

**Mana yang lagi best?**

It depends on you. Kalau nak murah, Daiso. Kalau kulit tak sensitive and nak yang nipis, MUJI.

  

  

  

Bottom line:

The main reason I bought this kind of mask is because I have a few toners that I don't really use so daripada membazir, why not I use it as a sheet mask :)

  

But if you don't have any extra toner, jangan lah guna essence/toner/serum biasa tu sebab membazir. Normally pakai 3-4 drops, nanti kalau nak rendam this mask, nak pakai berapa banyak pulak kan? Sayang je. So membazir. 

  

Unless, nak guna this mask untuk buat DIY mask, then I think it's okay. DIY tu contohnya rendam dengan cucumber ke, tea ke, aloevera ke, honey ke.
