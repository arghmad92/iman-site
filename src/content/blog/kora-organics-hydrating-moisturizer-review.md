**Packaging**

It comes in a white opaque tube packaging with a flip top lid. This is the best packaging for a moisturizer since the anti-oxidant is protected and keeping them stable. 

  

**Texture**

The texture is quite thick and quite hard to absorbs. Dia tak sinks into the skin nicely. And kena pakai sikit sikit, layer by layer. I don't really like the texture. However bila dah absorb, dia tak greasy sangat.

  

[![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhKfciWTpXoJVV_MsMHryol_NMvpmUJzTBxc-YLnFwQbnR6gsJE1GMy3SyhFwt2sIWY3ocpc8aEqxOn7PNLpKQLJHnCvkYnguPgDn0aoA514eIoAdmy9u65VjPOLmo_jLIcK96AkO6TW2Lq/s640/Photo+01-07-2018%252C+3+14+42+PM.jpg)

 It's a thick pale yellow cream.

  

[![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhse8zbHMgXpf2PQXxV7wPsfZ_XqNSsR5JLRQ82YE14ZoPwerXGSilyY6l-uJ6pcRggnuRLSNRNmvcJRBPQZWjg-R0A8axhyVRNodH-PMV-wcv4N9fxPcYrcUk0sIHvRZEpV6n8pzH2IBTu/s640/Photo+01-07-2018%252C+3+15+00+PM.jpg)

 Nampak tak bila sapu, dia susah nak absorb gitu. 

  

  

**Scent**

Since it contains essential oils, it smells like citrus-ish. Like in a spa gitu. I like it. But some people with sensitive skin might not be suitable.

  

**My thoughts:**

The ingredients are okay, especially kalau orang yang suka "organic", or "natural" skincare kononnya, this might be for you. However, it contains essential oil that might irritate sensitive skin. For me, I okay je and I like the scent.

  

The only thing I don't really like is the texture. Too thick for me. But it does makes my skin moisturize and hydrated. And tak breaks me out so okay lah.

  

This moisturizer is quite expensive for it's full size. RM181 for 50ml is expensive for me. Yang trial ni 25ml which is half the full size.

  

**Repurchase?** 

Maybe not. But I enjoyed using it :)
