**My experience**

I bought this mask? Scrub? A long long long time ago. I've been using it on and off. Memang ada dalam toilet tu tapi tak guna sangat. I only use this as a scrub, sebab malas nak pakai jadi mask. Padahal boleh je.

  

It has a micro beads, so memang best untuk scrub muka gently, bau pun wangi. It does the job, to exfoliate my face and I feel fresh after using this. Tak rasa drying ke apa. Best!

  

Radiant skin? I tak nampak sangat differences dia but I think kalau pakai as a clay mask, okay kot. I have dry skin so I don't really use clay mask. But I do feel my skin is softer after using this. Good product.

  

I think the packaging pun dah baru, this is the old packaging ha nampak tak, dah lama dah beli benda ni haha

  

**Who will love this?**

All skin types! Kalau oily/combination skin, can use this as a clay mask.
