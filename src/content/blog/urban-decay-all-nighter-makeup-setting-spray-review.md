Product Name: Urban Decay All Nighter Makeup Setting Spray

Product Brand: Urban Decay

Product Type: Makeup Setting Spray

Size: 30 mL (Travel size)

Price: RM 50.00

**Apa function setting spray?**

Setting spray untuk pakai lepas makeup, supaya makeup tak bercapuk and berdebu. Kiranya spray je setting spray ni dekat muka untuk bagi makeup nampak lagi flawless and tahan lama. Simpah air pun no problem. Makeup still intact.

  

**What is claims?**

\- Mists on weightlessly to keep your makeup looking new for up to 16 hours, without melting, cracking, fading or settling into fine lines.

\- Smoother-looking skin and vibrant makeup that lasts all day 

\-For all skin types 

\-Formulated without parabens, sulfates and phthalates

**The packaging:**

The product comes in a 30 ml white and purple plastic bottle with a spray. Dia ada dua cap, satu transparent, satu lagi purple cap luar punya. Botol dia senang kotor. I don't like it.

**How I feel:**

I'm not so sure about how it last for 16 hours tapi setakat yang pakai, yes, makeup macam tak cakey and bercapuk sangat. Still looks fresh even lepas balik rumah. Makeup jadi flawless sikit. It makes my makeup a bit dewy. Personally, I think it's good for dry skin sebab tak rasa matte sangat pun bila pakai, rasa more to dewy and tak irritates my skin. 

  

I bought the travel size punya je which is around RM50, quite worth it. Full size is around RM100. This setting spray lasts for almost a year, sebab I jarang makeup sangat. Kalau selalu makeup, maybe la cepat habis.

  

Overall, I love this product. Really good for my dry and senstive skin. And Urban Decay already launched the new packaging so this packaging dah tak ada dah.
