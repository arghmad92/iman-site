**How to use?**

I use this to set my makeup, after foundation & concealer. Just use a brush and sweep it on my face.

  

**How I feel:**

The first time I used this Althea Petal Velvet Powder, I use to set my under eyes concealer because I'm not wearing any foundation on that day. It has a really fine texture and sets my concealer beautifully.  
  
I have dry skin, and it didn't dry out my skin. I think this is great for combination-oily skin so the makeup won't melt.  
  

**Recommended?**

YES. If you need a new setting powder with an affordable price to try!

  

Thanks Althea for sending me this Althea Petal Velvet Powder! I really love it. You can purchase them from Althea's website :)
