First of all, **The Ordinary products!** I love The Ordinary sebab ingredients semua straight forward and of course, harga affordable. Like the Niacinamide serum, around RM49 rasanya I beli. Kalau beli sendiri, lagi murah around RM30 je tapi I order je, senang. Just search dekat ig hashtag #theordinarymalaysia pastu pilih je seller yang korang nak. 

  

**Azelaic Acid Suspension 10%** - brightens the skin tone, improve skin texture, reduce blemishes. I use this for my husband, tapi dia a bit drying. Rasa bercapuk capuk so kena pakai sikit sikit je.

  

**Natural Moisturizing Factor + HA** - Hydration. Tak buka lagi. Simpan dulu buat stock haha.

  

**Niacinamide + Zinc** \- Reduce blemishes, balance sebum activity and for glowinggg. Baru pakai for two weeks. I love how smooth my skin feels like. Nanti dah pakai 6 weeks ++ baru I buat review sebab untuk serum, lepas 6 minggu baru boleh nampak perubahan dia.
