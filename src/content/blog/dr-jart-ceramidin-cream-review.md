The texture is quite thick. A little goes along the way. Pakai sikit pun dah cukup. Kalau pakai banyak sangat, it's a bit heavy and thick, macam berpeluh sikit. 

  

  

  

  

**My thoughts**

Macam biasa, I bought this moisturizer sebab ada banyak good reviews and it contains ceramide. Nama pun Ceramidin Cream. It contains glycerin, ceramide and cholestrol which is good for your skin barrier.

  

Read about skin barrier: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/08/skincare-101-skin-barrier.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/08/skincare-101-skin-barrier.html)

  

Ceramide is good untuk retain and replenish our skin. Bila skin barrier kita terjaga, kulit nampak lagi lembut, gebu, less wrinkles, kurang sensitive and kurang breakout. Dalam Dr. Jart+ Ceramidin Cream ada Ceramide NP which is atas atas jugak dalam list, Ceramide AP, Ceramide AS, Ceramide NS and Ceramide EOP.

  

It also contains essential oils which may irritate your skin kalau tak sesuai. As for me, I okay je. Tapi I lagi prefer skincare yang tak ada essential oils lah.

  

This moisturizer is quite thick, so if you have oily skin, you might not like it sebab rasa melekit. If you have dry skin or banyak duduk dalam aircond, this might be okay for you. I suka this moisturizer sebab since dia a bit thick, I pakai sikit pun dah cukup. Tapi selalu reapply juga. Kalau ada dry patches, cepat je hilang. Suka!

  

However, the price is a bit expensive, around Rm195 kalau dekat Sephora. Kalau tempat lain maybe murah sikit kot. Might not repurchase unless they improve the formula.
