**MY THOUGHTS ON PURITO COMFY WATER SUN BLOCK**

When it comes to sunscreen, here are some criteria that I look in a sunscreen.

  

**1\. The filters**. I honestly prefer a chemical or hybrid sunscreen since the I don't like physical sunscreen due to it's white cast and the texture is a bit drying for me. However, although this is a physical sunscreen, it has no white cast AT ALL. If your skin is sensitive with chemical UV filters and you hate whitecast, this product is a must try for you.

  

**2\. The SPF and PA rating**. In Malaysia, SPF30-50 is enough. This product is SPF50 and PA++++ which gives s very good protection against UVA and UVB.

  

**3\. No irritating ingredients.** I would personally avoid alcohol since it makes my skin drier and product that are highly fragrance. This product does not contains both. However, it contains essential oils, tea tree oil which is good to fight acne and lavender oil. It might be a bit drying, depends on how your skin react with it, but it is completely okay for me. This product is not fungal acne safe.

  

**4\. Lightweight, nice texture and smells nice**. I love the texture and how it absorbs into my skin nicely, leaving a satin finish. 

  

  

  

  

  

Bottom line, this is a good physical sunscreen with no white cast, doesn't makes my skin feels dry, moisturizing, good under makeup and suitable to use with Malaysia's climate. I approve!
