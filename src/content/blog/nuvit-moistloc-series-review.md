Nuvit Moistloc Series Review  
  
  

  
  
  
  
Hi! Have you heard about Nuvit before? This is actually my first time trying this brand. So here is my thoughts on them. Is it good? Do I like them?  
  
There are four products that I will be featuring in this post which are the cleansing milk, hydrating toner, serum and gel.  
  

#### NUViT MoistLoc Hydrating Double Action Cleansing Milk

(100ml, RM27.90)

  
![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgBrhHkqib1fkGfwlq3Gc2_tIgJ6Qg_FpSEJNnxkvDbcI0DPSDd5ae5U0puIiU_9Euuz9VJcyASv4CqkS9bYJpsnFNus7DNuALeVXGholFpg8taojbMkYHgpfPM6uIN19A87ZssMvsWAtau/s640/3B8CC64F-1971-4561-B6E3-81366BFA5BE0.jpg)  
  

**How to use?**

_Apply onto wet skin and gently massage skin. Cleanse morning and/or evening. Rinse thoroughly and pat dry without rubbing. Use as a daily face wash for normal to dry skin to remove makeup, dirt, impurities, and hydrate skin._

  

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEi0sjCUXFW7U0NubGeHwy4IULGmLeWX7aVatRsGPDftvAC7hxFbz_0rrdX9GbUzlT9PokPRQauFQVBU2SF_TDU-SqmYkoXSyr432UeMuevnnOwqzwigzXqTpCIwsv469gZ5pjYX8i2DfRyK/s640/FF246036-1C53-408E-988A-1E0B3079096F.jpg)

  

  

It is free from fragrance but it contains essential oils (Orange, Lavender) in case if your skin is sensitive with essential oils. For me, I don't really mind unless it's peppermint/ bergamot because I don't like the _minty_ feeling. It is free from alcohol and paraben. It uses gentle surfactant with no SLS. However, if you have fungal acne, you might want to avoid this since it contains Polysorbate 60 and PEG-7 Glyceryl Cocoate.

  

This product claims to have _a perfect solution for skin-moisturizing system. It helps to activate skin hydrating system and restore long-lasting hydration to strengthen skin's barrier and prevent it's dehydration, protecting skin from dryness and external irritation, resulting in impeding water loss, immediately making skin tender and plump, revealing a more radiant skin tone._

  

I'm all about maintaining the skin barrier, you can read more about skin barrier here: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/08/skincare-101-skin-barrier.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/08/skincare-101-skin-barrier.html)  
  

For me, I only use this as my second cleanser during PM routine because I prefer doing double cleansing method. My first cleanser is any oil based cleanser to remove all the makeup and sunscreen. Then, I will continue using this cleanser.  
  
![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEijN8ZU8NrNHu2eoyHo89lojBjA29PhN9o7mW04Vu2NtUNx3QR_iyD4djz1PnoZsGfFxEvzE3Nu3rtpF93_2htPJYWywx_hCaD4jRKIFuWNAH8zlr0plJHUXMMXyz8eZx5fyensuzF94EE7/s640/599350E6-2BC8-4069-8362-487B6C663977.jpg)  
  

I tested the pH and it is around pH4-5.  
  

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEif1dWT0fd63Qn19uccq0zF4wLGA9cHGE4HETji4Mrf3Bbn2DRVvSmachzvGNMyBx3Kr852jxRBY4JaIgixTI19zb087FY2SVwgyb0o62rtQBtsUiaCuDAMzfP3mNHmtlSiq9mESY4yMxQA/s640/14C85967-702D-45E9-9858-6A8EC6956E6A.jpg)  

  
I personally think this is a good cleanser that is comfortable to use, no tight feeling after using this product, a low pH cleanser, no irritating ingredients, no harsh surfactants and contains moisturizing ingredients. I like this cleanser!

  

You can read more about how to choose a cleanser, here: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/07/choose-cleanser.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/07/choose-cleanser.html)

This toner is really light, almost like water and absorbs nicely into the skin. There is no distinct scene that I noticed and I love that it does not contains fragrance. I like using my toner using my palm and apply it 3-4 layers for extra soothing and hydrating effect.

(30ml, RM60.90)

I love the ingredient list! It contains a few soothing ingredients, such as panthenol, centella asiatica extract and chamomile extract. It also contains brightening ingredients such as niacinamide and licorice root extract. This is by far my fav product in this range! It's paraben free, silicone free, alcohol free and fragrance free. However, it is not fungal acne safe since it contains PEG-60 Hydrogenated Castor Oil and others.

  

I love the watery texture, a thicker version than the toner but it is not sticky. It absorbs nicely into the skin with a calming a soothing feeling. It has no noticeable scent
