Product Name: Dr. Morita Hyaluronic Acid Moisture Essence Facial Mask  
Produck Brand: Dr. Morita

Product Type: Sheet Mask

Price: RM5.90 per piece (25g)  
Availability: All nationwide Guardian stores  
Made in: Taiwan  
  
  

**How to use?**

Use after cleanser. (And after toner, essence, serum if you have)

1\. Unfold mask and remove white backing

2\. Align mask from top to bottom

2\. Leave on about 20-30 minutes and remove it -- Do not use it overnight tau

3\. Massage slightly with fingertips for better absorption of the excess essence on skin.

  
  

No need to rinse. 

  

**What it claims?**

Hyaluronic Acid: Super moisturizing factor, increase skin's firmness and elasticity

Q10 (Ubiquinone): Anti-oxidant, restore's skin vitality, lightens wrinkles and dilutes dull skin

**How I feel about it?**  
This is mask one of the most faved sheet mask? Ramai yang cakap best sebab tu I pun try. The price is quite okay. I bought this at Guardian. Dekat SASA pun ada jugak. Watsons tak ada tau. I put this inside the fridge first, for extra cooling effect.  
  
The fit is good, just nice. No weird scent, the essence is good, not too sticky. Cooling effect sebab letak dalam peti so sejukkk! Very soothing, no irritation, tak rasa gatal ke pedih ke apa, best je. I pakai for 30 mins, and sapu lebih lebih essence tu dekat leher, lengan, siku, lutut, kaki sebab taknak membazir haha.  
  
The essence absorbs after 3-4 minutes, kena tepuk tepuk sikit muka bagi essence dia absorbs well. And then, I apply my moisturizer. Tak melekit sangat. Best.  
  
Moisturizing? Yes. Memang rasa kulit supple and hydrated. Really great for my dry skin. And I think it's good for all skin type sebab dia tak melekit. I love this.  
  

**Recommended?**

Yesss!

  
**Will I repurchase?**

Yesss!
