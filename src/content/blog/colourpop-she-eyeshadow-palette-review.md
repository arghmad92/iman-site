Surprisingly, this is my first eyeshadow palette from Colourpop since I have other eyeshadow palettes so I don't feel like buying other palettes but.... This palette is really attractive and I don't have the kind of warm pink burgundy kind of shades yet, so why not?

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhiHf2LGjxy5DTE-BzNCCzF0tcTkScvkO-X4sDUsZ2B-CetxpzrNdLiTWT_uTzMR_2c0nuO8Bi5Ke-2lpC3SKVxjvSQomOuji0woP14X7YHokANNIPXT3qCotYs_lKLu8urI7Di0DYaoViR/s1600/Photo+22-01-2018%252C+11+03+16+AM.jpg)

  

There are 12 shades in this palette, 4 mattes and 8 shimmering/metallic eyeshadows. It's a rose, burgundy, metallic copper kind of tone which I don't really have yet, so I think the shades are really prettyyyy!

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhBP8yOfNldfdhV15v2TgsynhaoBej2DQAmBp1H8q7AIkFt2FRpQhD5_VJtUhHlQiz6xRIcnS-cZu7nCqz1vviEf0l8s3XvHWFn8RbAi9H0SsRMZjxjZZCY7FjXlf8hyLGqht7IQCypDJ2o/s1600/Photo+22-01-2018%252C+11+03+22+AM.jpg)

  

  

The shades that I used the most are Ladylike, which is a metallic light gold which is perfect as a highlighter too! Really pigmented, smooth and buttery which is easy to apply and blend. Mademoiselle and Empress are both my fav shades! Both are metallic, the texture is really smooth and the colour pay off is so good.

  

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEh5yylHUakvx7ews2DPd8nLB0b1lMFEZCUfvFFm2nPbIGIpp7Cra1Uia_KCqg-DvP0aHZlV1hXzCjOKHBKd5EdZuMkx1UfH0771JbNempbAd1QzX8M-SSqf3hGO7TXYwgBUh4fzw7_pJgQP/s1600/Screen+Shot+2018-01-22+at+11.48.12+AM.png)

  

  

One of the shade that I really like the colour is Gal. I like the shade, a glittery reddish-copper. However, the fall-out is really bad, bila apply dekat eyelid je memang habis berdebu kat muka. Really messy. I memang basahkan my brush supaya less fall-out and managable sikit. I suka warna dia, tapi texture dia alaaahaaaaai.

  

As you can see in the picture above, Mademoiselle and Empress memang cantik gilaaa, really smooth and easy to apply. For Gal, memang messy tapi warna dia cantikk.

  

  
  

  

The shades that I rarely used are Babe (soft shimmering light pink shade which is quite sheer for me), and Chick (I don't know why I rarely use this one but it's also quite sheer for me too)..

  

For the matte shades, Dear is a bit too bright for me. It's a pink-coral kind of shade, I don't really like the colour sebab rasa terang sangat tapi texture dia best. Really smooth and pigmented. Tak payah apply banyak pun, colour dia memang nampak! Missy is actually the main attraction of this palette for me, sebab I dah lama cari this kind of medium-dark red shade, almost like burgundy. Others shades are, okay.

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEh3S7aYQOkZpj0E96bboiw2RUS3HcAf-tPI3wBPnBy5oqUnoVBpNl3Sz5PweHFqW3MH2xkhxhDprXT9Z_6ZxCIu6DNmDvnqkhsCCxetzDUy-dSofjC0OPNXaczuSIYvN_wN111xGYo8PKl7/s1600/Screen+Shot+2018-01-22+at+11.46.20+AM.png)

All the shades in this palette and the shades that I like.  
  
I still need to use other eyeshadow palette for the base and transition colour, but you can still use this palette alone.

  

  

As for the packaging, it's like a cardboard packaging which I don't really like. Kena air confirm kembang. And it is quite small that what I imagined. Tapi I like the pink rose design!

  

  
  

  

**Overall**

For a makeup noob like me, this eyeshadow is not really wearable for daily use (For me lah. I know people yang like this kind of look and would use this everyday). I prefer something yang lagi hambar like brown and earthy tone eyeshadow for daily look. Pakai eyeshadow pun nampak macam tak pakai so whats the point of pakai entah hahaha. However, kalau ada events or weddings ke, it's nice to have this kind of shades. I love it and definitely will use this more, again and again <3

  

**Recommended?**

Of course. Tapi dah tak ada dekat website Colourpop.....
