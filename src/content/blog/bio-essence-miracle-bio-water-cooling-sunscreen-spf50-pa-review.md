**My experience**

I bought this during the Guardian sale sebab dia kata dekat packaging, "Dermatological Tested" (although dermatological tested is meaningless haha) and "For Sensitive Skin". Siap ada 2X cooling and moisturizing effect. Since I have dry skin, um yes let me try this. I did notice alcohol is the third ingredients but since dia kata for sensitive skin, I macam percaya je lah. Normal price is around RM40+ for 40ml. Quite expensive.

  

First impression, I really like the texture. Tak melekit, really lightweight as what it claims. If you have oily skin pun, it will absorb into your skin beautifully. No greasy or melekit feeling at all. The finishing is also nice. Dia macam nampak kulit lagi healthy with no white cast at all. Tak perlu risau kulit jadi kelabu. Apply banyak banyak pun takpe. The scent is quite strong, I don't really like the scent sebab bau floral dia kuat and rasa mengganggu. But that is just my personal preference.

  

  
  

However, this is not for me. Although dia kata for sensitive skin, my skin cannot tahan lah. Pedih GILA and merah GILA. I need to bilas muka, and memang merah and irritated. Maybe because of the alcohol or something else. But memang pedih sangat. Cooling effect dah jadi panas gila haha.

  

So yeah, good sunscreen but not for me.

  

**Pros**: No white cast, nice finish, not drying,not greasy, lightweight, easy to apply

  

**Cons**: Small, high fragrance, not for sensitive skin

  

  

  

**Recommended for?**

You can try this if your skin is okay with alcohol and other ingredients

  

**Who will not love this?**

Sensitive skin

  

**Will I repurchase?**

Nope.
