Hello! Today I am going to share about how I planned for for Japan!

  

  
  

Macam biasa, kalau travel, I suka plan sendiri sebab tak suka ikut timing orang and nak lebih bebas haha. Kalau travel dekat dekat, tak risau sangat. For Japan, my husband kata, suka hati lah nak plan itinerary semua so I planned everything by myself with the help of google lah of course!

  

  

**FLIGHT TICKET TO JAPAN**

I memang dah lama hint hint nak pergi Japan tapi takdelah terfikir dalam masa yang terdekat. Lepastu suddenly Air Asia X buat promo route baru, tiket tak sampai RM500 untuk sorang, return. Murah ok!  Landed dekat Narita Airport. 

  

[!

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjWcq2YmUFOiviwNKs0srFPyJXHBMuEn3Mynwc7M52TyxcYZPIi9gHtd94HRi9MxnJe0-yF-37I0BCaOk2CMptPw8zd8qJpQHXKJgnKxg7yMusZfLcttn6PJcuVtSRfNX5zVfUkOaCAMmja/s1600/EMPF6ebU4AANVGW.jpeg)

  

Benda murah macam ni, we buy first, think later! Sebab tiket murah ni bukan selalu. Kalau tak grab, rugi! So terus beli. (Ok lepastu ada perubahan plan, kitorang pergi Osaka dulu so tiket from KL-Narita tu burn je lah but that's another story)

**POCKET WIFI**

  

[!

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgmOvtZf831sDB72Y06qJ5tjMuM06UrkQswUjz2TFKHWwYL8YYElDtNc2Ynw4bQahKHxdh6W1aOxmJE1xoL2zx0OdIX83eAIHIad6O8RR4Qw5ux_242cy0RoYDy47A8HgNpMQpCmBsPNT4Z/s1600/8C16BBED-B4A4-4B3C-9FD5-C783EA4B0751.JPG)

  

I ambil Roaming Man! Sebab for me, senang. I prefer guna pocket wifi and boleh guna sampai 5 devices. Boleh lah share, connect dekat phone, ipad, laptop semua. Kalau travel dengan family or friends, best lah. Boleh share share je. Pickup and pulangkan dekat KLIA/KLIA2. So easy!

  

  

  

**ITINERARY**

Bab buat itinerary ni lah yang paling leceh tapi best! Mula mula I list dulu tempat mana yang nak pergi. Of course I nak pergi USJ, Disneyland and Disneysea. Takde pilih pilih! I nak pergi semua! Sebab taktahu bila lagi next nak pergi Japan hahaha #tamak

  

[!

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEi3IME_pd2cwAPqzl7xqSxv_PLTAAoccxTgWoheHg96oQ7QBKNjvcFuZ0d4qfBMHJtqnOhPzOjs_tZbN98woHbEhrMt_fSO-AN5NTRIBMYvYaJyORfI1ojohCFbpOQom8QIIMXX0si2jWta/s1600/phonto.PNG)

  

So this is my itinerary for Japan. I taknak ambitious sangat pergi semua tempat sebab nanti penat. Kena google tempat mana nak pergi, area mana, nak pakai pass apa. Kitorang beli satu pass je, ICOCA Card. Boleh pakai dekat Osaka and Tokyo.

  

Main transportation kat sana memang jalan kaki and train sahaja. I dah cakap dekat husband I, kalau penat, nak taxi. Tapi tak naik pun sebab mahal haha jimat duit.

  

Untuk tengok train, I guna apps Google Map sahaja. Tak perlu guna apps lain. Nak jalan jalan pun memang guna Google Map. Make sure kena ada internet connection je.
