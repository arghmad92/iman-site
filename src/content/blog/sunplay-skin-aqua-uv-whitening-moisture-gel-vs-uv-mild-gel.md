For this blogpost, I'll refer Sunplay Skin Aqua UV whitening moisture gel as "**Whitening Moisture Gel (pink)**" and Skin Aqua UV mild gel as "**Mild Gel (yellow)**"

  

### **Similarities**:

I bought both of this since both contain no alcohol and no fragrance. I memang nak cari sunscreen yang tak ada alcohol. You can read about it here: [Alcohol free sunscreen from drugstore](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/05/alcohol-free-sunscreen-drugstore.html). Kenapa nak cari yang tak ada alcohol? Sebab normally, skincare products with high alcohol makes my skin dry. And I have dry skin. Although it also depends on the formulation. Ada skincare yang buat kulit I pedih, ada yang okay. Also read: [Alcohol in skincare](http://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/06/skincare-101-alcohol-in-skincare.html)

  

  

  

### **Claims:**

Both claims to have hydrating ingredients, light, gentle and mild formula. Both also contains no alcohol, fragrance and mineral oil with low irritation.

  

**Whitening Moisture Gel (pink)**: Contains Arbutin to help fight dark spots for visibly fair & radiant skin

 **Mild Gel (yellow):** Protects skin against environmental pollution

  

Kalau nak ada brightening ingredients, boleh try yang Whitening Moisture Gel (pink). Kalau rasa nak pergi area yang banyak asap or jerebu, boleh try yang  Mild Gel (yellow)

  

### **SPF & PA rating**

**Whitening Moisture Gel (pink)**: SPF50 PA++

 **Mild Gel (yellow)**: SPF30 PA+++

  

Which one is better? Takde. Whitening Moisture Gel (pink) tu high SPF protection tapi moderate PA protection. Mild Gel (yellow) tu okay lah SPF30 for daily use and high PA protection. Enough kalau nak guna setiap hari dekat Malaysia kalau tak berjemur teruk sangat.

  

### UV Filters

  

**Whitening Moisture Gel (pink):** 

5 types of UV filters which are

1\. Ethylhexyl Methoxycinnamate (Octinoxate) - may cause allergic reaction to some people (tapi I okay je) 

2\. Homosalate - UVB protection 

3\. Ethylhexyl Triazone 

4\. Bis-ethylhexyloxyphenol methoxyphenyl triazine (Tinosorb S) 

5\. Diethylamino Hydroxybenzoyl Hexyl Benzoate - (Univul A Plus) which is a very good UVA protection

  

 **Mild Gel (yellow):** 

2 types of UV filters which also contains in Whitening Moisture Gel (pink):

1\. Ethylhexyl Methoxycinnamate (Octinoxate) 

2\. Diethylamino Hydroxybenzoyl Hexyl Benzoate (Univul A Plus)

  

Both sunscreen are chemical sunscreen sebab dia tak ada physical filter, either titanium dioxide or zinc oxide.

### **Scent**

**Whitening Moisture Gel (pink)**: Quite strong sunscreen smell

 **Mild Gel (yellow)**: No noticeable scent. Kalau ada pun, very mild

  

### **Price & size**

**Whitening Moisture Gel (pink):** 80g

 **Mild Gel (yellow):** 110g

  

The price is almost the same, around RM30+ during sale. Normal price around RM40+.

  

  

  

### Which one I like the most?

I prefer **Sunplay Skin Aqua UV Mild Gel (yellow)** because the texture isn't drying, I like how my skin feels after applying the sunscreen. It also has no noticeable smell, no whitecast, and size dia lagi besar, lambat nak habis. Although the SPF protection tak tinggi sampai SPF30 tapi ok la for daily use. For your reference, I have dry skin so if you have oily skin, you might not like this hehe
