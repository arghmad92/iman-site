**The Inkey List Vitamin B, C and E Moisturizer Review**

[!

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiHml-_QPVCP6PseKHVCSFA9kpmMpWWEkiUuF_OWDEtb7QeOTgx33aUaNxc95MspbsLXkgmiWvVxidVQacIQpzbuCnARXFfiMzng867YEgD6GljGJJFXajmugZPFlNWdZoMhu_gphl7Jea-/s1125/B715A7D3-4A25-4198-B1FA-77B96F88739A.jpeg)

  

What it claims?

This lightweight daily moisturizer delivers every essential vitamin for healthy-looking skin, including clarifying B3 (Niacinamide) to help balance oil, brightening antioxidant Vitamin C, and soothing Vitamin E. Perfect for normal skin types looking to keep skin healthy.

Ingredient List

[!

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEi7Lce3_lX1et8fw8l-Ylnu0wYWmIBXFF1XObz289R4WqGu2wSttYLS-fEisnGw6B_vaZOyy_Ogy0Qy6_ClLnKqg0LC1IDvwixZh4fxVuoagIBqZ3-DmcATGlm0Wb1UJZ3GJ6ohU-Wf1FgJ/s1125/16656D3A-32A0-4DCE-A667-C90150EDF369.jpeg)

  

My thoughts

I bought this during Sephora 20% sale. The normal price pun quite affordable, RM25 for 50ml. Macam harga drugstore product je kan? The ingredients pun nampak best, ada niacinamide, sodium ascorbyl phosphate (vitamin C derivative) and vitamin E. It comes in a tube with screwable cap. Design semua sama macam The Inkey List products yang lain, black and white. I suka sebab di ada tulis how to use and when to use. The PAO (Period After Opening) is 6 months.

  

It has a gel-cream texture. Upon applying, boleh rasa macam a bit thick but it absorbs nicely into the skin 30s-1min after applying on the skin. Lepas dah absorb, it leaves a smooth and nice feeling dan tak melekit. However, since I have dry skin, this moisturizer rasa macam not enough for my skin. If you have normal / combination skin, atau kulit yang tak kering sangat, you might love the texture! It has no fragrance and no irritating ingredients so if you have sensitive skin, this might be a great choice too.

My thoughts

I started to enjoy using skincare with peptides so of course I wanted to try this Peptide Moisturizer. It comes in  50ml pump bottle with very affordable price too. Dalam RM71 kalau tak silap dekat Sephora. Dia jenis kena tekan and the moisturizer akan keluar, tak perlu celup celup walaupun nampak macam jar.

  

I love the texture, it’s really moisturizing for my dry skin. Kalau pakai malam pun best. And macam biasa, all The Inkey List products tak ada fragrance and rritating ingredients. This one I guna sampai habis and would defo repurchase in the future.

  

**The Inkey list Ceramide Night Treatment Review**

What it claims?

Naturally found in the skin, Ceramide is the glue that binds skin cells together, helping the skin’s barrier feel rehydrated, supple and protected. This 3% blend of Ceramides works overnight at various levels of the skin to target dehydrated skin. This treatment is also formulated with a 2.5% multi-molecular Hyaluronic Acid, giving an all over surge of skin plumping hydration.

[!

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiqevEvAirF0Z1n5iUs_YIp3OYfQgwBIKmDvIyQt4xaTexX3_MP1SPcq3v_zihrC8lNMD6OCuG5LQzJxVRvIaZVEph2xtSEb0EEFwDZGfXV_ltr1j0zW83iQh_wRCoVyDvhSr-8oB0Gsx3W/s1125/DA070665-8C71-4A79-942E-F9CA1A80392D.jpeg)

  

My thoughts

This one kalau tengok dekat website The Inkey List, it is considered as treatment, bukan moisturizer. And to be used 2-3 times per week. Nak skip moisturizer and guna this one as moisturizer pun boleh jugak. I tried using this as my night time moisturizer tapi for my dry skin, rasa not enough. Kalau nak result lagi best, it is recommended to use any hydrating products before using this. I memang apply my hydrating toner tapi still rasa not enough for me. So I guna dia on top of my moisturizer tapi tak guna thick moisturizer. Hydrating toner - light moisturizer - this night treatment, memang best result dia!

  

It comes in a 30ml bottle with a pump. For me personally, 30 ml ni sikit sangat kalau nak guna as moisturizer. But it is worth to try especially if you want to repair your skin barrier or you have sensitive skin.
