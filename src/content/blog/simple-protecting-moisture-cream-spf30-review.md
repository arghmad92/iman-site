**What it claims?** 

Perfect blend of multi-vitamins and skin loving ingredients with SPF protection to give dehydrated and dull skin a boost whilst protecting from UVA/UVB rays. Perfect even for sensitive skin.  
  

**How to use?**

As a day moisturizer. After cleanser & toning.

  

**Packaging**

White plastic bottle with a pump  
  

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgoK_eR_OUVdKJFkFnDAh7ou2PLhPIF4IwMsqW1fx7c-e6U1vBdL5_69ygp6Bjjr1YA78Mu4anVLOMC6DfEtjg0KWrdubSgWv2EFGqEAGBXGLr18vyl_seRM40nWMLH6VcP9WPHG-R3xmQ/s640/Photo+Jun+23%252C+01+11+23.jpg)

  

**Colour/consistency:**

White creamy texture

  

**Scent:**

Slightly floral scent, with the normal sunscreen smell

  

**How I feel:**

What really good about this moisturizer is that it contains SPF. SPF is reaaaaallyyy important ok. Don't ever skip SPF. And SPF30 is just nice for daily use. Macam sekali gus je, sapu moisturizer + sunscreen k tak payah beli separate sunscreen dah. But it does leaves a white cast on the skin  
  
It also has a pump which is very hygienic and travel friendly. Boleh adjust how much nak guna, tak ada lah membazir.

  
Since I have dry skin, I think this cream is not really enough for me because I need a very thick moisturizer, unless I use another moisturizer before applying this cream. I think this works with combination skin. For very oily skin, I'm afraid it might be a bit greasy for you.  
  

**Recommended?**

Not really, but you can try if you're searching for a good moisturizer with SPF, and if you have normal or combination skin.

  

That's all. Thanks for reading!
