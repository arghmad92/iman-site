Macam mana nak hilangkan whitehead dan blackhead?

  

  
  

  

## Whitehead

  

![](https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-4NspJ8PGHTo/XE2ahs_L6YI/AAAAAAAAIGI/sXnDoGIiqK4u15_KeDDEWBp640muGj5tgCLcBGAs/s640/IMG_5575.jpg)

  

#### 1\. Basic skincare routine.

Basic skincare routine ni maksudnya guna cleanser, moisturizer and sunscreen setiap hari, dah pilih produk yang sesuai dengan kulit. Sometimes bila produk yang tak sesuai dengan kulit, boleh menyebabkan whitehead juga.

  

Boleh baca kat sini: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/02/skincare-101-basic-skincare-routine.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/02/skincare-101-basic-skincare-routine.html)

  

#### 2\. Keep your skin hydrated.

  

Kat sini I ada share kenapa hydration penting and macam mana nak make sure kulit sentiasa hydrated:  [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/08/why-hydration-is-important-and-how-to.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/08/why-hydration-is-important-and-how-to.html)

Contoh hydrating toners.
