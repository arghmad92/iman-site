**What it claims?**

Our 100% invisible, 100% game-changing makeup-gripping primer with SPF 40. This award-winning formula is also weightless and scentless, and it has a slightly glowy effect that allows you to create any type of (makeup or no-makeup) look. But that's not all... This is the last step in your morning skincare routine, before makeup or as a face-finisher. Apply a dime sized amount of Unseen Sunscreen after you cleanse and moisturize, and before your makeup if you are planning to wear any. Tip: Unseen serves as a great primer!

**My thoughts on Supergoop Unseen Sunscreen Broad Spectrum SPF40**

I'm glad that I only got the sample size cause this product buat kulit I pedih gilaaaaa. It contains no alcohol, no fragrance, so I think the main culprit is Avobenzone. There are some sunscreens yang ada avobenzone and my skin rasa pedih gila bila pakai, including Bio-essence Cooling Sunscreen (Review here: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/05/bio-essence-miracle-bio-water-cooling.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/05/bio-essence-miracle-bio-water-cooling.html) ), Neutrogena Ultra Sheer Sunscreen and Banana Boat Sport. However, sunscreen like Wardah Sun Care (Review here: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/04/wardah-sun-care-sunscreen-gel-spf30.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/04/wardah-sun-care-sunscreen-gel-spf30.html) ) and Banila Co Hello Sunny Hydrating Sun Essence (Review here: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/12/banila-co-hello-sunny-hydrating-sun.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/12/banila-co-hello-sunny-hydrating-sun.html) ) pun ada jugak but maybe the % is very low so tak buat kulit I pedih sangat or maybe because of the formulation. If your skin can tolerate avobenzone or other ingredients, it should be okay.

  

As for the texture, it leaves a powdery matte finishing which is good if you have combination or oily skin. It doesn't feel greasy or sticky at all! Rasa macam tak pakai sunscreen pun. So yes, it is 100% invisible!  
  
**Pros**: No white cast, very lightweight, not greasy at all, no alcohol, no fragrance, suitable for combination-oily skin
