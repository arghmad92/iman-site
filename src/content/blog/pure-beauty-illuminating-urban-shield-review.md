**My thoughts on Pure Beauty Illuminating Urban Shield**  
This is a chemical sunscreen since it contains only chemical filter. It has SPF50 and PA++++ which is the highest protection level from UVA. I prefer using a SPF30-50 sunscreen to get enough protection.  
  
As for the texture, it has a very heavy texture and hard to absorb. Even though I have dry skin, this sunscreen feels heavy and sticky on my skin. Kalau nak pakai sikit sangat, takut tak cukup protection. Nak pakai secukupnya, melekit pulak. It has a very dewy finishing, looks shiny and glowy. And walaupun dah lama pakai, dia tak absorb sangat dalam kulit. But it has no white cast so plus point kat situ.  
  
I normally use this when I want to go out without applying makeup because I feel like it's not a good makeup base. Kalau rasa nak keluar yang taknak pakai makeup, I'll use this one. It doesn't irritates my skin, tak rasa pedih, tak buat kulit breakout. If you can tahan the scent then okay lah. The scent is quite strong jugak but I think all Pure Beauty products bau macam ni. If you can't use this on your face, boleh guna dekat tangan or area lain.  
  
Overall, did not enjoyed this but still boleh guna sampai habis sebab pakai dekat tangan :P
