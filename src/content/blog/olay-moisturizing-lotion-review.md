My all time favourite moisturizer, Olay Moisturizing Lotion.

  

![](https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-WMMBLbcRxXs/Wbnn2QGf_nI/AAAAAAAAEws/tbp2uHKBiCENa8rIbruwgjTKZdqj6GR2gCLcBGAs/s640/Photo%2B13-09-2017%252C%2B6%2B09%2B58%2BPM.jpg)

  
  
  
  
  
  
I've been using this moisturizer since I was 16 which is 9 years ago. I never tried other moisturizers until this year. Tu pun sebab saja nak try moisturizer lain, sebab rasa boring. Tapi still, this one is the best for me <3  
  

**About the product:**

Product Name: Olay Moisturizing Lotion

Product Brand: Olay

Product Type: Moisturizer

Size: 150 mL 

Price: RM 42.90  
Availability: Guardian, Watsons, Caring Pharmacy  
  
**What is claims?**  
Moisturizes quickly to soothe dryness with lasting hydration. Light, Non-Greasy Formula. • Dermatologically Tested. • Non-Comedogenic (Won't Clog Pores). Moisturizes quickly to soothe dryness with lasting hydration. Conditions gently and leaves skin softer, smoother and more supple.  
  

**Ingredients**

Water, Glycerin, Sorbitan Stearate, Dimethicone, Mineral Oil, Petrolatum, Cetyl Ricinoleate, Dimethiconol, Cetyl Alcohol, Sucrose cocoate, Phenoxyethanol,  Methylparaben, Glyceryl hydroxystearate, Fragrance, Stearic Acid, Steareth-100, Disodium EDTA, Carbomer, Sodium Hydroxide, Propylparaben

  

**Ingredient analysis**  
Glycerin - skin-replenishing and restoring ingredient, helps maintain a healthy look and feel, defending against dryness.  
  
  
Dimethicone, fragrance & paraben- might be irritating, depending on your skin  
  
Dimethicone ni certain orang tak sesuai, although banyak gila product yang contains dimethicone ni. Paraben pun, it is actually okay je, unless your skin is sensitive to paraben. For me, okay je.  

**The packaging:**

The product comes in a 150ml white plastic bottle with a black plastic cap. Very minimal design. I like this kind of packaging sebab just tuang je, and of course it's hygienic and senang nak bawa travel.  
  

**The colour/consistency/texture:**

White lightweight lotion, not too thick.

  

**The scent:**

The smell is really calming, and wangi! Yelah, dah letak fragrance kan... Haha

**My experience**  
Since I have been using this moisturizer everyday since 9 years ago and I don't have anything bad with my skin, kiranya memang ok la kan? I seriously can't live without this (moisturizer, I mean). I need my moisturizer everyday. Kalau tak pakai, rasa hidup ini tak lengkap! Selalu kalau tertinggal, memang gelabah tak tenang hidup.  
  
It moisturizes my skin, suitable for all skin type tapi for oily skin, I think it's a bit greasy. For my skin, it's perfect <3 <3 <3 <3  
  
However, I stopped using this moisturizer, sebab nak try moisturizer lain. And also, Olay is not cruelty-free company. Dia tested on animals.... Walaupun I bukan lah suka binatang sangat pun, tapi macam kesian la jugak dekat binatang yang kena test test tu, padahal boleh je kalau tak test on animals. So yeah, I am trying to avoid company yang tak cruelty-free, including Olay....  
  

  

(Disclaimer: This is not a sponsored post. I bought everything using my own money. And, every skin is different. What works for me, might not work for you. This is just my personal experience)
