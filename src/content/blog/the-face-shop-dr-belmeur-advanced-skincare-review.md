THE FACE SHOP Dr. Belmeur Advanced Skincare Review

  

  
  

My favourite line from THE FACE SHOP is Dr. Belmeur. It is skin friendly ingredient and similar to our skin structure. And currently, they have three ranges that you can try depends on what your skin needs :)

  

**1\. Dr. Belmeur Daily Repair**. I've tried the moisturizer and the ato salt cream. You can read my review here: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/01/the-face-shop-dr-belmeur-daily-repair.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/01/the-face-shop-dr-belmeur-daily-repair.html)

**2\. Dr. Belmeur Clarifying**. This range is targeted for those with oily and acne-prone skin. I haven't tried this range yet.

**3\. Dr. Belmeur Advanced** is for improving damaged sensitive skin due to environmental stress which is what I am going to review in this post.

  

 **Dr. Belmeur Advanced Range** 

Dr. Belmeur Advanced range uses unique technology of **Skin Sync Rx™**, which contains a trio of star ingredients to moisturize, soothe and strengthen skin.

**1\. Ceramide**: to improve your skin barrier. I've talked about our skin barrier here [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/08/skincare-101-skin-barrier.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/08/skincare-101-skin-barrier.html) and because I love ceramide in skincare, I did a post about moisturizers that contains ceramide, here: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/12/moisturizers-with-ceramide-that-i-have.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/12/moisturizers-with-ceramide-that-i-have.html)

**2\. Panthenol**: anti-inflammatory, soothing. Panthenol also known as Vitamin B5. I did mentioned about panthenol is my Vitamins In Skincare post: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2019/02/vitamins-in-skincare.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2019/02/vitamins-in-skincare.html)

**3\. Centella Asiatica Extract**: (contains Madecassoside & Asiaticoside): regeneration of damaged skin. It also soothes the skin, as I mentioned in this post about soothing ingredients: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/11/soothing-ingredients.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/11/soothing-ingredients.html)

  

The products from this range are formulated without 8 additives which are synthetic pigments, artificial fragrance, triethanolamine, mineral oil, bensophenone-3, ethanol, tallc and benzoic acid.  They are delicately-scented with a 100% natural citrus-floral fragrance, however, some people can't tolerate with these essential oils. As for me, I am completely okay with them. It is dermatologically tested and completed skin irritation test on improving fine scratches & skin barrier.

  

I received 3 products from the Dr. Belmeur Advance Cica Recovery range which are their Ampoule, Serum and Cream.
