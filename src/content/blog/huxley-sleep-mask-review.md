Huxley Sleep Mask Review

  

  
  

I bought this mask long long time ago tapi sekarang baru sempat nak review.

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhCo5VksgcD6YcFju_ujiaYGP2Nao5aCTTGga-lMGy7MX6CMQmUZjMi6umXB3i8TgvtROTyaGgektcNKI-C0EeA33blA48aVC63ifqEbae0ZBvyCoQ4_NV-6NDGSsWGClzY2-o5t0CNb2lQ/s1600/1C7F0009-D892-4C35-8D53-280FDF6601E6.jpg)

  

**Ingredients**

Opuntia Ficus-indica Stem Extract, Glycerin, Butylene Glycol, Cyclopentasiloxane, Cyclohexasiloxane, Trehalose, Water, 1,2-hexanediol, Opuntia Ficus-indica Fruit Extract, Ceramide Np(Ceramide 3), Sodium Hyaluronate, Beta-glucan, Centella Asiatica Leaf Extract, Perilla Ocymoides Leaf Extract, Paeonia Suffruticosa Root Extract, Salvia Officinalis (Sage) Leaf Extract, Citrus Grandis (Grapefruit) Seed Extract, Acorus Gramineus Root/stem Extract, Perilla Frutescens Leaf Extract, Theobroma Cacao (Cocoa) Extract, Hydrogenated Lecithin, Ammonium Acryloyldimethyltaurate/vp Copolymer, Polysorbate 20, Dimethicone, Dimethiconol, Carbomer, Dimethicone/vinyl Dimethicone Crosspolymer, Propanediol, Ethylhexylglycerin, Alcohol, Dextrin, Caprylic/capric Triglyceride, Cetyl Ethylhexanoate, Polysorbate 80, Tromethamine, Disodium Edta, Phenoxyethanol, Fragrance.

  

Opuntia Ficus-Indica Stem Extract is prickly pear extract which is one of the best ingredient for antioxidant. Ceramide, beta glucan and centella are for soothing and repairing your skin barrier. However, it contains alcohol and fragrance but kalau hujung hujung list, I okay je. Just be careful if you have fungal acne cause it contains polysorbate.

  

  

  

**Scent**

I love Huxley's scent. It has this distinct herbal-ish scent.

  

**Packaging**

It comes in transparent tube with white flap cap.

  

**Texture**


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhoyV2ARiF3Vj9FaGRxIGdjU19dxBWLUIa0Lxst4RsrsBaYqSt2xZM_lqbCJqOLBXveYqZwyjwG57810XC1aNX3zvKYCWrWlbIpvrR7U00by6AcdXVMi9G4kM6DR6JNq3lw9HagGCtDx2oQ/s1600/C1CDB21E-539A-4D19-9E04-B248505AF7F0.jpg)

Lightweight gel texture

  

**My thoughts**

I bought this mask because there's a lot of good reviews. Although it contains alcohol and fragrance, dia tak buat kulit I rasa pedih and merah. Maybe sebab dia dekat hujung hujung list. I kinda like the Huxley scent, semua products Huxley lebih kurang je bau, macam herbal-ish sikit and so refreshing and buat kulit rasa soothe je.  
  
This mask is lightweight and absorbs nicely into the skin without leaving any greasiness or tacky feeling. If you have oily/combination skin, you might love the texture and how it absorbs nicely into the skin. It is hydrating but I didn't feel it moisturizes my skin enough for my dry skin.  
  
I think this sleeping mask is good for people with oily/combination skin :)
