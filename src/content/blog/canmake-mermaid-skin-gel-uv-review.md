**Packaging and design**

It comes in a 40 ml plastic squeezable tube with a screwable cap. The design is also quite nice with the shiny silver tube and some seashells. Comel. Tapi 40ml ni sikit sangat for a sunscreen. Tak sampai sebulan boleh habis!

  
  

  
**Texture and finishing**

It has a white gel-cream consistency, that leaves a very minimal white cast. Tapi bila dah fully absorb, tak ada pun nampak whitecast sangat. The texture is quite sticky sebab dia tak ada alcohol so dia lambat sikit absorb, tapi bila dah absorb, it leaves a beautiful satin finish.

  

**Scent**

I like the scent. It's almost like coconut, tapi I tak sure lah coconut ke apa, tapi bau dia very mild and best. Tak ada bau sunscreen yang kuat tu.

**Ingredients**

**My experience**

My first impression with this product is that it absorbs quite fast compared to my other sunscreens yang tak ada alcohol, sebab my previous sunscreen memang rasa melekit. And this one tak ada the sticky feeling which I feel a bit weird. Tapi tak rasa kering.

  

Bila pakai lama lama, I makin suka. Dah lah bau best, tak melekit, best pakai under makeup, tak ada whitecast sangat. And sekejap je habis lepastu I sedih sebab cepat sangat habis. Tak puas faham tak?! Nak beli lagi, tapi normal price mahal. Faham tak dilema tu?!

  

For me, ingredients dia okay je. Yang penting tak ada alcohol sebab kalau alcohol banyak sangat, my skin tak sesuai. And tak ada rasa panas ke, breakout ke, merah merah ke, tak ada. It's easy to apply, not drying at all, very comfortable. Dia tak sweat, water, sebum resistant so boleh hilangkan pakai air je. Senang nak pakai and kalau nak solat. 

  

Cuma yang sedihnya, harga dia quite expensive kalau tak ada sale and sikit sangat. Kenapa 40ml je, kenapaaaaaa.

  

Conclusion dia, BEST.

  

  
  

  

  

**Pros**: No alcohol, no fragrance, no paraben, very minimal white cast, nice finish, not drying, easy to apply

  

**Cons**: Small and quite expensive for me

  

  

**Recommended for?**

Dry/normal/combination/sensitive skin

  

**Who will not love this?**

Very oily

  

**Will I repurchase?**

YES OF COURSE (kalau ada sale)
