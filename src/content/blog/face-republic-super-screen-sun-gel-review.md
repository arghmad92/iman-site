**My thoughts on Face Republic Super Screen Sun Gel**  
This sunscreen is a chemical sunscreen since it contains only chemical filters (Octinoxate, Octocrylene, Ethylhexyl Salicylate, Enzacamene, Avobenzone, Tinosorb S). If you prefer a physical / mineral sunscreen, you can read this post: [Mineral / physical sunscreen](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2019/08/mineral-physical-sunscreen.html) . For me, I okay je pakai chemical sunscreen but depends on the formulation jugak.  
  
It's SPF50 and PA+++. SPF rating is the protection from UVB, and I prefer SPF30-50 kalau outdoor. PA rating is the protection from UVA. Paling tinggi is PA++++ (very high protection) and PA+++ is high protection so okay lah to use outdoor.  
  
I like the texture, it has a gel-lotion texture that is very smooth and nicely applied on the skin. Tak melekit bila apply and absorbs nicely into the skin, leaving a nice smooth satin finish. Since I have dry skin, I really like it. There is no whitecast (yang biasa buat kulit nampak kelabu bila pakai sunscreen) cuma the scent tu macam lingers lama jugak lah. Boleh bau the scent kalau orang datang bau kulit kita hahaha.  
  
In terms of the ingredients, it has no alcohol, tapi ada fragrance. If you have very sensitive skin, please check the ingredients first, risau kalau ada any sunscreen filters or ingredient yang tak sesuai with your skin. As for me, I didn't have any issue with the product. Tak buat kulit rasa pedih, gatal, merah merah and it's also can be used under makeup. The price is also quite affordable and easy to get. Worth to try!
