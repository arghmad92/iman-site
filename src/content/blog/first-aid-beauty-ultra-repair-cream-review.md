**Ingredients**

Water/Aqua/Eau, Stearic Acid, Glycerin, C12-15 Alkyl Benzoate, Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride, Glyceryl Stearate Se, Glyceryl Stearate, Cetearyl Alcohol, Butyrospermum Parkii Butter, Dimethicone, Squalane, Allantoin, Camellia Sinensis Leaf Extract, Caprylyl Glycol, Ceramide 3, Chrysanthemum Parthenium Extract, Colloidal Oatmeal, Disodium Edta, Eucalyptus Globulus Leaf Oil, Glycyrrhiza Glabra Root Extract, Sodium Hydroxide, Xanthan Gum, Phenoxyethanol.

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgX94CaAKuVZ6e02TVppctecZjuzdE0D3uixTO8BjnkovxOPq-gLZreV3DOrWCQ343slzFgpEhoUVXK5Q3QpQNTTo2X65otwfMZt_x0RQ93v7Oy0SUX4T6x454egMxG2xcGExO36n_s1nUp/s1600/776E66EB-E55E-4CC5-B1EE-C4EEF5A09328.jpg)

  

  

**Scent**

Medicated scent. Bau macam ubat or pergi hospital which I don't really mind pun.

  

**Texture**

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhRNyfsBkyB83o36shJpncddR4fvGdhn3e8fwNlmU4cuSc9MQauYomeCFxUCqXej6rt2L1kcQJ3o-u0PV__beXiGaZnI_tQ8bio47ocH6zwiUTdAPyQbG2OPLWoxho-fWJDq6UY1OT-7WTs/s1600/F86CD165-2616-48FF-B4A1-71D810E17A76.jpg)

White cream. The texture is quite thick but it absorbs nicely into the skin with no greasy feeling.

  

  

  

**My thoughts**

It contains no alcohol, fragrance, mineral oil, paraben, petrolatum which is good for sensitive skin.

  

It contains glycerin, ceramide and squalane which is good for your skin barrier.

  

Read about skin barrier: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/08/skincare-101-skin-barrier.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/08/skincare-101-skin-barrier.html)  
  
I love this moisturizer since it didn't irritate my skin, moisturizes my skin and tak buat rasa melekit or greasy. It contains essential oil (eucalyptus) but my skin is okay with it.  
  
It also contains soothing ingredients (Allantoin, Camellia Sinensis Leaf Extract, Chrysanthemum Parthenium Extract and Colloidal Oatmeal) which is good for redness or irritation.  
  
The only problem I have with this moisturizer is it pills, especially kalau reapply. (Pills = keluar macam daki daki) so kena layer my products properly. Botton line, this is a good moisturizer for dry skin!
