**Why I bought it?** 

I have been wanting to try this moisturizer since I saw some good reviews about this product. There’s a few products that I wanted to try from Paula’s Choice. Tapi the price is a bit steep so sabar dulu. Then tiba tiba ada sale 20% so yes, Im so gonna try this one dulu! Tapi beli satu je lah, yang lain kita sabar dulu hehe. I bought this one for RM152 (normal price RM190). 

  

  

 **Description:**

Omega+ Complex Moisturizer immediately hydrates, soothes, and steadily addresses signs of dry, flaky skin. A network of natural replenishing ingredients instantly infuses dehydrated skin with lasting moisture that restores youthful suppleness, resilience, and vibrancy.

It comes in a 50ml light blue plastic bottle with a pump and transparent plastic cap. It feels expensive (dah memang harga dia quite expensive haha) and sturdy. All the details are printed clearly on the packaging.

 Cuma I tak minat sangat packaging macam ni sebab tak nampak berapa banyak lagi yang tinggal. Tahu tahu je dah habis. Tapi elok lah packaging like this, more hygienic and nak jaga the formula. 

  

  

 **Texture**: 

White thick cream 

  

![](https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-PH2YExKOnDo/XpF3PhTaptI/AAAAAAAAJOA/yoYInjTPej4aIJDlYyncstGMVKPAJOVHwCLcBGAsYHQ/s640/7C83E5AD-3718-45CF-ACF7-CA3165C1094D.JPG)

  

 **Scent**: 

No fragrance. A bit plastic-ish scent. (Bila bau lama lama, it reminds me of gas entonox yang dapat time nak bersalin tu....) 

  

  

  

 **Thoughts**: 

Oh I looooooove this. Or am I a bit biased sebab dah spend duit so terpaksa la cakap best? Haha ok tak lah. I reaaaaally love this and sedih bila dia dah habis. Rasa nak terus beli lagi sebab nanti rindu :(

  

 Since I have dry skin, I love the texture. It’s quite thick but absorbs nicely into my skin, leaving my skin feels moisturized and soft. Dia rasa macam heavy sikit, tapi in a nice way yang my skin suka and tak rasa berpeluh peluh or tebal macam pakai cream tu. I really love the texture and formula. So nice!  

  

It contains ceramides, antioxidants and omega fatty acids which is good to repair your skin barrier and healthier skin. Takde alcohol, takde fragrance, takde essential oils, takde irritating ingredients. Bila pakai, takde rasa pedih pedih ke panas panas or buat kulit rasa gatal or merah. 

  

 If you are looking for a good moisturizer for drier skin type that is packed with great ingredients, you can try this. Setakat yang I pakai, memang kulit tak rasa kering, tight, takde dry patches langsung. Smooth je selalu! Totally worth the money. 

  

 **Verdict**: 5/5 - Totally recommended for drier skin type and will absolutely repurchase
