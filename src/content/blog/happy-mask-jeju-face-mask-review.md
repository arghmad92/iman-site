All six Happy Masks that was sent to me. So this is my thoughts on them.

  

  
  

I received 6 types which are:

1\. Jeju Tsubaki (Ultra Soothing)

2\. Jeju Lotus Flower (Brightening)

3\. Jeju Lily (Brightening)

4\. Seaweed (Moisturizing)

5\. Green Tea (Anti-Oxidant)

6\. Aloe Vera (Ultra Soothing)

  

**How to use?**

Apply on cleansed face for 10-15mins and then remove from face. Massage slightly with fingertips to aid absorption of the excess essence on skin. No need to rinse. Discard mask after use. Use daily for best result.

It is specially developed with AQUAPlux to provide ultimate skin hydration, leaving skin feeling moist and relaxed after every single mask enjoyment.

  

  

  

  

  

It is free from **parabens** (you can read about paraben here: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/06/skincare-101-paraben-bahaya-ke-tak.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/06/skincare-101-paraben-bahaya-ke-tak.html) ), **TEA** (triethanolamine which acts as pH balancer and emulsion stabilizer but may be dangerous for your skin, however the amount used in cosmetics is normally 1%. Kalau risau, better avoid), **mineral oil** (depends on your skin, my skin okay je kalau ada mineral oil) and **colourant**. It is also free from alcohol but they didn't mention it in the description. Rasanya bagus juga kalau diorang mention since ramai yang nak avoid alcohol.

  

  

The common star ingredients for all of these masks are:

\- **Glycerin**: A humectant (attract water). Best for hydration.

\- **Panthenol**: Also known as pro vitamin B5, ability to attract and hold moisture

\- **Allantoin**: Soothing ingredient

\- **Dipotassium Glycyrrhizate**: Also known as Licorice Extract and one of the best ingredient for soothing and improve the skin tone.

  

It also contains hydrolyzed collagen. The source is from marine. The fragrance in these masks are quite high, so if you have sensitive skin, you might wanna avoid this.

To deliver skin hydration, leaving it soft and supple. Some star ingredients (selain daripada I mention kat atas tu) are Laminaria Japonica Extract (**kelp = brown seaweed** for soothing and moisture), Undaria Pinnatifida Extract (**brown algae**), Camellia Sinensis Leaf Extract (**green tea**), and Nelumbo Nucifera Flower Water (**lotus**). My skin feels well hydrated after using this sheet mask and there's also some soothing effect. As for the claims, yes, it makes my skin feels soft and supple.
