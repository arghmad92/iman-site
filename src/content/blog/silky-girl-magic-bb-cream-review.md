## Silky Girl Magic BB Cream Review

  

**Packaging**

It comes with a 20mL light blue squeezable plastic tube. Easy to dispense since it has small hole. Very nice packaging and easy for travelling.

  

![](https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-3fzV9E-hVWg/WaQRlTz0J6I/AAAAAAAAEhg/nRQkQa8zMCMWR0RIEP9yQzGWhAwWtWjmgCLcBGAs/s640/Photo%2B28-08-2017%252C%2B8%2B31%2B38%2BPM%2B%25282%2529.jpg)

  

**Scent**

Very distinct chemical smell which I don't really like

  

**Texture/consistency**

Creamy texture that glides smoothly. However, it emphasis my dry patches. It has a medium coverage, able to conceal my redness besides my nose.

  

![](https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-lhmbpGaSsrg/WaQRleBU8sI/AAAAAAAAEhk/qLAy5W71_bExjgRUsTnXp4sIrebDJlCHQCLcBGAs/s640/Photo%2B28-08-2017%252C%2B8%2B31%2B38%2BPM.jpg)

  

**Coverage**

Medium coverage but buildable

  

![](https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-rl-iU3Yvvfg/WaQR7QAN4OI/AAAAAAAAEhw/E8hxoGiQw0s8osfZJRQ6ES6EkRrjg1acwCLcBGAs/s640/Screen%2BShot%2B2017-08-28%2Bat%2B8.51.32%2BPM.png)

  

  

**My review**

Honestly, I don't like it. It emphasis my dry patches, very drying, cakey and cracks when I apply. It also becomes orange-ish, bercapuk, entah lah I don't know, but my skin looks 76587 worst when applying this product. I think this product memang tak sesuai for dry-skin, but if you have oily skin, you can try this out.

  

But the good thing about this product, it doesn't breaks me out, tak rasa gatal, tak rasa pedih, tak ada merah merah. No irritation. It also has SPF30 and PA+++, and can cover imperfection.

  

  

**Recommended?**

Not really, if you have dry skin.

  

  

Performance: 2/5 - I don't really like it

Quality: 3/5 - Ingredients are quite good

Value: 3/5 - Price is quite affordable

  

**Overall**: **2.67/5**
