**Important: IC both parents, buku pink/checkup**

1\. Comfy outfit untuk duduk dalam ward & baju nak balik

2\. Towel. Kat sini I ada share [bath towels yang best](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2021/09/top-5-towels.html)

3\. Socks. Lagi fluffy lagi best! [Fluffy socks](https://shp.ee/d9gbe3t)

4\. Bengkung. Contohnya yang [instant and adjustable macam ni](https://shp.ee/mdpg56t)

5\. Skincare

6\. Toiletries

7\. Telekung, sejadah & Quran - sementara tunggu sebelum bersalin

8\. [Nursing pad](https://shp.ee/9hr27ht)

9\. Maternity pad. I suka either brand [Kotex Overnight Panties](https://shp.ee/2ahvq5t) atau [Librese Overnight Panties](https://shp.ee/aj5fakt)

9\. Botol air

10\. Foods. I suka dried fruits kat sini: [Mahnaz Food](https://shp.ee/67rbtwt)

11\. Nursing pillow

](https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-2YVKaBioIjA/YTh459ZRkSI/AAAAAAAAJ8c/pi3A2JQF-Pw2F_Ouap0OLYuZ99NAppwbgCLcBGAsYHQ/s1192/Screenshot%2B2021-09-08%2Bat%2B4.48.15%2BPM.png)

  

1\. Baju baby lengkap + diaper. Susun siap siap ikut hari.

2\. Toiletries - Kalau hospital private, normally akan dapat sekali dalam package. Depends on hospital

3\. Baby wipes. I suka [Pureen Baby Wipes](https://shp.ee/ecx8iuc)

4. [Baby towel](https://shp.ee/skve4ge)

5. [Muslin Wrap](https://shp.ee/vk6u4jy) untuk bedung baby or as blanket

  

**Labour room bag**

Labour room bag ni biasanya bag kecil je untuk bawa pergi labour room. Contohnya phone, charger, al-quran, sejadah & telekung, bekalan makanan, air, baju untuk salin and barang barang baby.

  

**Daddy's Stuff**

Barang barang daddy pun perlu juga. Baju, toiletries, cash, charger etc.
