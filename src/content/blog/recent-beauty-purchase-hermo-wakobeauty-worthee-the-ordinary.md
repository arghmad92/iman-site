**What I bought:**

**1\. Cosrx Acne Pimple Pad** - Ada 70 pads, I nak guna as toner, just sapu je cotton pad tu dekat muka

**2\. Cosrx Acne Pimple Patch** \- Ni I dah beli haritu, dah review dah, memang best. Wajib ada dalam simpanan, just in case naik jerawat, tempek je. Seriously I suka! Review here: [Cosrx Acne Pimple Master Patch Review](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/06/cosrx-acne-pimple-master-patch-review.html)

**3\. Benton Snail Bee High Content Steam Cream** - Sebab ada banyak great reviews. Good for scars, moisturizing, kalau ada any blemishes ke. Tak sabar nak guna yang ni!

**4\. TheFaceShop Face Mask** - ni sebab RM1 je so I tambah je la dalam cart haha

**Worthee Cosmetics** - Paula's Choice authorized reseller. I bough 2% BHA Liquid. I read some reviews yang kata this Paula's Choice BHA is better than Cosrx BHA so I'm gonna give it a try. Yang ni size kecil je, RM45. You guys can check their website.

  

More on AHA & BHA: [Exfoliation, AHA & BHA](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/09/exfoliate-aha-bha.html)
