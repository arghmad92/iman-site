Cosrx Ultimate Moisturizing Honey Overnight Mask Review

  

  

  

  

  

  

  

  

  
  

  

I bought this mask during my trip to Korea. Dulu dia dalam jar so I malas nak try, bila dalam tube ni nampak menarik pula so I pun beli je lah :)

  

  

  

  

**Ingredients**

Propolis Extract, Butylene Glycol, Glycerin, Betaine, 1,2-Hexanediol, PEG-60 Hydrogenated Castor Oil, Arginine, Cassia Obtusifolia Seed Extract, Dimethicone, Ethylhexylglycerin, Carbomer, Sodium Hyaluronate, BeesWax, Allantoin, Panthenol, Sodium Polyacrylate, Adenosine  
  

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEijaSDDdgKVpAujFV0PFdcZDqiO1cMt_o_zW2vMRIkQIDdzZjZoIRjw1Zqy2mmVObvDvxz5ow9t4TRy0rjdkRTxm6ssIMZWMi9QhGmGP0yBfEiwGXsklVs7KvqMajnmwaaq00dICuJmJYSm/s1600/841B98BF-192C-4D6D-9CB1-EA73485D22D2.JPG)  
  

  

If you have fungal acne, you might wanna avoid this since it contains PEG-60 Hydrogenated Castor Oil that may not be suitable for you. Also, it contains Butylene Glycol and Dimethicone that may clog your pores, depends on some people. For me, I am totally okay with the ingredients.

  

**Scent**

No noticeable scent

  

**Packaging**  

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgkRT5zqg1ggOjO7Q2VbqA7rcUOMtPtMtV1atu2QT1A4kwZHv3fcYQA3G47x8MD_izxahrIovLILS0921mIGbc4uZrBJxKWsrpgzSNgeQ3FlFAzLi6EUZVh6QOMx-g3Pw5mPiv_13sowEg9/s1600/7DBEAFD9-4047-41B6-88BE-61FF9AF62084.JPG)

It comes in a 60ml tube packaging with a flap cap which I really like! I don't like jar packaging sebab malas nak celup celup.

  

**Texture**  

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhV_HaJjHNSwxiXp0Ir1RvBa_ZVynPpJ8YgWdmDsM6yBZk15NiGvcGYVSDrMXaOtuihZ7hBkTBJhJlwHW6jh0xkIo4BFaFvNuDZI-6lAbkSL87ys5pQJak-q_dWpyPCkFbV5eYcW8hyphenhyphenfaEs/s1600/C5113085-8F89-4F56-BCE6-965516497DFE.jpg)

It's like a gel texture, very lightweight and absorbs into my skin nicely without any greasy feeling

  

  

  

**My thoughts**

I love the ingredients since it contains a lot of soothing properties. Propolis Extract is very soothing and a good antioxidant as well as Allantoin, Panthenol and Sodium Hyaluronate. I also love that it doesn't have alcohol or fragrance in it so it is suitable for sensitive skin.

  

However, since I have dry skin, this mask it not enough for me. It is soothing, but not moisturizing enough. For me, I personally think this mask is suitable for normal/combination skin since it absorbs nicely into the skin without feeling greasy at all. If you have dry skin and you want a sleeping mask that can reduce your dryness, better find other masks.

  

Overall, I really like this mask! The ingredient is good and the packaging is good. I normally use this as my normal moisturizer during the day if I feel like using it.
