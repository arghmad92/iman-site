**1\. SAFI Ampoule Renewal Hydra Complex**

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiSf-UQb6k_jDBYA4f34M1LoY709Px11d7h4CT6gyXrWBNLJI7Jx6syKfr1KyFrUAz5zvGZA2zYvxJobHdBVfw6jKgADtxUaLTvYJSF1PLi1-X74UJ_7pZQx_kHbwQYoGT9mZT8du18DTFh/s1600/FFAC4FFA-D629-4846-AB48-DB77DEE7DFA9.jpg)

  

This is a new product from SAFI. It contains Mandelic Acid 5% which is a chemical exfoliate. You can read about exfoliator here: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2017/09/exfoliate-aha-bha.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2017/09/exfoliate-aha-bha.html)

  

It is quite hard to find a chemical exfoliator dekat drugstore and I am so happy that SAFI recently launched this product. Tapi dia dalam pack. Hopefully in the future dia jual individual. A good chemical exfoliate if you are a beginner, since Mandelic Acid is a gentle AHA.

  

  

**2\. IUNIK Vitamin Hyaluronic Acid Vitalizing Toner**

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEh5D5RGaV0dxh0-J2L9U5TK-QBS6upxeLYlXhX6TzC9UcoTyGHTw2eTatfG7YmcYyYJCST5xrImp5HdA7REEqhjrl5fuYZ1ro940EnE58os2OIFH1oapQBaTzw1TvF5UAHGy_lZVhMMK8_c/s1600/3E354F45-0B94-4D1E-9698-128A4CC1D674.JPG)

  

I got this from IG @alittleloving . This one is 200ml and I guna lama gila, like 7-8 months! Lepastu gelabah bila dah habis sebab tak fikir nak ganti dengan toner apa. It is a bit thick, almost like gel, tak watery macam toner biasa. I like this toner! A good hydrating toner.

  

Also read, Hydrating Toners That I've Tried: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/07/hydrating-toners.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/07/hydrating-toners.html)
