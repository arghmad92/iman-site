**My morning skincare routine**

Previously, I just use cleanser, first treatment essence, moisturizer and sunscreen. Tapi semua products yang i pakai 5 months ago, semua dah emptied. Right now, I have added few serums and a toner.

  

My skincare routine is not fixed. Tengok lah mood macam mana. Yang wajib is just:

1\. Cleanse

2\. Moisturize

3\. Sunscreen (kalau keluar)

  

Yang lain lain tu add on je.

  

[!

](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEh8Ceg-HqUKFi0ZmW2U_ynJmPTKOISloehFJdw-co8mFy72xXYkHKwOgTemm52vyx7tmonNoX7GYPs79EyH4s9lSYxFBuHSFIWMLUg4WbR00wAVBefyRby1dv0QdV0OifGn3uJ-Cn2rKrhS/s1600/Photo+29-12-2017%252C+11+57+56+AM.jpg)

  

**1\. Cleanser** - _Glossier Milky Jelly Cleanser_. This cleanser is one of my wishlist since I love Glossier's design, and it is very gentle with low pH. I also love the smell, it's kind of a lychee smell which is sooo good. Doesn't make my skin feels tight. Gonna write a full review about this cleanser soon <3

  

**2\. Toner** - _Pixi Glow Tonic_. I've wrote the review here: [Pixi Glow Tonic Review](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/12/pixi-glow-tonic-review.html). Currently my fav! Makes my skin very glowing and improve my skin texture.

  

**3\. Serums**. I'm using _The Ordinary Hyaluronic Acid_ and _The Ordinary Niacinamide 10% + Zinc_. I love the combination of both serum in my routine. Makes my skin hydrated and reduce my pores + glowing.

Review: [The Ordinary Niacinamide Review](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/11/the-ordinary-niacinamide-10-zinc-1.html)

  

**4\. Moisturizer** - Currently I am using _The Face Shop Dr Belmeur Moisturizer_. I kept changing my moisturizer sebab tengah try mana yang best. Currently I like this one, but will keep trying others sampai jumpa yang best. Yang best setakat ni, for my dry skin is MUJI Moisturizing Milk. Kalau dah giveup tak jumpa jugak yang best, I'll buy this one semula. Right now, still exploring.

  

[!

](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjOPPBhWJitFgrogo-nOghzEkuCY8tE4trxAhc0e24RQro7d_vuclBfjINnQshatNOQ7LzvvtdvfuvV01BG9a8PhYJIPpcTVrAP8rM4L6WeI53gvhC-ZDmUad4WHRlYl2WFQS6hAba-q_ZZ/s1600/Photo+29-12-2017%252C+12+03+56+PM.jpg)

I've tried both, the Ato Salt Cream which have quite some good reviews. But I prefer the moisturizer more. 

  

**5\. Sunscreen** - _Hada Labo Air Aqua UV_. Good for my dry skin. I like it. But will explore other sunscreen sebab nak cari yang the best for me. Also read: [Hada Labo Air Aqua UV review](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/12/hada-labo-air-aqua-uv-spf50pa-day.html)

  

That is all for my morning routine.
