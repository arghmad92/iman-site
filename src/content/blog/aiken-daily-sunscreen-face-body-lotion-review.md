Aiken Daily Sunscreen Face & Body Lotion Review

  
  
  
  

I bought this at Caring Pharmacy around RM14+. Tapi rasanya dekat any pharmacy pun ada. Watsons/Guardian tak pernah nampak lagi but I might be wrong.

  

  
  

  

**What it claims?**

A complete sun protection with UVA & UVB filters that protects skin from signs of premature ageing, sunburn and skin redness.

  

**Ingredients**


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjs-ToWKViMbdTe_USPxC0o9cBydDQup2v82A_9XqBvPjXF_Sj7dk5j6MqvN-nBHK5_eUOWKOp-kmY1gOaBi-Zl-SCWVZtH_Nq8fDKQsKsoxMjWnsPhHApbQauloTFHGVXbJdt1EqcbW_eP/s1600/EFB5DFB0-3DC8-47F1-9260-2E0F5662761E+2.jpg)

  

**Sunscreen filters:**

1. Ethylhexyl methoxycinnamate (Octinoxate)

2. Octocrylene

3. Methylene bis-benzotriazolyl tetramethylbutylphenol (Tinosorb M)

  

  
  

  

**Why I bought it?**

Because it's pretty cheap, RM14+ for 150g? That's really affordable! And it has no alcohol in it.

  

**Packaging and design**  

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEh9WzaMLMTzyi5wHQ7Zt3yjTYieIWSt_2ufBq8vrlasCNHQajKCKI4MxXqj_sonXVM5U0WOz3bzbqTSlPpWMSQDMG6BjJktEBamYjRivUr_RjB_l1WKdPCsNA3hsKiE-yYU02RQ-eCPWe7r/s1600/68B13801-3E3E-40D1-AEA6-BBF8C3C19128+2.jpg)

It comes in a 150g white plastic squeezable bottle with a flap cap. First impression nampak macam packaging cleanser tapi bukan.   
  
  
  

**Texture and finishing**  

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEi9KLufNqG7DXR4Ny1Bs1rI3vbM7i_AEJCzdA19NExR9vwEqjVmuzNl8-qZ_Aq-cxAqvIUyIjP9jGoA8WmwtOYCAONwYjOzN-TX5bRLToXc-32M25QqkMqB3hbQniCmloy4dQAsTOm1xUb-/s1600/392C8677-D866-4934-8473-E7C51FDEC290+2.jpg)

  

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEi3A6ouTkGj3GTkBt8I1N9hCZVfQMHpZBinGJ88WAGzOrGirlI1Khu26A-622jjaj6IrhkuPQOy3yiJUW4kIuT6e-dAT9wCfUXgan0XxKGmZUZ2SdtWCpf-dYXId5IkP2-z2HXIgVcenMoA/s1600/22C3F255-1E9D-49CE-A91B-56526CBB9AF3+2.jpg)

  

  
It has a lotion texture which is quite thick and greasy especially for oilier skin type. However kalau pakai dekat tangan or kaki and tak berpeluh sangat, should be okay. Or, if you have drier skin type.

  

**Scent**

The fragrance is VERY strong. Macam sabun LUX tapi kuat sangat wangi dia. Which I don't really prefer. Kalau pakai dekat bahagian badan, okay lah. Kalau dekat muka, kena tahan nafas. I don't like it at all.

  

  

**My thoughts**

Apart from the scent, this is a good sunscreen for me. although the texture is quite greasy, but I like how it feels on my skin, especially on days yang I malas pakai makeup and nak keluar pakai sunscreen je. And since size dia besar and harga quite affordable, takde lah rasa sedih bila pakai banyak banyak.

  

If you have sensitive skin, you might wanna avoid this since it contains Octocrylene which may cause sensitivity but for me, I can use this sunscreen.

  

  

  

  

**

**Recommended for?**

**  

Drier skin type

  

**Who will not love this?**

Very oily skin

  

**Will I repurchase?**

Nope, unless they come out with no fragrance
