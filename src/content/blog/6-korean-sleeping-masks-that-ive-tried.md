Here are some korean sleeping masks that I have tried.

  
  
  

**Cosrx Ultimate Moisturizing Honey Overnight Mask**

This mask is good if you are looking for a soothing product as it contains Propolis Extract, Cassia Obtusifolia Seed Extract and beeswax. It has a gel texture which absorbs nicely into your skin and suitable for normal to combination skin type.  
  
I like it because it is in a tube packaging, has no fragrance, no alcohol and no irritating ingredients. However, if you have fungal acne, you might want to avoid this since it contains PEG-60 Hydrogenated Castor Oil and few ingredients that may clog your pores.  
  

Full review: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2019/01/cosrx-ultimate-moisturizing-honey.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2019/01/cosrx-ultimate-moisturizing-honey.html)  
  

  

  

**Klairs Freshly Juiced Vitamin E Mask**

This mask is good if you are looking for a brightening mask since it contains niacinamide which is a good brightening ingredients. I like the pudding texture and the "tacky" feeling on my skin. I love my sleeping mask that way, biar I rasa ada something dekat kulit since I have dry skin. The herbal-ish scent is also refreshing.

  

It contains no fragrance, no alcohol and no silicone. However, you might wanna avoid this if you have fungal acne because it contains PEG-60 Hydrogenated Castor Oil and if you are sensitive with essential oils, this is not for you.

  

Full review: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/04/klairs-freshly-juiced-vitamin-e-mask.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/04/klairs-freshly-juiced-vitamin-e-mask.html)

  

**Laneige Water Sleeping Mask**

This product was once a very famous product and my first sleeping mask. I really love the refreshing scent and the blue gel texture makes it so pretty!

  

After trying other sleeping masks, I feel Laneige Water Sleeping Mask is **overrated**. Yes it's a good sleeping mask tapi ada banyak lagi option lain that you can try with cheaper price. It contains alcohol, fragrance, colorant, silicone and not fungal acne safe.

  

Full review: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2017/01/review-laneige-water-sleeping-mask.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2017/01/review-laneige-water-sleeping-mask.html)

  

**Huxley Sleep Mask**

I love Huxley's signature herbal-ish relaxing scent. It has a lightweight texture and absorbs nicely into the skin. If you have oily or combination skin, you might love this.

  

It contains alcohol and essential oils. It is also not fungal acne safe and not silicone free.

  

You can read about alcohol here: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/06/skincare-101-alcohol-in-skincare.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/06/skincare-101-alcohol-in-skincare.html)

  

Full review: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2019/01/huxley-sleep-mask-review.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2019/01/huxley-sleep-mask-review.html)

  

  

  

  

**Innisfree Orchid Sleeping Pack**

I only have a sample size so there is no full review on this product. This is a good sleeping mask if you want a brightening and moisturizing mask. It contains arbutin which is a good brightening ingredients and a cream texture which is good if you have drier skin type.

  

It is alcohol free but it contains fragrance, essential oils, silicones and not safe for fungal acne.

  

**Glow Recipe Watermelon Glow Sleeping Mask**

This is also one of my wishlist yang dah lama sangat I teringin nak try. I tak buat full review lagi but if dah review, I'll update here.

  

I loooove the watermelon scent and the pink gel texture. It looks so pretty on my vanity. The best part is that it contains AHA which is good for your skin. It's fungal acne safe but it contains alcohol and fragrance. This sleeping mask is good if you want a brightening mask!
