Common questions.  
  
**Boleh ke pakai Niacinamide and Vitamin C in the same routine?**  
\- At first masa awal awal I baca about Niacinamide & Vitamin C, I rasa memang tak boleh pakai in the same routine. Sebab katanya, Vitamin C and Niacinicamide boleh cancel out each other or neutralizes the effectiveness  
\- Lepastu I baca lagi, only pure vitamin C yang L-Ascorbic Acid (LAA) je tak boleh pakai in the same routine with niacinamide sebab reaction dia boleh buat flushing (muka merah/irritation) lagi lagi kulit sensitive.  
\- However, when I asked around twitter, ramai yang kata, diorang pakai LAA with Niacinamide, okay je. In fact, tak ada masalah pun. (20% LAA, 10% Niacinamide)  
\- My opinion, you **can** use Niacinamide and Vitamin C in the same routine. Dia tak cancel out each other, and dia punya reaction yang jadi flushing, buat kulit jadi merah merah tu, ONLY in certain conditions (high temperature, bukan temperature kat rumah kita yang environment biasa biasa je)  
\- Kalau nak tahu irritate ke tak, try sendiri.  
\- My experience? Tak ada apa apa benda pelik yang berlaku. Maybe because I use 5% LAA je, so tak ada irritation.  
\- Kalau was was dan ragu ragu, pakai lah asing asing, pagi & malam. Semuanya depends dekat kulit masing masing.  
  
Dekat website The Ordinary, dia dah mention,  
"_If topical Vitamin C is used as part of skincare, it should be applied at **alternate times** with this formula (ideally Vitamin C in the PM and this formula in the AM). Otherwise, Niacinamide can affect integrity of pure-form Vitamin C (L-Ascorbic Acid)._" So kalau risau Niacinamide will effect Vitamin C, pakai Niacinamide waktu pagi, Vitamin C waktu malam.  
  
  
**Boleh ke pakai Niacinamide dengan acids (AHA/BHA) in the same routine?**  
\- For me, boleh je. Untuk acid & niacinamide react yang boleh menyebabkan kulit irritate, dia kena guna strong acid. Acid (AHA/BHA) yang kita guna normally tu, pH dia tak rendah sangat.  
\- Jadi, kalau ada masalah pores, pakai lah BHA and Niacinamide. Kalau nak glowing, pakai Niacinamide and Vitamin C. I use both, macam I mention dekat atas tu.
