**Essence Volume Stylist Curl & Hold Mascara Review (Pink)**

This is a good mascara. It volumize, curls and lengthen my lashes. It doesn't clump and separated my lashes. I really like how it looks on me. It's not waterproof (I prefer a non waterproof mascara since senang nak remove untuk solat ke apa). It doesn't smudge, doesn't flake and doesn't feel heavy at all. It's easy to apply too! However, it is quite messy to remove, habis hitam bawah mata and susah nak hilangkan hitam tu. So, I malas nak pakai sebab malas nak beriya cuci cause I know it will be very messy.

  

Rate: 3.5/5 - It's a good mascara and very affordable, cuma susah nak bersihkan :(

**Essence Volume Stylist Lash Extension Mascara Review (Purple)**

It volumize and lengthen my lashes, but not so much on curl. Contains lengthening fibres for fuller and longer lashes. It's not clumpy, not waterproof, and didn't smudge a lot. It doesn't feel heavy, rasa selesa je pakai. It's easy to apply and lagi senang nak cuci compared to the previous one. However, it is quite hard to apply on my lower lashes cause it tends to be a bit messy.

  

Rate: 4/5
