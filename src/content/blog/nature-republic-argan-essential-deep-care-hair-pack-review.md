**What it claims?** 

Changes damaged hair into silky and shiny hair. No silicon. Nutritional care pack that offers intensive care to damaged hair by blow dry, perm, and hair dying.  
  

**How to use?**

After shampoo, apply it evenly on damaged hair. Wear a hair cap for better effect. Wash it off with lukewarm water after 5-10 minutes. Recommended to use once or twice a week.

  

**Packaging**

Brown plastic jar with screw-off plastic lid

**Colour/consistency:**

Thick buttery cream texture

  

**Scent:**

Argan oil + fragrance

  

**How I feel:**

I LOVE THIS THING. My hair becomes very soft and silky smooth. This thing really works. The smell is also very nice. Wangiiii. Lepas shampoo, I apply this mask masa rambut still tengah basah, and biar for 5-10 minutes. Dia quite thick and rasa macam buttery cream sikit. Lepastu bilas. Masa bilas tu terus rasa alahai lembutnya rambut.  
  
Tapi lepastu, lantai toilet jadi melekit/greasy hahaha so kena bersihkan lantai pulak. Sebenarnya, dia lebih kurang macam conditioner je. I do this once a week.  
  
Memang rambut jadi lagi lembut, wangi and senang nak urus. Tapi bukan forever lah. Sebab tu kena selalu pakai.

  

**Recommended?**

YESSS, if you have dry/damaged hair. Please try thissss.
