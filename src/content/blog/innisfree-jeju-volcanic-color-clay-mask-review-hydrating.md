**The packaging:**

This clay mask comes in a 70ml blue plastic tube. I love the packaging, tak macam the normal Innisfree punya clay mask yang dalam jar tu because that one, can be unhygienic. This one best sebab bila nak guna, just picit je. VERY EASYYY!  
  

**The colour/consistency/texture:**

It's literally blue! The texture is just like the normal clay mask, less drying.

  

**The scent:**

Very refreshing scent, I LOVE.

**How to use?**

Normally I use this after cleanser & toner. Apply, tunggu dia berubah warna (around 10 minutes) lepastu terus basuh. Then apply moisturizer.

  

Sometimes I apply in the morning, lepastu double cleanse. Use only once a week.

  

**How I feel:**

I've tried the clay mask in the mousse form, read here: [\[REVIEW\] INNISFREE SUPER VOLCANIC CLAY MOUSSE MASK](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2016/10/review-innisfree-super-volcanic-clay.html)  
  
But that one irritates me, ye lah dry skin kalau pakai clay mask, lagi lah drying. Oily & combination skin je sesuai. Dry skin ni depends, sometimes memang pedih & drying.  
  
But this one, really soothing! Tak tahu lah hydrating sangat tak, tapi best. Rasa pores semua bersih!  
  
**Pros**  
Packaging is great, really works for me, easy to get.  
  
**Cons**  
The price is quite pricey, but other than that, best!  
  
  

**Recommended?**

YES.
