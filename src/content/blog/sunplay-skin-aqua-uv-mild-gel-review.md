You can get this from any drugstore (Watsons/Guardian) for around RM40. Kalau sale dapat RM30++ je.  
  
**What it claims?**  
\- Protects skin against environmental pollution including PM2.5 particles.  
\- With 3 hydrating ingredients for long lasting hydration  
\- Super light & watery texture that spreads easily over skin for an invisible weightless feel.  
\- Mild & Gentle Formula  
\- Free of fragrance, colorant, alcohol and mineral oil.  
  
**Why I bought it?**  
Because it has no alcohol and fragrance. Tu je sebab I teringin nak try hehe  
  
  
  
**Packaging and design**

It comes in a 110 ml yellow plastic squeezable bottle with a white screwable cap. Dah kata dah, packaging macam ni ada potensi penutup hilang. So thats the case lah. The bottle is also opaque, tak transparent so tak nampak berapa banyak yang tinggal.   
  
![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhgfW1JQ2orEvKQM3ZAjy_9XV5MUp4uIaLNJaNX9fZv-inW6SS_j8xtWO2iUA13ORBe8mPVPKAklqU_-pBzn0zdFYaEWgITrPlP9c4NsYe8E4rlkdm44S0tNZofDiDEt1Y_I-F0ko5zKnBZ/s640/Screen+Shot+2018-07-02+at+4.48.53+PM.png)  
  
  

**Texture and finishing**

  

It has a white gel consistency, with a very very minimal white cast. Almost tak ada white cast. I SUUUPER LOVE the texture. Easy to spread and it absorbs beautifully without any sticky or greasy feeling and makes my skin looks dewy, gosh I love the texture on my skin. Very comfortable!

**Ingredients**

_Water, Ethylhexyl Methoxycinnamate, Butylene Glycol, Pentylene Glycol, Diethylamino Hydroxybenzoyl Hexyl Benzoate, Polyglyceryl-10 Pentaisostearate, Phenoxyethanol, Triethanolamine, Acrylates/C10-30 Alkyl Acrylate Crosspolymer, Methylparaben, Peg-12 Dimethicone, Xanthan Gum, Disodium Edta, Glycol Dimethacrylate Crosspolymer, Stearoyl Inulin, Polystyrene, Polyvinyl Alcohol, Sodium Hyaluronate, Arginine, Ammonium Acrylates Copolymer, Hydrolyzed Collagen, Polyquaternium-51_  
  
The UV filters are Ethylhexyl Methoxycinnamate (Octinoxate) that may cause allergic reaction to some people (tapi I okay je) and Diethylamino Hydroxybenzoyl Hexyl Benzoate (Univul A Plus) which is a very good UVA protection. About the paraben, you can read here: [Paraben, bahaya ke tak?](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/06/skincare-101-paraben-bahaya-ke-tak.html)  
  
  
And about the collagen, I try PM dekat Facebook and Sunplay Malaysia claims it's not from animal derivatives. Tapi macam mana collagen bukan daripada animal.... Since plants tak ada collagen. Maybe from marine / fish tapi tu dikira animal derivatives....? Takpelah, asalkan bukan daripada babi.

**My experience**

I didn't have any high expectation about this product sebab tak banyak baca reviews. I just nampak dekat Watsons, check ingredients macam best lepastu terus beli bila sale.

  

As long as takde alcohol and fragrance, I'm okay. The UV filters pun okay for me. Ada dua je UV filters and sama macam Canmake Mermaid Gel UV (which I love jugak). Review: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/04/canmake-mermaid-skin-gel-uv-review.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/04/canmake-mermaid-skin-gel-uv-review.html) So yeah, it doesn't breaks me out, buat I rasa pedih ke semua takde, rasa selesa je. No irritation what so ever.

  

It is also quite big, 110ml compared to other sunscreens yang I ada so boleh tahan lama. Cuma I tak suka packaging dia sebab senang nak hilang. Like I mentioned above, I love the texture and how it feels on my skin. It is also affordable and easy to get!

  
  

  

  

**Recommended for?**

All skin type!

  

**Who will not love this?**

Very oily skin since I rasa it will make you feel a bit greasy tapi kena try lah dulu sebab ada je yang oily skin and dia rasa best

  

**Will I repurchase?**

YES OF COURSE!!
