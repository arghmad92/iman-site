## What is in my beauty bag for going out?

  

###  Skincare stuffs in my beauty bag

  

[![](https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-sTsGvU6QdXc/WaeCdtEhbpI/AAAAAAAAEjc/Int6cr3vwtU482yiqaxGUdmSpLVg042fQCLcBGAs/s640/Photo%2B31-08-2017%252C%2B11%2B11%2B18%2BAM.jpg)

  

 **1. Micellar water** - to remove makeups

My all time favourite is Garnier Micellar Cleansing Water. Works to remove minimal makeups, non-waterproof mascara and sunscreen.

  

**2\. Sunscreen** - sebab sunscreen kena re-apply every 2-3 hours tau.

My fav is Cosrx Aloe Soothing Sun Cream. You can read the review here, [COSRX ALOE SOOTHING SUN CREAM REVIEW](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/08/cosrx-aloe-soothing-sun-cream-review.html)

  

Also read: [SUNBLOCK & SUNCREEN 101](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/08/sunblock-suncreen-101.html)

  

**3\. Face mist** \- to freshen up my face. Yang ni pakai kalau rasa muka kering. Spray je, terus rasa segar sikit. I'm currently using the Laneige Water Bank Mineral Skin Mist

  

You can also read: [DAILY SKINCARE ROUTINE (SOLAT VERSION)](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/08/daily-skincare-routine-solat-version.html)

  

  

### Makeup stuffs

  

[![](https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-7Y7kWgeBZ6k/WaeCdupxpSI/AAAAAAAAEjg/1dSbBERZ0yocFWjXwFgjEZfhRqpYRUjZQCLcBGAs/s640/Photo%2B31-08-2017%252C%2B11%2B11%2B48%2BAM.jpg)

  

**1\. Compact powder**

I rarely use this one, sometimes I only use it on my undereyes as a concealer. 

  

**2\. Highlighter + blusher + bronzer**

I will always love the Wet N Wild Illuminating Palette. You can read the review here, [WET N WILD MEGAGLOW ILLUMINATING PALETTE REVIEW](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/08/wet-n-wild-megaglow-illuminating.html)

  

**3\. Lipbalm**

I'm using Sierra Bees. Takde minty feeling and takde warna.

  

**4\. Lipsticks**

My current fav lipsticks are Tarte Sunkissed (review here, [\[REVIEW\] TARTE COSMETICS RAINFOREST OF THE SEA COLOR SPLASH LIPSTICK](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/04/review-tarte-cosmetics-rainforest-of.html)) and Maybelline Touch of Nude.
