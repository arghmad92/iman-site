The ingredients that concerns me the most are methylisothiazolinone and fragrance. Methylisothiazolinone is a preservative tapi only recommended to use on rinse-off product macam cleanser on shampoo je. Kalau leave-on, dia sensitizing, even with low %. So macam, risau sikit bila apply kat kulit and tak bilas. Plus, ada fragrance, which is better to be avoid if you have sensitive skin.

  

  
  

  

Other ingredients nampak macam okay, some bunch of extracts. Yeast, bilberry, sugar cane (glycolic acid - AHA derived from sugar cane but don't have the same beneficial effect), ascorbyl palmitate is a stable vitamin C form (brightening effect).

**Packaging**

Transparent clear bottle with flap top lid which is my fav kind of packaging. The label is in yellow tapi susah sikit nak baca sebab dia print out atas bottle. Maybe better print atas white label would be better kot? Sebab susah nak baca apa yang dia tulis.

  

**Scent**

Citrus-floral scent but it is not over-powering. 

  

**How I use**

Since I dah ada banyak product for my face, I use this on my body as a serum. So yes, body pun kena cleanse, exfoliate, use serum, lotion, moisturizer, butter and oils :P

  

  

  
  

  

**My thoughts:**

The texture feels quite thick tapi bila spread it out, rasa very thin. I'm impressed. It also absorbs beautifully and buat kulit rasa soft and supple. Tapi lama lama rasa macam gatal gatal sikit tapi takde lah sampai jadi merah.

  

It claims to moisturize and nourish the skin, which I totally agree! In terms of radiance, I didn't notice much difference. Maybe with consistent use, okay kot.

  

Ada a few serums lagi from Savee which are Anti-Wrinkles, H20, Vitamin C and Blue serum.
