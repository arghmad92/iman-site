**What it claims?**  
The KLAIRS Freshly Juiced Vitamin E Mask is incorporated with vitamin E, which is well known as an excellent antioxidant. Vitamin E is the ultimate skin care booster that not only delivers an excellent antioxidant power, but also has various effects in benefiting dry, sensitive, and aging skin.  
  
The KLAIRS Freshly Juiced Vitamin E Mask is most effective when used with **vitamin C**. The two vitamins enhance the effectiveness of each other through the antioxidant network and protect the skin from active oxygen, preventing aging. In addition, vitamin E prevents vitamin C from being oxidized before being absorbed by the skin, intensifying the skin-improving effects of vitamin C.

**Ingredient analysis**

It does not contains alcohol or fragrance which is a good thing, yay! Some great ingredients are Glycerin (for hydration), Tocopheryl Acetate (Vitamin E, tapi some poeple with acne sometimes tak sesuai, tapi bukan semua, depends on how your skin react), Niacinamide (my fav ingredient for brightening), Ceramide (great for our skin barrier and hydration), Arginine (hydration and wrinkles), Centella Asiatica Extract (for redness and to soothe skin), Adenosine ( anti-inflammatory, helps repair barrier and protection)... Oh sooo many great ingredients...

  

However, it contains essential oils so if your skin is suuuuuper sensitive with essential oils, this might not be good for you.

As you can see in the picture above, nampak macam tak pernah guna kan? Sebab dia jadi rata balik, even dah scoop banyak kali!

**My experience with Klairs Freshly Juiced Vitamin E Mask**

  

My first impression with this mask is I really love the scent. Upon application, it is quite hard to spread the product evenly sebab dia quite thick and melekit. Kena ambil sikit je. I selalu terambil banyak pastu membazir.... A little goes along the way.

  

And, it's hard to absorb. Lamaaaa gilaaaa nak tunggu dia absorb and lamaaa gila rasa melekit. If you don't sleep in the aircond or tidur berpeluh, this might not be good for you.... Unless you apply it reaaaally thin, like sikit sangaaat, maybe okay.

  

However, I like the stickiness on my dry skin. Bila bangun pagi pun rasa soft and supple je kulit. In terms of brightening, I do notice some minor improvement je.

  

I didn't experienced any irritation, pedih, gatal gatal, tak ada. Semua okay je :)

**Overall**

It's a good sleeping mask with a lot of great ingredients!

  

**Who will like this?**

People with dry-normal skin and dull complexion

  

**Who will not like this?**

People with oily or really oily skin, suuuper sensitive to essential oils, or react negatively to the ingredients.
