There are only three shades available in Malaysia which are Light Ivory, Light Beige and Medium Tawny. Again, the stocks are very limited at SASA. Yang ni pun tengok lah, semua shades dah habis. Nasib baik I sempat beli.  
  
  
**My thoughts on Wet n Wild PhotoFocus Concealer**  
Just like the foundation, I straight away went to SASA to get this concealer! Didn't read the reviews sangat, terus je beli.  
  
Also read: [WET N WILD PHOTOFOCUS FOUNDATION REVIEW](https://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/09/wet-n-wild-photofocus-foundation-review.html)  
  
The normal price is RM24.90 tapi ada 20% discount so I get it for only RM19++. So affordable, kan? I bought the lightest shade, Light Ivory.  
  
I love the packaging, in a transparent glass tube with a doe-foot applicator. The size is quite small tapi I think the product inside tu banyak la jugak. It's quite creamy and easy to blend, tapi kena blend cepat sikit lah. Kalau tak, dia dah kering and susah nak blend.

**What I love about Wet n Wild PhotoFocus Concealer?**  
\- It is lightweight, I dont feel cakey at all bila pakai this concealer  
\- Great coverage. Dark circles semua almost hilang. Tak nampak sangat.  
\- Easy to blend! Pakai jari pun no problem. Tapi kena cepat.  
\- No smell at all. No fragrance.  
\- Really affordable  
\- The packaging. Senang nak bawa travel  
\- The shade really matches my skin tone.

This is me, wearing the Wet n Wild PhotoFocus Concealer under my eyes, with no foundation/bb cream/any base. Boleh la hide sikit dark circles tu.

  

**What I don't like about Wet n Wild PhotoFocus Concealer?**

\-Creases easily. Kalau tak set, memang concealer tu semua settles into my fine lines. So kena set pakai powder, tapi kalau set pakai powder, rasa kering sangat pulak.

  

Overall, I like this product for daily use kalau nak pakai kejap je. Kalau pakai lama lama, dia memang crease, nampak habis berkedut and bercapuk, which is tak la teruk sangat, tapi I don't feel comfortable. Kalau kejap kejap, best! I love it. Blends easily and nampak flawless!

  

**Recommended?**

Yes.

  

Performance: 4/5 

Quality: 4.5/5

Value: 5/5

  

**Overall**: **4.5/5**
