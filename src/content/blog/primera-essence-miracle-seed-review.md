Lotus seed extract - Contains high levels of amino acids and linoleic acid for healthy, moisturized skin   

Niacinamide - Brightens and clarifies the skin   

Adenosine - Energizes the skin to reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles   

Hyaluronic acid - A humectant that attracts moisture to the skin to keep it supple

**My thoughts**

  

Kalau I dah habiskan, maksudnya I suka lah kan? 150ml and I finished it in 3 months. I use this right after cleansing, for morning and night routine. Setiap kali guna, I buat 3 layers sebab nak rasa dia punya hydrating effect tu lagi umph gitu. Padan la cepat sangat habis, Lepastu continue dengan toner, serum, moisturiozer or whatever lah yang I guna. Kiranya this is like the first treatment essence la, pakai sebelum benda lain.

  

The ingredients list is short, with 14 ingredients. There are no alcohol, no fragrance, no irritating ingredients. Key ingredient dia **lotus seed extract** which is one of the best ingredient for soothing and anti-oxidant. Ada 93.1% tu. So if you have redness, kulit macam sensitive pedih pedih gitu, this essence is really good for you.

It also contains niacinamide, adenosine and hyaluronic acid. Semua ni good ingredients for your skin. Tapi ada juga orang yang tak sesuai with these ingredients tau. Check semula kulit masing masing.

  

Botton line, this is a good essence for my skin, I can see my skin is well hydrated, less redness and rasa soothe sangat lepas guna. Tapi kalau nak repurchase, I fikir banyak kali juga sebab the price is quite expensive, around RM160++ rasanya.
