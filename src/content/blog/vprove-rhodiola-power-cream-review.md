In terms of the packaging, like I mentioned before, I really LOVE this kind of packaging since this cream is rich with anti-oxidant. It's really useless to have a cream or moisturizer that is rich with anti-oxidant but it's in a jar packaging since the anti-oxidant will be less effective if it's being exposed to air.

  

It has a really strong scent which some people don't mind. The texture is creamy but glides smoothly when applied. It's not greasy at all and absorbs quite fast, leaving a matte finish. Personally, 

I think it's really suitable for people with combination and normal skin. For dry skin, I think this cream is not really enough. It's great as a day cream and makes your skin smooth and really good as a makeup base.

  

In terms of the ingredients, I love the Rhodiola extract as an anti-oxidant, niacinamide, Madecassoside, NMF etc which is really good for the skin.

  

I didnt experience any irritation, breakouts, redness etc. It's good. It makes my skin feels soft, however if you have dry skin as me, you need to add with other moisturizing cream.
