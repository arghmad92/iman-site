**My thoughts on Cuura Invisible Protector**

This is a hybrid sunscreen since it contains both physical and chemical filters. The filters are the same used in Yadah Oh My Suncreen. Certain people are sensitive with chemical filters and they would like to avoid them so this is your personal choice lah either you want to use it or not.

  

Review: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2019/06/yadah-oh-my-sunscreen-review.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2019/06/yadah-oh-my-sunscreen-review.html)

  

Also read about mineral / physical filters and why people prefer them: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2019/08/mineral-physical-sunscreen.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2019/08/mineral-physical-sunscreen.html)

  

It has SPF50 which is enough kalau duduk Malaysia. However I am bit confused with the PA rating. Dekat kotak tulis PA++, dekat the bottle tulis PA+++ and dekat website tulis PA++++.

  

I love the texture and it's really suitable for my dry skin. There is no irritating ingredients such as alcohol, fragrance, essential oils. It's a bit hard to find local products yang tak ada semua ni, and I'm glad Cuura tak letak semua benda benda ni in this sunscreen. Kalau ada, confirm I tak akan beli sendiri dah haha. There's no pilling, tak ada keluar macam habuk habuk tu kalau reapply and it's good to use underneath makeup too. Tak buat makeup bercapuk or pelik.

  

As for it's claim, "moisturizing, light texture, non- greasy, effective light coverage, and does not clog your pores", I would agree! It's non-greasy for me but if you have really oily skin, it might be a bit greasy for you. And this product is not fungal acne safe.

  

This sunscreen is waterproof so you need to remove it properly kalau nak solat. Using micellar water should be enough to remove it.

  

  

**Pros**

No irritating ingredients (no alcohol, no fragrance, no essential oils)

No white cast

Cute design (sorry I bias kalau pink :P)

  

**Cons**

Size is a bit small, 30ml kejap sangat boleh guna

The price is quite expensive compared to other sunscreen on the market

Not fungal acne safe
