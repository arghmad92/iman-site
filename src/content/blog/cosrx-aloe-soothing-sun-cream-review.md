Cosrx Aloe Soothing Sun Cream Review

  
**What it claims?**  
Aloe Soothing Sun Cream is not just the normal sun cream. It not only completely blocks the UVA and UVB, but also moisturizes your dry skin. The combination of scientifically proven sun protection and natural ingredients and provide the skin protection layer. Even when you are under the strongest sun light, you have nothing to worry about. Hydrate your skin with the most powerful moisturizing ingredient aloe while protect from the daily sun light.  
  

**How to apply?**

The last step for skincare. Maksudnya lepas cleanser, toner, moisturizer, baru lah apply sunscreen ni. Lepastu continue makeup pulak (primer, foundation etc)

  

**The packaging:**

The product comes in a 50 ml plastic squeezable tube with a black cap. Senang nak picit and guna tapi tak nampak dia banyak mana. Nanti tahu tahu je, dah habis...

The colour of this is white. It feels like a moisturizer and very lightweight.

**Ingredients**:

Water, Ethylhexyl Methoxycinnamate, Glycerin, Propylene Glycol, Cyclopentasiloxane, Bis-Ethylhexyloxyphenol Methoxyphenyl Triazine, Phenylbenzimidazole Sulfonic Acid, Dicaprylyl Carbonate, Isoamyl P-Methoxycinnamate, Potassium Cetyl Phosphate, Alcohol,Dimethicone, Glyceryl Stearate, Butylene Glycol, Titanium Dioxide, C14-22 Alcohols, Polymethyl Methacrylate, Cetearyl Alcohol, Aloe Arborescens Leaf Extract, Dipotassium Glycyrrhizate, Tocopheryl Acetate, Peg-100 Stearate, Silica, C12-20 Alkyl Glucoside, Dimethicone/Vinyl Dimethicone Crosspolymer, Aluminum Hydroxide, Stearic Acid, Caprylyl Glycol, Acrylates/C10-30 Alkyl Acrylate Crosspolymer, Carbomer, Xanthan Gum, Triethanolamine Sodium Hydroxide, Fragrance, Phenoxyethanol, Disodium Edta

  

**How I feel:**

I have sensitive skin I pretty much want to avoid anything yang ada alcohol or fragrance. However, this product do contain both alcohol and fragrance. Tapi alcohol bukan top 6 ingredient. For me, bila apply, tak ada rasa pedih ke apa ke, no tingling feeling, no irritation. Maybe sebab dah apply semua moisturizer so tak ada lah the ingredients (alcohol, fragrance) yang kena direct dekat kulit. Kalau dalam toner ke, maybe pedih.  
  
It is really moisturizing, tapi tak thick. Very lightweight and takde white cast, maksudnya kulit tak jadi warna putih macam lepas pakai sunblock tebal tu. And tak greasy.  
  
**Overall**  
Yes, I love this and I use this everyday! Very suitable for dry - normal skin. Kalau oily skin, maybe a bit greasy/melekit sebab yang ni, dia moisturizing.  
  

**Recommended?**

Yes! But patch test first if you have sensitive skin.
