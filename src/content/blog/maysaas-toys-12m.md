### 1\. Bead maze

[!

](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEidpq5WJaYgCAhy6Fbfk3PdUbCQ2nwDX5UgaH7NDy07AAcJ_aokt4gr3dq57nrSKlS3vHhj9cCa-YaS-1zlr37VJWJbv8jYKeQ-05eui6Y6HgG-R5G6erFWuJmUGIarkdxVeJrw7Ac2N4wF/s1600/Photo+10-07-2018%252C+2+05+42+PM.jpg)  

  

I bought this sebab benda ni selalu ada dekat tempat mainan budak budak and also hospitals kalau tengah menunggu tu and Maysaa macam berminat nak main. 

  

[!

](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEh-5YnuFVl42timG-o6NXlr97A87ywkzAkIsAclGEWu0sE68PTzeGz3HuuUhwfAoZL_zimVJenLjhMgzOzM-yETotjY8LiclC7uYCwaUctCBIJkLrKILSpxqAJj-kbRl1LqGlwDpt6KFrm-/s1600/Photo+10-07-2018%252C+12+15+38+PM.jpg)

  

Yang ni boleh search je dekat Shopee "bead maze" nanti banyak yang keluar. I bought this for around RM40+. Seronok tengok Maysaa focus satu satu gerakkan beads tu.

  

### 2\. Ring stacker

  

[!

](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhMiL_6Vmdgz2vG-SOoMsM2hOU0gMFqq371aA1VuYvGUn22jTUcJdtvS65MYYAyJ6lmEwYMXfGZDsXt8hyphenhyphenvJlSnwmo6wpYwe7oEDcTosscUhWTiVEksCYT32PNIOt4AAaXPpRtA2EhfONFc/s1600/Photo+10-07-2018%252C+2+04+45+PM.jpg)

  

Yang ni plastic je, gigit sikit je dah penyet haha. I bought drom Mr DIY for around RM18+. Enough untuk practice coordination skill, masukkan ring tu satu satu walaupun dia tak reti lagi mana satu yang first haha.

  

### 3\. Shape sorter

  

[!

](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhgN1ekjq_nHC8dG8-Pf-JeJUjR5XV6L3UrsK8rQWDdb3dUelsqQf6qdkkuXxBtz8kuLSzeyox-rTLhoqxiDFEI1QZf5QXFwLNgUuo6mcH_vUUbFOyIxYMsLnx3Yl9MApPV4b2JL3Ra0K72/s1600/Photo+10-07-2018%252C+2+07+52+PM.jpg)
