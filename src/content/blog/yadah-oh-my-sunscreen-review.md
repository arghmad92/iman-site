Yadah Oh My Sunscreen Review

  

  

I only bought the 20ml to try. Kalau ikut website Watsons, the price is RM65 for 50ml kalau takde sale. For me, the price is a bit on a high side.

**Packaging**: It comes in a 20ml opaque grey tube and a black screwable cap. I like the nozzle size, not too big and not too small, just nice to dispense the product. All the details and ingredients are printed clearly on the box.

  

**Scent**: Since it contains Bergamot Oil, it smells herbal-ish. If your skin is sensitive with essential oils, you might need to avoid this.

  

  

**Texture**: It has a white creamy texture but it blends nicely into the skin without any whitecast or any sticky feeling. The finishing is almost satin-matte but it's not drying.

**My thoughts Yadah Oh My Sunscreen** 

It has SPF35 which is enough kalau duduk Malaysia tapi I lagi suka kalau SPF50 to get enough protection. This is a hybrid sunscreen, it has a physical filter (Titanium Dioxide) and chemical filters Octinoxate, Octocrylene and Avobenzone. 

  

  

  

  

Some of the sunscreen that I've tried yang ada avobenzone, such as Supergoop Unseen Sunscreen, Bio-essence Cooling Sunscreen, Neutrogena Ultra Sheer Sunscreen and Banana Boat Sport buat kulit I jadi pedih and gatal gatal. Maybe because the % of avobenzone is quite high. However, my skin is okay with this sunscreen. Tak ada rasa pedih pedih ataupun gatal gatal langsung. 

  

I love the texture, it absorbs nicely into the skin. Normally I suka my sunscreen a bit sticky but this one macam just nice. If you have dry or combination skin, you might love this! There is no alcohol and fragrance too! However, this sunscreen is not fungal acne friendly.

  

**Recommended**? Yes! If you have extra money, you can try this.
