### 5\. YangPang Chicken 

  
[!

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhiTNlEAytj2Y0bAxO11xfWDRHKFs3CgOAMtKwZIGK1SapkHjLHs3hRSFYeBALjXsoLA_17FjcvXwpAbYhO8CAyumQBHgjfOW2c7Nt4QQkaBLizo0zSu98DciUBRPA-Jv_fF03nyx5BTRkA/s1600/092EFF33-2052-42E0-A559-862E2191BF16.JPG)

#### MAKANAN HALAL DI INCHEON INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT 

  
Ada Nimat Halal Korean Cuisine dekat dengan Gate 118, area food court. Kena cari Gate 118. Masa haritu kitorang Gate 124 so dekat je lah nak pergi makan. I bought Bulgogi Kimbap for 5500KRW.
