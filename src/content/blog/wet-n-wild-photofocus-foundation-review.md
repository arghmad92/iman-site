**Packaging**  
This product comes in a 30mL glass bottle. Tapi tak pecah pun kalau jatuh, kot? The black pastic lid tu ada a paddle/spatula attached to apply the foundation. I prefer pump becuase it's easier to dispense the product.  Tak messy. Tapi yang ni pun, okay lah. Cuma I jenis clumsy orang nya, takut tertumpah je bila guna -.-  
  
**Texture/coverage/finish**  
It is quite runny, tak thick sangat. Tapi bila dah blend, dia medium coverage, and buildable. Great coverage. For me, dia tak matte finish. In the middle of dewy-matte. Satin-ish or semi-matte kot.  
  
**Scent**  
Chemical-scent, like a paint, tapi kejap je. Bila dah pakai, tak bau sangat.

**My review on Wet n Wild PhotoFocus Foundation**   
My first impression on this foundation, is the scent. Kuat sangat bau chemical dia. Tapi kejap je. I applied it and intsantly it clings on my dry patches. Since I have dry skin, memang segala dryness tu nampak. I don't like it. Especially the area tepi hidung, really dry. I set it with my NYX Dewy Setting Spray. After awhile, it is getting better. For my first experience, it was ok, except, a bit drying.  
  
So, I tried it again, but this time, I make sure I moisturized my skin carefully, biar muka dah lembab. Then, I applied it with a damp sponge. I sprayed my face mist on the sponge and blend the foundation. After that, I set it with powder and my NYX Dewy Setting Spray. The result? No more dry patches and the foundation looks better on my skin.

  

[!

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEh6_7tVjIyKWUBSJnWM4xeFEz0Euxg3RSUwFwMNhHugQUo3jRpQa2Nv_JdTaqGhu_hnAgo1OcuaAu6jSLZke-DHglEfxzEp17IZc8xy2o_xXmZjrodb3T2fFuJlJBSPHCLvErMapCQpuvA/s1600/Photo+05-09-2017%252C+10+25+10+PM.jpg)  

  
After jalan jalan, kena panas sikit, berpeluh, after 8 hours, it is still ok. It is not full coverage. Medium but blendable. Light blemishes, semua boleh cover. Even my dark circles are less noticable. I love it. Can use it for attending events and weddings! (Since I malas nak pakai foundation kalau keluar biasa biasa)  
  
My tips on applying foundation for dry skin:  
1\. Make sure to moisturize your skin, and use a hydrating primer  
2\. Blend the foundation with a damp sponge. Use any face mist.  
3\. Set it with a dewy setting spray.  
  
For oily skin yang nak this foundation nampak matte:  
1\. Use mattifying primer but you still need to moisturize  
2\. Set it with setting powder and setting spray.  
  
Overall, I think this foundation is suitable for all skin type, tapi kena pandai adjust lah supaya sesuai dengan kulit masing masing.
