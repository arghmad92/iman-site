**Ingredients**

Glycerin, Water, Sodium Cocoyl Glycinate, Cocamidopropyl Betaine, Decyl Glucoside, Citric Acid, Xanthan Gum, Butylene Glycol, Camellia Sinensis (Black Tea) Leaf Extract, Ethylhexylglycerin, Cycnoches Cooperi (Black Orchid) Extract, Phenoxyethanol, Chlorphenesin, Potassium Sorbate, Sodium Benzoate

  

  

**What it claims?**

_This gentle, fragrance free Amino Acid cream cleanser deeply cleanse and remove impurities. Formulated with the precious blend of Black Tea & Black Orchid extracts helps to detox the skin, while keeping skin healthy and protected from premature aging._

I don't really believe in skin detoxing ni tapi takpelah, kita buat buat tak nampak je lah claim yang tu haha.

I also tested the pH value for this cleanser, the pH value is around pH5-6 which is good for me since I really prefer a low pH cleanser! You can also read why I prefer a low pH cleanser: [http://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2017/08/skincare-101-low-ph-cleanser.html](http://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2017/08/skincare-101-low-ph-cleanser.html) 

  

It comes in a 150ml plastic tube with white flap cap. All the details are printed clearly on the packaging. 

  

I ada share on [How to choose a cleanser?](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/07/choose-cleanser.html) and based on my personal experience, I really like this cleanser. It has a creamy texture but takdelah banyak sangat buih and my skin feels smooth and nice je bila cuci muka. It doesn't have any fragrance and I rasa bau dia macam bau plastic gitu. Tapi baguslah takde fragrance sebab I memang prefer macam tu. Sometimes product yang banyak sangat fragrance and bau wangi sangat buat kulit I jadi pedih. So this product memang tak buat rasa pedih and tak ada rasa ketat ataupun kering lepas guna.

  

It has no alcohol, no sulphates, no paraben, no essential oils, no irritating ingredients and fungal acne safe. Best!

  

  

Overall, I really enjoyed this product and I'll repurchase it. Yay!
