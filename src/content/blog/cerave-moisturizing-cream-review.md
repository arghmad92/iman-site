**Texture**. The texture is quite thick. It's good for dry skin or people yang duduk tempat yang tak berpeluh macam dalam aircond. Pakai nipis nipis pun rasa jugak thickness dia. 

  

**Scent**. No noticeable scent that I noticed. Cuma bau chemical gitu, takde fragrance kan.

  

**My experience**

  

I use this as a normal moisturizer, pagi and malam. My normal routine:

1\. Cleanse

2\. Tone

3\. Moisturize - **Cerave Moisturizing Cream**

4\. Sunscreen (Kalau siang)

  

It doesn't makes my skin feels greasy and melekit kalau pakai nipis nipis waktu siang. No irritation, and makes my skin feels good. Kalau terlebih pakai, maybe rasa melekit sikit.

  

However, bila I pakai this cream without my hydrating toner, I still rasa kulit I tight sikit. Kalau ada hydrating toner, baru perfect and rasa lengkap. Maybe it's just me. (I alternate Klairs toner and Etude House Soon Jung)

  

The ingredients are good, contains glycerin, ceramides, and cholesterol that can improve your skin barrier. If your skin barrier rasa terganggu, this is a good moisturizer.

  

A good basic moisturizer if you have a dry/sensitive skin. Tapi dia tak ada lah antioxidant or any skin brightening properties so don't expect too high lah. 

  

I bough this from website iherb for RM18.80. Also read [Iherb haul](http://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/04/iherb-haul-malaysia.html). It's hard to get in Malaysia, dah lah kena tunggu lama. Hopefully cepat lah masuk Guardian / Watsons.  
  

  

  

**Recommended for?** 

People with dry or sensitive skin

  

**Who will not love this?**

Oilier skin type since this is quite thick, unless duduk dalam aircond.

  

**Repurchase?**

Maybe not since I don't really like the thick texture. I suka moisturizer yang absorbs nicely into my skin. Unless senang nak dapat dekat Malaysia, then maybe I beli bila desperate :)
