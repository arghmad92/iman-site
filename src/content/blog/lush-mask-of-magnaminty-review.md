**Ingredients**

I terlupa nak snap gambar ingredients lepastu dah malas nak tangkap gambar semula haha maafkan saya :P

  

So let me type the ingredient list.

  

Honey, Kaoline, Bentonite Gel, Talc, Glycerine, Ground Aduki Beans (Phaseolus Angularis), Evening Primrose Seeds (Oenothera biennis), Peppermint Oil (Mentha Piperita), Tagetes Oil (Tagetes Minuta), Vanilla Absolute (Vanilla Planifolia) \*Limonene Fragrance Chlorophyllin  

  

**What it claims?**

This tingly, minty face and body mask was formulated to be intensely cleansing but never harsh or abrasive. Kaolin clay and peppermint oil rid your pores of debris, giving you a delightfully tingly clean sensation. We add loads of ground aduki beans to exfoliate flaky or dry skin, vanilla absolute to calm redness and honey to soothe and moisturize.

**Texture**

A green creamy paste like texture with beads (the beans).

  

  

**My thoughts on LUSH Mask Of Magnaminty**

The moment I read the ingredient, I was very sceptical about the ingredients. The strong scent of peppermint really makes me scared and rasa pedih bila nak apply. Peppermint Oil is potentially irritating for certain sensitive skin.

  

Because I was scared to apply it on my face, I applied it on my body first. Ala ala patch test sebelum guna. Boy, it felt like my skin was burning. Pedih gilaaaaaaa. And I quickly basuh and it makes my skin red...

  

Later, after a few days, I tried it again. On. my body too. Takut nak apply dekat muka. And it felt a tingling sensation tapi takde lah pedih sampai merah kulit. And it felt good bila dia dah makin keras and gosok atas kulit as a scrub. My skin feels smoother and fresher.

  

Did I used it on my face? No thank you. I guna dekat area badan sahaja cause I don't wanna risk it. I don't like the tingling feeling.

  

In conclusion, guna dengan risiko sendiri :P
