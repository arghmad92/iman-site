Pak John Steamboat & BBQ Buffet IOI City Mall

  

  

  

### Apa yang ada dekat Pak John Steamboat & BBQ Buffet IOI City Mall ni?

Ada lebih 160 dishes yang boleh dimakan, berkonsepkan buffet. 

  

  
  

  

### Harga Pak John Steamboat & BBQ Buffet

  

Harga weekday

**Adult**: Lunch RM32.90+, Dinner 42.90+

**Kids**: Lunch RM17.95+, Dinner RM22.95+

  

\*Lunch: 11am-5pm

\*Dinner: 5pm-10pm

  

Harga weekend

**Adult**: RM42.90+

**Kids**: RM22.95+

  

Kids below 5 years old: EAT FOR FREE
