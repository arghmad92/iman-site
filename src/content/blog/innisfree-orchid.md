Innisfree Orchid Skin, Essence, Lotion and Cream

  

  
  

**What it claims?**

-   Powerful antioxidant that makes dry skin radiant and strengthens the skin’s defense system.
-    Enhances the resilience of the skin to correct visible wrinkles and clear the skin tone for radiant, healthy skin overall.

  

They also have the gel cream, eye cream (which some people says it can reduce dark circles, one of the best eye cream - but I haven't tried it yet), sleeping pack and intense cream but I don't have the sample size, so I can't review them.

  

First of all, what are the difference between skin, essence, lotion, cream? For me, yang paling senang nak bezakan, the texture.

  

  

  

[![](https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-ruTO0ScWTUA/Wbib1HqKD6I/AAAAAAAAEu8/E_bJwkm8gwwdFWhsHUC9DSpojZRd8M3SgCLcBGAs/s640/Screen%2BShot%2B2017-09-13%2Bat%2B10.44.55%2BAM.png)

](https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-ruTO0ScWTUA/Wbib1HqKD6I/AAAAAAAAEu8/E_bJwkm8gwwdFWhsHUC9DSpojZRd8M3SgCLcBGAs/s1600/Screen%2BShot%2B2017-09-13%2Bat%2B10.44.55%2BAM.png)

  

**Skin** is basically a toner, watery texture and senang mengalir, macam air. Korean people panggil "Skin". Kalau Japanese punya product, biasanya panggil "lotion", padahal kalau Western people, "lotion" tu macam lotion cream tu. #NowYouKnow

  

**Essence** is thicker than toner. Dia still colourless, tapi melekit sikit. Kalau toner, biasanya function dia untuk bersihkan kulit yang tak habis cuci masa cleansing and hydrating, essence ni pula untuk prepare kulit supaya kulit kita boleh absorb next product better. Essence ni sometimes dia ada extra ingredient than toner either untuk brightening, moisturizing, anti-aging.

  

**Lotion and cream** \- both basically untuk moisturizing, tapi cream lagi thick. Beza dari segi texture dia.

  

Gel cream: For oily/combination (more to oily) skin

Lotion: For combination skin

Cream: For combination (more to dry)/dry skin

Intense cream: For very dry skin
