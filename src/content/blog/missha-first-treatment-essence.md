### **Missha The First Treatment Essence Intensive Moist Ingredient**

**Saccharomyces Ferment Filtrate**, **Bifida Ferment Lysate**, Water, Diethoxyethyl Succinate, **Niacinamide**, Hordeum Distichon (Barley) Extract, Phenoxyethanol, Dipropylene Glycol, Dimethicone, **Glycerin**, Polyquaternium-51, 1,2-Hexanediol, **Adenosine**, Lecithin, Potassium Sorbate, Cassia Alata Leaf Extract, Lactic Acid, Acetic Acid

  

### **Ingredient Analysis**

  

**Saccharomyces Ferment Filtrate** - It's an antioxidant, anti-inflammatory that can hydrates, fights signs of aging, brightens skin

  

**Bifida Ferment Lysate** - Ingredient ni tak banyak sangat research, tapi basically an antioxidants, hydrating and soothing properties

  

**Niacinamide** - One of my favourite ingredient. Visibly improve the appearance of enlarged pores, uneven skin tone, fine lines, dullness, and a weakened skin surface. For me, niacinamide = glowing skin!

  

**Glycerin** - skin-replenishing and restoring ingredient. Helps maintain a healthy look and feel, defending against dryness.

  

**Adenosine** - good soothing and skin-restoring agent. Has proven anti-wrinkle benefits due to its ability to energize skin’s surface so it looks smoother and younger.

  

  

OMG banyak gila ingredient yang best dekat dalam ni, tak ada ingredient yang pelik pelik, semua best and banyak benefit.

### **How to use Missha The First Treatment Essence Intensive Moist**

Since it's a first treatment essence, I use this essence right after cleanser.

  

**Low pH cleanser - Missha First Treatment Essence - Toner - Essence**

  

This is not the normal essence yang pakai after toner sebab dia veryyyy thin. Memang as a first treatment, lepastu baru pakai toner and other essences, kalau ada.

  

  

### **My experience with** **Missha The First Treatment Essence Intensive Moist**

I've been using this essence for 12 weeks already. And the essence dah tinggal separuh....  
  
I love this essence and I can't live without this. Instantly holy grail. Wajib pakai, kalau tak pakai, rasa hidup ini tak lengkap.  
  
The price is quite high, kena kumpul duit kalau nak beli lepastu guna berjimat sebabb sayang sangat...  
  
Basically, memang kulit rasa hydrated, very supple, no more dry patches, irritations semua tak ada. Kulit rasa lembut and lembap. It absorbs quickly, tak rasa melekit langsung.  
  
Hydrating? Yes! Glowing? Yes! My pigmentations also reduced, slightly. But this is not my main concern. Overall, I am so in love. Dah la ingredient semua best.
