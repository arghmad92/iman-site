**First of all, what is oil cleanser actually?**

Basically, we use oil, to clean our face. Any kind of oil pun tak apa, asalkan oil tu tak sumbatkan pores.  
  
**Why do we need oil cleanser?**  
Sebab, oil cleanser boleh remove makeups, SPF, oils + minyak semua dekat muka kita. And, boleh bersihkan sampai ke dalam pores kita, bagi bersih.

**The packaging:**

The product comes in a 200ml transparent plastic bottle with a pump. I love the pump because it's easy to dispense the oil, takde lah berminyak botol tu nanti. I also comes with a lock, for travelling nanti just lock the pump so tak tumpah, however, I've lost the lock hahaha.  
  

**The colour/consistency/texture:**

It's colourless, like the normal oil. Consistency like oil, quite thick.

  

**The scent:**

The product claim that it is fragrance-free, hence tak ada bau langsung tapi tak bau chemical yang kuat, just like normal oil kot. This product has no scent at all.

**Ingredients analysis**  
There are four types of oil, Olive Oil, Macadamia Oil, Jojoba Oil & Roseship Oil. All of these oils are having the comedogenic rate of 1-2, which means tak sumbatkan pori. Kalau rating tinggi 4-5 tu, kena elakkan sebab dia mungkin boleh sumbatkan pores and mula lah jerawat tumbh, blackhead, whitehead etc etc.  
  
It also contains Super Hyaluronic Acid! Which means, dia boleh pegang (lock) moisture dekat kulit kita, twice lagi effective. So kulit kita akan lebih moisturized, tak mengeringkan kulit.

**How to use?**

Make sure tangan kering and muka kering tau, maksudnya tak perlu basahkan dengan air dulu. Kena kering tak ada air. Just pump 1-2 pump of oil cleanser ni, massage dekat muka. Lepastu emulsify it with water. Tambah air sikit sikit bagi dia macam milky sikit bagi dia dissolve semua makeups, oil, dirts. Then, baru bilas pakai air betul betul.

**How I feel:**

Since this is my first oil cleanser, before this pakai makeup remover biasa. Tuang atas kapas, lap muka, settle. But this oil cleanser, mula mula rasa pelik sebab dia kena tangan and muka kering, lepas tu massage je??? Ok I noob.  
  
Mula mula rasa betul ke ni, melekit nya? Tapi best sangat sebab semua makeups hilang. And jimat cotton pad sebab tak perlu guna pun, I letak oil cleanser ni dalam toilet je. Bila nak cuci makeup, just pump the oil cleanser, massage, bilas, done. So easy. Dah la it reaaalllyyyyy works. Nak remove waterproof mascara, semua la, senang.  
  
It is very light, not greasy, doesn't irritates my skin. Ingredients dia semua best. I love love love this product. However, sometimes bila I over massaga bahagian mata, nanti pandangan jadi kabur. Like cloudy gitu. I guess it's a normal thing kalau pakai oil cleanser kot.  
  
Lepas pakai, dia tak ada rasa melekit, greasy feeling macam baru lepas simbah minyak. Tak ada. Rasa bersih je. And it's ph balanced, tak mengeringkan kulit, and memang sesuai for all skin type.  
  
**Pros**  
Really works, doesn't irritates my skin, my skin feels better!  
  
**Cons**  
Quite expensive, unless you buy it during sale. Tunggu sale je lah, baru beli. Like me. Kalau nak bayar full price macam kena fikir banyak kali haha.  
  
  

**Recommended?**

YES. You really should try this oil cleanser!
