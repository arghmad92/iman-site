Purito Fermented Complex 94 Boosting Essence Review

  

  
  

**ABOUT THE BRAND**

PURITO is a combined word of "Purify" and "To” (comes from a Chinese character, 土 meaning “Soil”).  “Purify” means to purify their natural ingredients to create safe and clean products.  “To” means to go back to basics to strengthen and repair collapsed skin barriers.

  

I love a skincare brands that are 100% vegan, skin friendly and cruelty free. Based on their website, they use environmental-friendly recycled packaging. All the ingredients are EWG green level.  

  

**WHAT IT CLAIMS?**

1\. Thin liquid - type essence  
2\. Specialized for delivering moisture deep under the rough skin  
3\. Improves the condition of stressed skin to make skin clear and elastic  
4\. Raises the absorption and effectiveness of the skincare step

**SCENT**

There is no noticeable scent that I noticed. It has no fragrance in it so it is good if your skin is sensitive with fragrance. 

  

**TEXTURE**

It has a thin liquid texture, a thicker texture than a normal toner but lighter than a serum. I love the texture, it is not sticky and makes my skin feels really hydrated.

**HOW I USE THIS?**

I normally use this after I cleanse and tone my skin. After that, I will apply my moisturizer. So the step is: Cleanser - toner - Purito Fermented Complex 94 Boosting Essence - moisturizer - sunscreen (during the day).

  

I use this during my morning and night routine, twice per day using my palm. I enjoy applying 2-3 layers of this product for extra hydrating benefit :)  
  
On the box, they suggested to let the product absorb for 30-50 seconds and iterate it 4 times in total. For me, 2-3 layers is enough for my skin. 

  

**MY THOUGHTS ON THIS?**

I love the ingredient list with no alcohol and no fragrance. It contains probiotic ingredients (lactobacillus) which is good for hydrating and soothing the skin and also strengthen the skin barrier. It also contains 3% Niacinamide and Morus Alba Bark Extract that is a brightening ingredients.  
  
I enjoy using this as it really hydrates my skin and makes my skin feels soft and supple. I also notice a slight improvement in my skin tone. It absorbs nicely into the skin with no dries down with a slight tackiness that I love because my skin is dry (sometimes really dry).

  

Bottom line, this is a good hydrating essence to add in your skincare routine if you have dry or dehydrated skin.
