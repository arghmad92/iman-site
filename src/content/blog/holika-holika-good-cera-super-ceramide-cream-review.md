**My thoughts**

The main reason why I bought this product is because the name is "Super Ceramide Cream" and it claims to moisturize your skin for 72 hours. Too good to be true eh?  
  
So anyways, I have tried a few moisturizer with ceramides, you can read about em here: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/12/moisturizers-with-ceramide-that-i-have.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/12/moisturizers-with-ceramide-that-i-have.html)  
  
  

Basically ceramide is good to repair your skin barrier so I do prefer a moisturizer with ceramide to maintain my skin barrier.  
  

Read about skin barrier: [https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/08/skincare-101-skin-barrier.html](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/08/skincare-101-skin-barrier.html)  
  
I got this from Guardian, during the 40% sale. If I'm not mistaken, the price for this product is around RM100+ so bila sale, dapat lah around RM60+. Kalau tak sale, maybe I tak beli because this is considered quite an expensive moisturizer from drugstore. Nasib baik ada sale, boleh lah try.

  
I like how how this moisturizer absorbs into my skin. It's a cream moisturizer but it's not too thick, I ambil banyak and apply banyak dekat kulit pun, dia tak jadi macam too thick yang nampak warna putih putih kat kulit lepastu lambat nak absorb. Dia macam melts nicely into the skin lepastu rasa kulit memang moisturized. Sukaaaa! Tapi part 72 hours tu I rasa macam tak je...  
  
The citrus-ish scent does lingers, and if you have sensitive skin and can't tolerate essential oils, you might not like this. Kalau dia dalam tube and takde essential oil, I might consider to repurchase this again.  
  

  
  
**Pros**:  
Nice texture and absorbs nicely into my skin  
  
**Cons**:  
Contains essential oils  
Not fungal acne safe  
Jar packaging  
  
  

That's all, thanks for reading!
