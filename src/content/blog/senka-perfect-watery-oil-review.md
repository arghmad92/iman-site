**What is oil cleanser actually?**

Basically, we use oil, to clean our face. Any kind of oil pun tak apa, asalkan oil tu tak sumbatkan pores.  
  
  
  
  
**Why do we need oil cleanser?**  
Oil cleanser boleh remove makeups, sunscreens, minyak semua dekat muka kita. And, boleh bersihkan sampai ke dalam pores kita, bagi bersih.  
  

[![](https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-fWxTXer2Pi0/WsZKniwy49I/AAAAAAAAGO0/AH6m8-TscBcDrl36-vXRH-96YUsoXYaHgCLcBGAs/s640/Photo%2B05-04-2018%252C%2B2%2B40%2B06%2BAM.jpg)

  
  

**The packaging:**

The product comes in a 230ml blue transparent plastic bottle with a pump. I love pump packaging sebab it's easy to dispense the oil, takde lah berminyak botol tu nanti kalau nak ambil extra oil, and senang. Like always, I don't understand anything dekat packaging dia cause it's in Japan :(  
  

**The colour/consistency/texture:**

It's colourless, like the normal oil. 

  

**The scent:**

OMG it has a very strong fragance, macam shampoo, which I really hate :(  
  

[![](https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-5y5HKgzGt9o/WsZKnXrVw5I/AAAAAAAAGOs/68C3RRKF5g4AB9_p5jUM89vrZIqMxvQ1gCLcBGAs/s640/Photo%2B05-04-2018%252C%2B2%2B40%2B18%2BAM.jpg)

  

**Performance**

I bought this sebab saja nak try since it got quite good reviews. So, why not? Although it contains mineral oil, my skin okay je with mineral oil, so I okay je nak try. Ada certain skin yang tak sesuai dengan mineral oil, maybe boleh breakout. So it depends on your skin.

  

I don't like the scent. In fact, I hate it. Bau macam shampoo yang kuat gila, rasa macam sapu shampoo dekat muka!

  

It removes light makeup and sunscreen tapi tak berapa nak emulsify bila tambah air so I don't really enjoy it. Mata pun sometimes rasa cloudy so rasa tak best.

  

For me, it's a quite a good product tapi I je yang tak enjoy sangat. Depends on preference orang lah kot.

  

**Pros**: Removes light makeup and sunscreen, quite affordable, easy to get

  

**Cons**: The fragrance is very strong, hard to remove heavy makeup, contains mineral oil, might not be suitable for sensitive skin

  

  

  

**Who will like this?**

Anyone that don't have sensitive skin and suka product yang wangi gila

  

**Who will not like this?**

Sensitive skin

  

**Repurchase?**

Nope
