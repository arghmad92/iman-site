Wet N Wild Megaglow Illuminating Palette Review

  

**Packaging**

For the old packaging, it comes with 9g of products in a pan with screw lid top. I don't really like it. Leceh lah nak pulas pulas untuk bukak. Nope.

  

For the new packaging, they improves it. It comes with 10g of products in a pan with flip-open. Easier. Tak payah pulas pulas. Senang nak handle. The pan is quite bigger than the old packaging.

  

**Texture and pigmentation**

It has a powdery texture, not too chalky sampai berdebu tu. Quite buttery, tapi pigmentation biasa biasa je. Tak ada lah 'BAMM' sangat, even the highlighter. Which is a good thing, sebab I use this for everyday makeup je, tak perlu nak letops sangat highlighter apa semua.

**What I like about Wet N Wild Megaglow Illuminating Palette**

In comparison, strip 1, 2, 3 in both packaging is quite the same. However, in the new packaging, the 4th strip is lighter compared to the old version. But I like the bronze gold tone.

  

As a highlighter, I normally use my finger je, and apply on my cheekbone, bridge of my nose. I really like the first strip. Lepas spray my dewy setting spray, terus nampak muka glowing. A very nice, natural glow, macam tak pakai makeup tapi glowing. It's very sheer, barely visible as you can see at the swatches above tu. Yes, this is my secret to a natural glowing face :P

  

As a blusher, I normally combine strip 3 and 4, but it's quite dark for my skin tone. Blusher nampak beriya sangat, so kalau nak daily look, I'll combine strip 2 & 3.

  

Sometimes, I use the 4th strip as my quick eyeshadow and apply the 1st strip in the inner corner of my eyes untuk nampak lebih bermaya.

  

Basically, this palette is a must in my bag. Wajib. I don't really know why I like this palette so much. Maybe because it's cheap? The old packaging murah lah, yang baru punya ni for me, a bit expensive but still affordable. I like the pigmentation, the glow, the finishing.

  

**Recommended?**

YES, absolutely yes.

  

Performance: 5/5 - I really like it

Quality: 4/5 - Multipurpose product

Value: 4.5/5 - Price is quite affordable

  

**Overall**: **4.5/5**
