**What I like?**  
Out of all the products that I've tried, I am so interested to buy the Supple Preparation All Over Lotion and the Mid-day Blue Sun Lotion. Although I don't really like the white-cast thingy tu but the texture is very nice and not drying at all (I have dry skin tau!). I also heard people with oily skin really love the Supple Preparation Facial Toner!  
  
**Overall**  
The price is quite expensive, a bit on a higher side, which makes me fikir banyak kali kalau nak beli full size punya. But, it's good. (Kalau duit bukan masalah, I beli je) (Tapi I still want to try other products jugak)

  

  

**Will I recommend?**  
I recommend this for those who have sensitive skin and have tried everything else tapi semua macam tak jalan je, try la beli sample Klairs ni and try dulu ok ke tak. Kalau ok, baru beli yang full size.
