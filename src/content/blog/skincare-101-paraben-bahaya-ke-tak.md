## **What is paraben?**

[!

](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEirWI_0JWLzFgUqnPIpGmCG_MKKXoUn0HnkJZskOyca8cauWEaGVCwVaDpB9XoBVpliIwKF5JGDwEBHvvC2H14s4nuREoLyv1NU68A_uaZ84x6rpSMzkOQGmeGjWkw3qLKMan89bHjR5Noc/s1600/75JvXuWf.jpg)

So I made a poll on twitter. 39% daripada 9923 orang tahu paraben tu apa. 61% taktahu.

Paraben ialah salah satu bahan pengawet (preservative) supaya product tahan lama.

_"The main function of a preservative in cosmetic preparation is to **prevent the growth of microorganisms** during the use of a cosmetic product and to **protect the quality of the product** from possible damages such as bad odour or composition changes caused by the growth of microorganisms. Therefore, preservative is essential to **maintain the quality and safety** of a cosmetic preparation during the period of its use and shelf life."_

  

For more info, boleh baca dekat portal rasmi Kementerian Kesihatan Malaysia about paraben: [http://www.myhealth.gov.my/en/use-preservative-cosmetic-preparation/](http://www.myhealth.gov.my/en/use-preservative-cosmetic-preparation/)

  

  

  

## **Paraben bahaya ke tak?**

[!

](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjPV03lxiAQyRMkqtNbwcWulhW54EUn_USjZrwu265jVpb9LSojj035fI94Xm8P3Fnma8X7epZHI551NbcinlxQi6epuaqO1RS47uW8SjzYCRgaVElDI7w0jMjxFd53IwXdPiHs3tFVeGjL/s1600/wmltU2cs.jpg)

  

55% daripada 7100 cakap paraben tu bahaya. SO, bahaya ke tak sebenarnya?

  

Based on Kementerian kesihatan Malaysia, ada beberapa jenis preservatives yang dibenarkan guna (List of preservatives allowed for use in cosmetic products) termasuklah:

1\. Butylparaben

2\. Propyl paraben

3\. Methyl paraben

\*With specific maximum authorized concentration, limitations and requirements. Contohnya untuk butyl paraben and propyl paraben, "not to be used in leave-on products designed for application on the nappy area of children under three years of age."

  

Untuk list penuh, boleh tengok dekat website NPRA (Biro Pengawalan Farmaseutikal Kebangsaan) link ni: [http://npra.moh.gov.my/images/Guidelines\_Central/Guidelines\_on\_Cosmetic/2017/feb2017/Annex\_VI\_release-15\_Feb\_2017-\_201701.pdf](http://npra.moh.gov.my/images/Guidelines_Central/Guidelines_on_Cosmetic/2017/feb2017/Annex_VI_release-15_Feb_2017-_201701.pdf)

## **So, nak avoid paraben ke tak?**

  

[!

](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjLf76T_SAM0lFcGFNvvEAKUiQdwP94zkDeMyyL_3QbTtqNsKB7559eaGcp9HSPof8D2Ukpmwe7kq83KztZ_qXXB6LBdioj9gmE-EXiJhJ5Ztt763ZuVQZkmlZ6Q6o54GyMtSYY333XnuJM/s1600/GG6DKqOa.jpg)

  

60% daripada 7676 orang kata dia akan avoid product yang ada paraben.

  

Kalau ikut nasihat daripada Kementerian Kesihatan Malaysia, link [http://www.myhealth.gov.my/en/use-preservative-cosmetic-preparation/](http://www.myhealth.gov.my/en/use-preservative-cosmetic-preparation/)

  

[!

](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEh5wzPqTxmh317gW0GG39gASEbdMdoSHdxWrJK5WMuhmVG3zX-tvYjiRczRX1Y1dtFWB00xqBYG76CSQwejtcdtuVSj14h2by5xdAGGRZxXFuhFN-WCxHX0McsIV9Tl8Yc80_sGadgTJtpG/s1600/Screen+Shot+2018-06-22+at+10.51.30+AM.png)

  

1\. Baca label untuk apa apa warning atau ingredients

  

2\. Kalau rasa rasa ada allergi dengan paraben, better elakkan. Kalau taktahu, boleh try buat patch test dulu.

  

Cara buat patch test: [www.imanabdulrahim.com/2017/10/patch-test](http://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2017/10/patch-test.html). Kalau beli product baru, please patch test dulu kalau nak tahu sesuai ke tak. 

  

3\. Kalau ada apa apa soalan, atau nak report side effect, boleh email dekat **kosmetik@npra.gov.my**

Conclusion dia? Depends on kulit masing masing. For me, I tak ada any negative effect on paraben so I'll continue using it. If you have negative effect from using paraben atau pun allergic, better jangan guna. Kalau rasa was was juga lepas baca ni, boleh juga avoid. Tiada paksaan untuk guna and ada banyak lagi produk lain yang tak ada paraben :)
