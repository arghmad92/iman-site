Ni gambar during acne and sebulan selepas I tempek kan segala macam benda. Tak ada la sekali gus. Satu satu lah. Sebelum ni just basic skincare routine gitu je.

  

**The basic products:**

  

[!

](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjTlH3NvDkYNqSHtVKPPphZpmFmX4vZUKoP_KFXi4l4tVF7x5TdQ36ZHoKznJOfET7lUdlxCAwR_iboP7-Qlch_kXarum_Vpl3fUe46zwI5WDz-3zPgmeNULOkcCqHLJfqbu3H_dVggEbw/s1600/Photo+16-10-2017%252C+11+53+09+AM.jpg)

  

**1\. Cleanser** - Use a gentle, low pH cleanser. This _Cosrx Low pH Good Morning Cleanser_ is good!

Review: [COSRX LOW PH GOOD MORNING GEL CLEANSER REVIEW](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/06/cosrx-low-ph-good-morning-gel-cleanser.html) (Price RM25-RM39 at Hermo or Shopee, depends lah ada sale ke tak)

You can also use Cetaphil or Simple Gel Cleanser (depends on your skin, do patch test before)

  

**2\. Toner** - _Cosrx AHA/BHA Clarifying Treatment Toner_. This toner is just an ok toner for me. Can reduce whitehead, blackhead and blemishes. Quite drying for dry skin but it's good for oily skin. Please avoid any toner that contains alcohol. (Price RM45-50 at Hermo or Shopee, depends lah ada sale ke tak)

  

**3\. Essence** - _Cosrx Advanced Snail 96 Mucin Power Essence_. Great for hydration, soothe the skin. Sometimes kalau kulit oily sangat, just use this essence as moisturized. (Price RM49.90-RM65 at Hermo or Shopee, depends lah ada sale ke tak)

  

**4\. Moisturizer**. Use lightweight and gentle moisturizer. Sometimes skip je moisturizer ni sebab essence dah hydrating enough, so it depends on your skin jugak.

  

**5\. Sunscreen** \- This is very important. Kalau dah pakai actives, kulit jadi photosensitive and senang burn, so pakai la sunscreen. Boleh juga prevent dari acne scars semua tu jadi makin gelap. _Biore UV Watery Essence_ is good enough for oily skin. (Price RM27-30 at Guardian or Watsons depends lah ada sale ke tak)

Paula's Choice 2% BHA Liquid

  

**Salicylic Acid**: Good for acne. Boleh penetrate sampai ke dalam pores, sangat efektif untuk kurangkan segala breakout, blackheads and whiteheads. Boleh calm down the skin, impove texture kulit jugak.

  

  

**2\. Benzoyl Peroxide** - Salah satu cara yang paling berkesan untuk jerawat. Dia punya strength dari 2.5% sampai 10%. Yang ni Oxy 10 means 10% of benzoyl peroxide, yang paling kuat. Tapi a bit drying, and sometimes pedih sikit. Kalau risau kulit sensitif, ambil Oxy 5 pun ok jugak.

](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhBaxNH9E2vUCelMrjwvpVRoMomlmwGU59Nj3S8pAsw_qEu4HNjcGGBLkgX0BXMDGIlCF39qbNkc5zE8y36FhJRtIbHdmHHhUyOboJuMCy3cz42JiG_m1jMYyfZjhnF3UlVbd0OTfAqWOs/s1600/Photo+16-10-2017%252C+11+54+44+AM.jpg)

Oxy 10

  

  

**3\. Clay mask** - 2-3 times per week. Innisfree is good. Freeman pun boleh juga for cheaper option (Around RM20-30 at Guardian) Mainly untuk kurangkan oil, muka berminyak minyak tapi ni temporary solution je. Bukannya pakai ni, minyak terus takut takut nak keluar. When to use? Normally lepas dah pakai toner, sapu clay mask ni. Biar 15-20 minutes lepas tu bilas. Then sambung la essence ke serum ke sheet mask ke moisturizer ke

However, ni semua guideline je. Jangan la tiru satu satu lepastu kalau tak berjaya, nak marah. Know your skin first. Mana tahu sebenarnya kulit korang sensitif dengan ingredients tu, lepastu habis naik breakout. Patch test dulu. Letak dekat belakang telinga atau tempat yang paling sensitive (bawah dagu ke, jawline ke) untuk tahu product tu ok ke tak. Kalau rasa pedih pedih, jangan la guna.

  

Baca reviews dulu. Baca good reviews, bad reviews. Jangan terus main beli je. Tahu kenapa beli and kenapa nak try product tu.

  

Kalau teruk sangat, terus pergi jumpa doctor.
