![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgbep1ZKycixFrkn4bHWJl92R25VIFKHLj96ghBWA0NRFjf-BPaADG_Zh_P3RzCVd3EA8J7ys15OrrpZCt6CFebY7Yw_ELndAZINx5rgsS_iMH02qAMnNs1g4yJwQWJfNNJVyBA6IQ4-nKd/s1600/BC3EB288-BB37-4B64-94BE-178D8370CCDF.JPG)

Ni tepung, yeast, gula, garam. Kacau kacau, lepastu masukkan air, telur dan bancuhan susu tadi.

  

Lepas dah uli, biar dia naik / kembang dulu. Sambil tu, buat filling.

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhFv9yW-40sTPyOctS_3QiD4b1GM2uaY9kxxZXSGV1bo5W1Cw_lmol1KxGR0Fkx9qAS-cyYIVNCBeHnB7Za-hYasNCO_Uq0SLblC8UWhVycHEcj8Rnl7VDnqz3LR00BqyflxEkEcWjkaXvd/s1600/0E9221F7-8F06-4479-95A5-531BA331645F.JPG)

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjPCrTrawmoi3Eze1I-zGd2wlNefJ-HDZlzuQGDDVK9IId-g7Km2uizNsV1HePwxotE_2cNUNMnBzT7FW_Mgc-9MGus79qbQtnko1XGXl0E41UE3JFyOyG-1Oi0h1ZcU2oG_JOarBd7-gL8/s1600/9B012C24-F397-4582-ADF3-F2EDF8DD489B.JPG)

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiaYhMEBW5HwZ2hiDCVywH4rBg0eKeddgdMGUc1SK4iFEa348Pb9sL82iXAkPMbilk_AroWFR0v35w_CZVBOfcqUHBEkrbGNomLDeHErjJnaFlgtcWt42gn1rtGVhxwwECYl2_DIYXujvuq/s1600/1435887D-1787-4725-A8EF-854BB5C1998D.JPG)
