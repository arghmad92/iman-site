Basically, both tak ada irritating ingredients like alcohol, fragrance, essential oils and sulphates. Kalau dulu dulu, memang susah sangat nak cari cleanser yang tak ada semua benda ni, especially yang takde irritating ingredients, fragrance, and sulphates tu memang selalu ada dalam cleanser. I try to avoid them sebab I have sensitive skin but it also depends on the formulation of the product. Kalau ada fragrance sikit sikit dalam cleanser, okay lah sebab lepastu kita bilas kan.

  

Tapi kalau takde, lagi bagus. Macam both cleansers ni, I like! Both guna gentle surfactant, buat cleanser berbuih tapi tak pedih, both ada oatmeal extract yang best untuk soothe skin inflammation and strengthen your skin barrier. Sebab tu nama dia **OAT**ELLIA and **OAT**MINO.

  

Oatellia: Oat + Camellia (Camellia Sinensis is green tea) - Ni I rasa je tau, tak sure betul ke tak hahaha

Oatmino: Oat + Amino Acids (Sebab dia ada 15 types of amino acids)

  

Yang beza dia, Oatellia ada Salicylic Acid (BHA) and Lactic Acid (AHA). Tapi AHA yang digunakan dalam cleanser ni tak tinggi sangat rasanya je sebab dia dekat tengah tengah list. The main exfoliating ingredient is from the 2% Salicylic Acid. Salicylic Acid is good if you have acne, breakout, excess oil or blackhead. It may cause purging because it is a chemical exfoliator. 

  

In my personal opinion, 2% salicylic acid is quite high, especially if you are a beginners. Tapi disebabkan product ni wash off, kita bilas lepas guna, dia tak stay lama atas kulit. Kalau leave on macam toner or serum, kena berhati hati sikit. Lagipun, cleanser ni formulated with soothing ingredients jugak, takdelah dia exfoliating sahaja. Apa apa pun, make sure you already have a basic skincare routine, you know how to hydrate your skin and patch test first before guna.

  

**Boleh guna Oatellia every day tak?**

Boleh try guna dua/tiga hari sekali dulu, untuk tengok how your skin react. Kalau rasa okay, you can use it everyday, recommend sekali sehari, waktu malam sahaja. Siang siang guna lah Oatmino sebab Oatmino is a gentle cleanser, guna setiap hari pun boleh.

  

**Kalau pregnant atau breastfeeding, boleh guna Oatellia tak?**

Since it contains 2% Salicylic Acid, kena berhati hati. Normally doctors suggest untuk tak guna Salicylic Acid above 2% especially in leave on products (toner, serum etc). For me, kalau nak selamat, I tak guna during pregnancy especially in my first trimester walaupun dia hanya cleanser, lepastu kita bilas. Better safe than sorry. Apa apa pun, consult with your doctor dulu, okay?

**THOUGHTS / FIRST IMPRESSION**

  

**Oatmino Refreshing Cream Cleanser**: I use this cleanser everyday, AM and PM. I really love the texture, it's creamy and rasa best cuci muka pakai this one. Lepas cuci muka pun tak rasa ketat, pedih ataupun kering. Kulit rasa bersih, lembut and selesa. It is low pH (kulit manusia memang slightly low pH so lagi bagus guna cleanser yang low pH supaya tak ganggu our skin barrier). It has low to medium cleansing power, sesuai untuk orang with dry/combination/normal skin type, people with damaged skin barrier, or yang prefer mild gentle cleanser for everyday use.

  

  

**Oatellia Exfoliating Gel Cleanser**: I only use this cleanser ⅔ kali seminggu je. Although it is an exfoliating cleanser, dia tak buat kulit jadi kering and pedih, thanks to the soothing ingredients. It's also low pH. If you have oily/combination skin, acne, breakout, excess oil, clogged pores or blackhead, you'll love this cleanser. It has a moderate cleansing formula.

  

Overall, both are good and serve different purpose so boleh pilih yang mana lagi sesuai for your skin.
