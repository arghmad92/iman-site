 Missha Time Revolution The First Treatment Essence (Intense Moist), MUJI Moisturizing Milk (High Moisture) and Laneige Lip Sleeping Mask!

  
  
  
  
  

In 2017, I haven't really tried a lot of skincare during the first few months. My skincare was so basic. Cleanse and moisturize. Done. So I haven't tried a lot. I only started to buy and try this and that, mid 2017. So anyways, lets get started.

  

  

 **Missha Time Revolution The First Treatment Essence (Intense Moist)**

I love how this essence really hydrate my skin, makes my skin look brighter and glow. I love how it absorbs into my skin, making my skin feels really soft and supple. Definitely my forever favourite. I've finished this bottle already, currently trying other essence but will repurchase this <3

  

Full review: [Missha Time Revolution The First Treatment Essence Review](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/09/missha-first-treatment-essence.html)

  

  

  
  
  
  
  

**MUJI Moisturizing Milk (High Moisture)**

One of my best discovery in 2017. I have been eyeing on MUJI's skincare for quite a long time, searching for reviews but there's almost none. No reviews from bloggers, youtubers etc. But, YOLO. Sampai bila nak tunggu kan. So I just beli and pakai. Try sendiri. It is reallyyyy good. And sekarang ni ramai dah yang guna. Finished mine already, and will repurchase kalau tak jumpa moisturizer yang lagi best, since this moisturizer is quite affordable.

  

Full review: [MUJI Moisturizing Milk (High Moisture) Review](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/08/muji-moisturizing-milk-review.html)

  

**Laneige Lip Sleeping Mask**

Lip care is also skincare, right? My fav lip product, I use this every single night, everytime. Guna macam lipbalm je, kisah apa kan. REALLY WORTH IT. Just go and buy. Thanks.

  

Full review: [Laneige Lip Sleeping Mask](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/07/laneige-lip-sleeping-mask-review.html)
