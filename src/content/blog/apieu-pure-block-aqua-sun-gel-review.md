**My thoughts on Apieu Pure Block Aqua Sun Gel**   
This sunscreen is a chemical sunscreen since it only has chemical filters. It's SPF50 and PA+++ which is enough to use outdoor. I prefer using SPF30-50 and PA+++ / PA++++ to get enough protection.  
  
It has a gel texture that absorbs nicely into the skin without any greasiness. It has a slight cooling effect tapi bukan pedih. The texture is so nice for my dry skin, doesn't make my skin feels dry and it has a dewy finish, nampak macam natural glow and tak ada white cast (white cast ni yang nampak muka macam kelabu).  
  
Overall, I really enjoyed this sunscreen except for the scent but it's not a big deal. It's really suitable for my dry skin, no irritation, and the price is also affordable. If they have an unscented version, I'd totally buy.
