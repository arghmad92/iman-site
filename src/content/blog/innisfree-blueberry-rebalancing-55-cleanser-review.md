My thoughts on  Innisfree Blueberry Rebalancing 5.5 Cleanser

  

  
  

  

**What it claims?**

1\. **Sub-acidic cleanser for well-balanced skin conditions** \- Minimized skin stress produced during cleansing by controlling the oil-water balance and sub-acidic pH level so the skin can feel comfortable. Your skin reaches its best skin condition before moving on to your skincare routine. 

2\. **Moist cleansing that leaves the essential moisture for the skin** - Protects the essential moisture and healthy lipid layer to keep skin feeling dewy and supple after cleansing.

**How to use? (Based on the web site)**

ake out and adequate amount on wet hands and lather up. Gently massage the entire face then rinse off with lukewarm water.

**My thought on  Innisfree Blueberry Rebalancing 5.5 Cleanser**

  

If you wanna read on how to choose a cleanser: [SKINCARE 101: HOW TO CHOOSE A FACIAL CLEANSER?](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/07/choose-cleanser.html)

  

Among the criteria:

  

1\. Tak buat kulit rasa ketat and tegang? Checked. Lepas pakai memang tak rasa tight or dry, rasa fresh je.

  

2\. Low pH cleanser? Yes. As mentioned before, the pH value is around pH5.

  

3\. No irritating ingredients? Yes! No SLS, fragrance, menthol, alcohol etc. However, it essential oils (orange peel oil and lavender oil) so kalau siapa yang tak boleh dengan essential oils, you might want to avoid this but I am okay with it.

  

4\. Use gentle surfactant? Yes, there is no SLS.

  

As for the claims, "sub-acidic cleanser for well-balanced skin conditions", yes! "Moist cleansing that leaves the essential moisture for the skin**"**? Yes, since there is no tight feeling after using this cleanser. However, I don't really like the citrus scent which is quite strong for me. I prefer something milder. This is one of the most raved cleanser in 2016 and I memang dah masuk dalam wishlist sebab nak try. 2 years later, baru boleh try hahaha.

  

  

**Would I repurchase?** 

Yes, if they change the scent into milder scent :)
