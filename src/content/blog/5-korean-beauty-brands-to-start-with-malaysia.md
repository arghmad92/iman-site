**Why Korean skincare?**  
I love using Korean skincare because it focuses on hydration and healthy glow. Kalau sekarang, orang panggil glass skin atau chok chok skin (which means bouncy soft skin lah gitu).  
  

[!

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjCsJGs0VRntdYkcJj3Sf9eq3bPJw65CUdDaxKYD9IttZ3NtPEHI9I8Da-cBISq5kqnY8XhfJzOqBSVAbP61tAl6tQQsEuabrWvF43NwjB5PfQeR81CJVFHLropr8MpLekK-COcG8-Mj6NP/s1600/hSuuXz7n.jpg)
