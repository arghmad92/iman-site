Harini nak share how I buat chicken broth untuk Maysaa. I buat ni since Maysaa 8-9 bulan.

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhIPCTY_k1EKZJTy7H7V_4KIDCFZHg0Jnoo_j_X7J7GTDy0-LJxKbuxZoeqJavvb0wKTtjC660GaI6baJZ2f8An4yP9CiQ3kh36WF5DDkoD-iX6XvzBWqPfY5rmyu1NwJjPguoIf2gNDheV/s1600/Photo+16-05-2018%252C+9+13+12+PM.jpg)

  

  

  
  

  

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgiv_RMmz1ywvlCPp_HzFxZYUH2Pe-A3OnTfFbSYfSD0qvC6Atl8w8qzBrerhM4TH4jgVGZ7h0Ry4ciaX5Mhp7mTisCgIYR0CMbZRHho6u-93zml6v7PDHf3ffEWeYezEHNTODXxwZEZAp_/s1600/Photo+16-05-2018%252C+9+11+09+PM.jpg)

  

**Bahan bahan yang diperlukan:**

1\. Rangka ayam / kaki ayam. Tapi I tak minat kaki ayam walaupun dia bagus so I guna rangka ayam je

2\. Bawang putih, bawang besar

3\. Halia

4\. Celery

5\. Carrot

6\. Tomato

7\. Rempah (bunga lawang, pelaga, kayu manis, cengkih)

8\. Lemon - perah

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhIPCTY_k1EKZJTy7H7V_4KIDCFZHg0Jnoo_j_X7J7GTDy0-LJxKbuxZoeqJavvb0wKTtjC660GaI6baJZ2f8An4yP9CiQ3kh36WF5DDkoD-iX6XvzBWqPfY5rmyu1NwJjPguoIf2gNDheV/s1600/Photo+16-05-2018%252C+9+13+12+PM.jpg)

  

Masukkan semua dalam periuk. Make sure stainless steel. Guna api kecil je. I normally biar atas api for 2-3 hours, tapi kena selalu tengok.

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgQU4ZNQfq5t8NGFrQK_giULrMYecsasFlW3qOwhWEGLFywZE4YPH3wVKPKBeG18fTWHAekJ3EKDM4CgDYFv0d8j59emx_eYqZBbm5xkzndFzTTvLmP8nzAN8PQQM639Zrmr3RdgQcooIVo/s1600/Photo+17-05-2018%252C+12+28+47+AM.jpg)

  

Agak agak dah siap tu, tuttup api and biar sejuk sikit. I tapis ambil air dia sahaja. Yang lain semua buang. Tapi ayam dengan carrot tu sometimes I ratah :P

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEi9HkSQiHPlx3JqeQWQUgDWsoNNQurC5zHyaY3tYNVI1ctoPDuz-lFksNmbkfD3h0ubY8N8F3gKbmB9GQn8vIsF5bLUWa-vJ0Xj9NPmtbRTXLAiH3UoV3cnWXPGgNoUeH5_HfUDq7S3QvQn/s1600/Photo+17-05-2018%252C+12+39+57+AM.jpg)

  

Lepas tu I masukkan dalam bekas macam ni. 4oz. I beli dekat kedai bakeri, dia jual 100 pieces for RM8 macam tu lebih kurang. Entah la lupa dah haha. And sebab guna periuk kecil, hasil pun sikit je lah. 

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiU49UL0cw_97ljQdObiM_0x_dd8vjQG0X2WUdtzul1PW3j8MffAAD-mHZGtPr3c6GAwEEMVvPMketSkz4azqBbdybJtZq4I_ubMn9BWe84phJv2SOgDFH6i-secdDjJ314eMGOfUT8alTx/s1600/Photo+17-05-2018%252C+12+41+33+AM.jpg)

  

 Lepastu boleh la masuk dalam freezer and bekukan. Nanti kalau nak guna, defrost and terus boleh guna. Done!
