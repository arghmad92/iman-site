**Where is it located?**

Level 2 Isetan, The Gardens. Just masuk je Isetan Level 2 yang ada DIGI dengan Gongcha tu (one of my fav drinks!), go to your left, and nampak bahagian jual stroller ni. Dia dekat belakang tu.

  

The room itself is so pretty, ada tiga tempat nak breastfeed, ada kerusi panjang nak tunggu and tempat nak salin diapers.

**Jusco Midvalley**  
  

I also went to the baby room dekat dalam Jusco, Mid Valley. Same level dengan kids punya barang, dekat area toilet. I was veryyyy dirty and smelly and gloomy. I don't know why.

  

Tapi sebab Maysaa dah lapar, I redha je lah susukan dia dekat bilik tu but lantai dia basah... And gelap. And sempit. Sedih betul rasa. Tak sempat nak ambil gambar sebab ramai orang dekat dalam tu, and a bit riuh.

  

Tapi satu je best. Dia ada weighing scale. Sempat la letak Maysaa and timbang berat dia hahaha #Sempat

  

That's all. I haven't tried other baby rooms sebab Maysaa normally feed dalam kereta. Tapi time kitorang pergi The Gardens & Mid Valley ni, kitorang naik Uber so alang alang tu try je lah baby room dia.
