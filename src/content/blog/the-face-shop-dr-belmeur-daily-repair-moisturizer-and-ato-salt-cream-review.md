![](https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-W5cOOZcewkk/WldOhKczRKI/AAAAAAAAFx4/2YcVj_7Ux2c-M97gGJcFsFdRcDFWOIKjwCLcBGAs/s640/Photo%2B11-01-2018%252C%2B7%2B21%2B35%2BPM.jpg)

  
  
  
  
  
  

I am still searching for the best moisturizer for my dry skin. One of the moisturizer that I really wanted to try is The Face Shop Dr. Belmeur Daily Repair Ato Salt Cream.

  

Why?

  

I am looking for a moisturizer that does not have alcohol and fragrance it in. Another criteria is, I want to have glycerin, niacinamide (brightening effect) and ceramide (which is really good for dry/dehydrated skin and can repair your skin's moisture barrier). I used the website skincarisma, where you can search for products that you want with ingredients that your skin needs. #tips

  

So anyways, I found out that The Face Shop's Dr. Belmeur Daily Repair Ato Salt Cream fits my criteria. Tapi nak beli, mahal......

  
  
  
  
  
  

Luckily, I joined this run event, tiba tiba dapat this travel set. OMG I AM SO EXCITED sebab dah lama dah wish list. It also comes with the moisturizer.

  

  

First of all, about the **Ato Salt Cream**.

  

**What it claims?**

Mineral salt cream that provides deep moisturization and itch relief for dry skin. 

Skin-moisturizing and barrier-enhancing solution.

 Contatins sea salt, peony extract, phytosphingosine and ceramide. 

Skin-sync Rx™ is used to help maintain healthy skin, including moisturizing and treating dry, flaky skin, relieving itching and stretching the skin barrier.

 Formulated without 10 additives: parabens, synthetic pigments, artificial fragrance, triethanolamine, mineral oil, benzophenone-3, propylene glycol, ethanol, talc, benzoic acid. 

Formulated without artificial fragrance

Dermatologically tested.

  

  

Sounds so good, right??? I've finished two travel size sample already. My experience was, quite good! It is quite thick, I also felt really heavy for my skin. It doesn't absorbs quite well. I feel the greasiness, melekit etc. But that's okay. Just apply a really thin layer and you'll be fine.

  

My skin feels really moisturized, no more dry patches, no irritation. Suka! Will definitely use this kalau ada dry patches, and I will always have this in stock, guna bila perlu je.

  

**Overall**

A great moisturizer for dry skin, ingredients are really good

  

**Rate**: 4/5

  

**Recommended for?**

Dry and sensitive skin. If you have burning (because of the sun), I think this one is really good.

  

**Who will not love this?**

Oily/combination skin because this is quite thick.

  

**Will I repurchase?**

Yes

Moving on to the **The Face Shop Dr. Belmeur Daily Repair Moisturizer**. Didn't plan to try this. Tapi dah ada dalam set tu, I try je lah. Surprisingly, I like this texture more than the Ato Salt Cream. It is not as thick as the Ato Salt Cream which absorbs into my skin beautifully, leaving a very well hydrated skin. Oh I love how it feels on my skin.

  

**What is claims?**

A low-irritant moisturizer that calms sensitive skin and protects skin damaged by the external environment. 

Daily low-irritant moisturizing solution: Contains phytoshpingosine, moroheiya, birch tree sap and Skin-sync Rx™ which helps form a moisture layer to calm dry skin. 

Formulated without 9 additives: parabens, synthetic pigments, triethanolamine, mineral oil, benzophenone-3, animal-derived ingredients, propylene glycol, talc, benzoic acid. 

Allergen-free fragrance: formulated without 26 types of additives with possible allergens, to minimize skin irritation. 

Dermatologically tested.

  

I like this moisturizer more that Ato Salt Cream because of it's texture. It does not contain niacinamide and ceramide, but that's okay. It still works for my dry skin and I love it.

  

**Overall**

A great moisturizer for dry skin. I recommend this!!!! Will suggest this if anyone ask me about moisturizer for dry skin. This one is on the list!

  

**Rate**: 4/5

  

  

  

  

  

**Recommended for?**

Dry and sensitive skin

  

**Who will not love this?**

Oily/combination skin

  

**Will I repurchase?**

Yes (but of course I am going to try other moisturizers first hehehe)
