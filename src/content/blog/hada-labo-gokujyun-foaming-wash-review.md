Ever since I know about the importance of low pH cleanser, of course I'll only buy low pH cleanser je. Click here to read more: [SKINCARE 101 : LOW PH CLEANSER](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/08/skincare-101-low-ph-cleanser.html)

  

Basically, low pH cleanser is important to maintain our skin's moisture barrier. Supaya kulit tak ada banyak masalah.

  

  

  

So right now, the criteria yang I pentingkan kalau nak beli cleanser:

1\. Mesti low pH (pH4-6)

2\. Gentle, tak mengeringkan kulit

3\. Tak ada SLS sebab SLS ni drying, so I would like to avoid it.

  

I tak kisah sangat kalau dia ada paraben, sebab for me, paraben ok je, unless orang yang kulit dia sensitive with paraben, so elakkan lah. I pun tak kisah dia punya texture, foam ke gel ke cream ke, ok je.

**My experience with Hada Labo Gokujyun Foaming Wash**

The reason why I bought this is because there are quite a lot of good reviews about this product, so why not? It's Hada Labo (normally Hada Labo punya products memang best), the ingredients are okay, and most importantly, it's low pH.

  

For reference, I have dry skin. I need a cleanser yang tak akan mengeringkan lagi kulit. Taknak cleanser yang rasa ketat kat muka lepas pakai, yang lagi mengeringkan and memedihkan muka. Nak yang best and selesa please.

  

Normally foaming cleanser tak sesuai for dry skin sebab foam lagi mengeringkan kulit? But takpelah, I will try this first since it also claims ada Hyaluronic Acid which can hold moisture for long-lasting hydration.

  

So anyways, I've been using this cleanser everyday for 6 weeks plus. This is my main cleanser. (Sebab I still ada cleanser lain yang sometimes I guna kalau pergi travel or balik rumah parents, tak kuasa nak angkut semua benda) But this is the main cleanser.

  

It does the job lah, as a cleanser, to clean your face? Haha. Kulit rasa bersih, tapi tak mengeringkan kulit, takde irritation apa. Ok je for me. Tapi I tak ada la rasa hydrated lepas cuci muka. Rasa normal je.

  

And I rasa, this product tahan lama gila. Setahun pun tak habis rasanya. We'll see berapa lama tahan. I will keep using this (walaupun rasa dah boring sikit, rasa nak try cleanser lain hahaha). Yep, it's good.

  

**Rate**: 4.5/5

  

**Who will like this?**

I think any skin okay je, if you don't have any other skin concern (acne etc). If you're looking for a gentle low pH cleanser, you can try this one! I think it's good for dry, normal, combo skin.

  

**Who will not like this?**

If you're looking for a cleanser yang boleh bersihkan makeups, kurangkan acne, scars etc, this cleanser is not it. Although, it's good to use a gentle and low pH cleanser for all skin type, supaya kulit tak makin teruk and irritated, but it depends on your preference. (For me, I prefer using a gentle cleanser lepastu tackle my skin issues using serums etc. Cleanser focus on cleaning je)

  

**Repurchase?**

Since I pun taktahu yang sekarang ni bila nak habis, maybe tak repurchase kot. Nak try cleanser lain. Tapiiii, this is really good. I totally recommend this.
