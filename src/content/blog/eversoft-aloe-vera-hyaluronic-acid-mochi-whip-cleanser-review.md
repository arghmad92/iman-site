Eversoft Aloe Vera & Hyaluronic Acid Mochi Whip Cleanser Review

  

  
  

I used to love the Eversoft's Avocado cleanser so when I saw this at Guardian, of course I gotta buy. The price is really affordable, less than RM15 for 120ml and it can lasts for 2-3 months. Murah!

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEi9OTWEgRCl1MJiWKqtF2c8kdtyh5gDQmNhdkr9KZAUNhvv5Ck1mM8ajpJZZ_AI8j1jEpkeL-5mzztGaFV1aJuHpgj6saZ6TTDcCjJQYAfitwdzV9aJIsRbvCub7nIqXeDT_cjj5GsE22ly/s1600/E70B6D8F-9C3B-4EC0-B9D1-B5DB18643409.jpg)

  

It claims to result in **dewy and bouncy skin** and **moisture balanced skin**. So is this true? I'll share my thoughts below. Nantikan. Cewah.

  

How to use? Squeeze 1.5cm of foam onto palm and lather with water. Massage gently onto face and splash clean for a refreshing, dewy feeling.

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEga4ncSa_Q9x8J1kal7NGJf8BdeBgTLgHszsWvTAeRcdkQw4HQiN_hkCyQ9FKN9WZtukqEobiU9SRr3e7oWDMVqAvBp8Jrm4ObXX_v3Kuv8KlXC3qKj0RZdNQ089rJyPwA7shXM2-HljzrH/s1600/512C227C-87C4-4743-8A35-E02D62FFCAA6.jpg)

  

This is how it looks like after I lather it with water. Ni pun I guna sikit je. Buih dia banyak. Padan lah dia claim as "Mochi Whip" kot.

  

  

 **Ingredients**

Water, PEG-8, Myristic Acid, Stearic Acid, Potassium Hydroxide, Palmitic Acid, Glycerin, Lauric Acid, Butylene Glycol, Glyceryl Stearate SE, Polyquaternium-7, Tetrasodium EDTA, BHT, PEG-90M, Aloe Barbadensis (Organic Aloe Vera) Leaf Extract, Pearl Powder, Sodium Hyaluronate, Fragrance, Phenoxyethanol

  

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEh3I3XGX7Mpr2_X86378xIgSyfKOZF1AYc2OBmsNoQ3c-8-AtqJ30ZGYDVbMNopntAFVaNKnfQ1NdMw-fe2otkfrkb4Y9xsXgsWt8WJlJTC00J738OiXksAq-zSeDdYiwUd1HhdHgfKdxRG/s1600/IMG_0625.jpg)

  

When I tested the pH value, I found out that the pH is around pH8 which is quite high jugak. You can also read why I prefer a low pH cleanser: [http://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2017/08/skincare-101-low-ph-cleanser.html](http://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2017/08/skincare-101-low-ph-cleanser.html)

  

It comes in a 120ml plastic tube with a light green flap cap, sama macam normal cleanser packaging. I also like the design, very catchy and kemas. One of the resign why I bought it too, sebab design cantik and segala details semua printed dekat belakang including full ingredient list.

  

I ada share on [How to choose a cleanser?](https://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2018/07/choose-cleanser.html) << Click here to read

  

In my personal experience, when I use this cleanser, I don't feel any itchiness or pedih pedih, cuma lepas cuci tu, kulit rasa tegang and ketat. Too drying for my skin. Does it makes my skin feels dewy and bouncier? Nope. Do I like my skin after using this cleanser? Also nope. I didn't really enjoy using this cleanser and there's a slight scent to it. Bau wangi fragrance which I don't really fancy tapi takdelah over powering sampai sakit hidung.

  

  

The good side is, there is no SLS, no alcohol, no irritating ingredients (except fragrance). However, if you have fungal acne, you might wanna avoid this cleanser since it contains Myristic Acid, Stearic Acid, Palmitic Acid and Lauric Acid.

  

Will I recommend this product? If you have really dry skin or dry skin like me, you might not enjoy this. Also, I prefer a low pH cleanser to maintain my skin barrier.

  

**Would I repurchase?** 

No, not really. Cowieee.
