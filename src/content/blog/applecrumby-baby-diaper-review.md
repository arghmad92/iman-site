**What I like about the diaper**

First impression, of course, nipis gila! I never tried a diaper that is this thin. Ok ye la yela, anak baru satu, tak try banyak diapers pun. So sebab first time, rasa la beza dia. And lembut sangaaaat! Ultra soft.  
  
So bila pakai, rasa macam baby tak pakai diapers. Fit dia pun just nice. Tak pernah bocor langsung bila pakai. Leak guard terrer. Pakai overnight for almost 12 hours pun tak pernah bocor!  
(Or sebab my baby tak ganas sangat so dia still secure? We'll see in the future...)  
  
Ada Wetness Indicator Strips yang strip warna kuning kat depan diapers tu, akan jadi warna biru kalau dah penuh. Tapi, Maysaa jarang la sampai strip tu jadi semua biru. Biasa terus salin je kalau rasa dah lama. And, susah nak check strip tu, kena bukak the whole seluar so macam a bit leceh.  
  
Waistband dia stretchable and selesa, tape dia melekat kuat. So tak ada la tertanggal ke apa. (Again, maybe my baby kecik lagi so dia belum ganas).  
  
Material dia pun best, katanya guna natural ingredient, tak ada chemical. Tak pernah lagi kena nappy rash, tak pernah lagi peha dia merah sebab diapers ketat sangat. Tapi kalau pakai lama sangat, dia akan berbau sikit. Well, of course lah berbau hahaha tapi I pakai Drypers, cepat gila berbau!

**What I like about the wipes**

It's thick and safe to use. I tak suka pakai wipes yang wangi sebab risau kulit baby sensitive dengan fragrance. This wipes memang tak ada bau and suitable for sensitive skin. Tapi it's a bit pricey so I may stick dengan wipes yang I biasa guna haha.

  

**Overall**

I love this, very worth the money. Will repurchase this diapers again and again! (Unless jumpa yang lagi best & affordable)
