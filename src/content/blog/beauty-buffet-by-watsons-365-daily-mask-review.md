Product Brand: Beauty Buffet by Watsons 365 Daily Mask

Product Type: Sheet Mask

Price: RM6.90 per piece (23g)  
Availability: All nationwide Watsons stores  
Made in: Taiwan  
  
Purple packaging: Water Lily Hydrating Mask  
  
Yellow: Royal Jelly Nourishing Mask  
  
Ada banyak lagi jenis lain contohnya Black Pearl Whitening Mask (biru), Rose Water Whitening Mask (pink) and Green Caviar Elastic Sheet Mask (hijau), Cherry Blossom Whitening Mask (peach). One of the reason why I bought it is because of the packaging. Comel!  
  
  

![](https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-_mAgOvV6ZnE/WfBOsa8wyEI/AAAAAAAAFME/jyLMQY65yko-dX81im8fDHZ4JUoVlbqeACLcBGAs/s640/2016071512281527925.jpg)

  
  

**How to use?**

Use after cleanser. (And after toner, essence, serum if you have)

1\. Unfold mask and remove white backing

2\. Align mask from top to bottom

2\. Leave on about 20-30 minutes and remove it -- Do not use it overnight tau

3\. Massage slightly with fingertips for better absorption of the excess essence on skin.

  

No need to rinse. 

  
  
  
**Water Lily Hydrating Mask**  
  
**Main ingredients:**  
**1\. Water Lily Extract** \- Nourishing & moisturizing. Enhance skin's hydration and luminosity  
**2\. Saccharide Isomerate** - Water binding ingredient that retains & intensifies hydration. Lock-in moisture & boost radiance.  
**3\. Aloe Extract** - Hydrator, replenish moisture  
  
![](https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-glHunl8vcNk/WfBVf0yNQ8I/AAAAAAAAFMc/Bdw2znzZYRslZaXfymQNMotfmKq0hzXVgCLcBGAs/s640/Photo%2B25-10-2017%252C%2B3%2B11%2B50%2BPM.jpg)  
  
  

  

  

**Material**: Normal sheet mask with plastic sheet

**Essence**: Clear essence tapi macam tak ada la banyak sangat essence dia.  
**Scent**: No distinct smell that I noticed.  
**Fit**: The fit is just nice to cover all my face and nose, surprisingly. I love the fit!  
**Adherence**: Quite ok even after 30 minutes.  
  
**How I feel about it?**  
No irritation, tak gatal, tak panas, very soothing and hydrating. After 20 minutes, I remove the sheet mask, and let the essence absorb. Tak sticky sangat and I love how my skin feels. Very hydrating.  
  
  

**Recommended?**

Yesss, if you have dry/dehydrated skin

  
**Will I repurchase?**

Yesss

  

**Royal Jelly Nourishing Mask**

  

**Main ingredients:**

**1\. Royal Jelly Extract** \- Soothe, firm & refine

**2\. Acerola Fruit Extract** - Rick in vitamins the help replenish, nourish and firm your skin

**3\. Aloe Barbadensis Extract** - Hydrator, replenish moisture

  

![](https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-KnbIyoKfrB8/WfBVf-SYwHI/AAAAAAAAFMY/tzbrfji7w7wZLI0lW9XqSBEeGVmgvWkDACLcBGAs/s640/Photo%2B25-10-2017%252C%2B3%2B12%2B09%2BPM.jpg)

  

**Material**: Normal sheet mask with plastic sheet

**Essence**: Clear essence tapi macam tak ada la banyak sangat essence dia.  
**Scent**: Weird honey smell that lingers... I don't like it  
**Fit**: The fit is just nice to cover all my face and nose, surprisingly. I love the fit!  
**Adherence**: Quite ok even after 30 minutes.  
  
**How I feel about it?**  
No irritation, tak gatal, tak panas, very soothing and hydrating. However, I hate the smell. The honey smell is very strong :( After I removed the mask, the stickiness is there. I pat and biar 5 minutes pun still rasa melekit yang I don't really like. Lepas pakai moisturizer baru ok. For my dry skin pun melekit, imagine yang combination or oily skin. Tapi rasa la plump tu. I just don't like the stickiness.  
  
  

**Recommended?**

Nope, unless you have a very dry skin and duduk dalam aircond.

  
**Will I repurchase?**

Nope  
  
  
Lepasni nak try mask yang lain pulak, hopefully best!
