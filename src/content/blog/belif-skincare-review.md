**belif Cleansing Oil Fresh review**

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjKoRYG5t_Iel9OkLEzlsL-4REGyFjzM8qhWiN_06K0WSlOD-AA-TjeYugeJHXMXbU42MlGhLo-6139yPUH-W9XwwGnIceWRg3s3nCas_qfkY4pMGyA6d-tbxh1tanfN7u0CxEZj-a-U-4f/s640/Photo+13-12-2017%252C+3+05+17+PM.jpg)

  

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEh8KyK2XYqMQmDMwtSErUWqiUBZuGWTJ8bPCC5keOYijMhy-0BFOlhjHA3GaZ6uIGmWaQJcBC37Uzy8aB1VmSk7g-XzfDFuVY3ZXwxxashOABkVRBw-H1LjJepPA32-FpYzitvrWhsBOtfE/s640/Photo+13-12-2017%252C+3+05+24+PM.jpg)

  

**What it claims?**

This cleansing oil, suitable for oily to combination skin types, removes make-up and eliminates blackheads, leaving you with a light finished feeling. Soapberry and Sweet Flag ingredients in the cleansing oil prevents future breakouts.

  

**My experience?**

Even though I have dry skin, this product works well to remove my makeup and sunscreen. This is my current everyday oil cleanser. It didn't dry out my skin and works really well. The texture is not as thick as my other oil cleanser and it didn't emulsify a lot, but it's good enough for me.  
  
I love oil cleanser because it can clean my pores and reduce any closed comedones. I really recommend you to do double cleansing method (first step using oil cleanser and second step using water based cleanser). This is seriously a life changer.  
  

  

  

**belif Oil Control Moisturizer Fresh review**

  

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiKd9WtsX22rU_I3TO-IUaWzgwWU-6fy9HIhfpkCHJEPTc8PKHV17zSt4gCUFCkCjHaENpOxVU_4cbJfRY3l_OdTELoDK5PZdV4duDCEfk5qEF4wnoamscw3KoxxxkpXhJnszlIn36BCBQP/s640/Photo+13-12-2017%252C+3+06+01+PM.jpg)

  

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgezHXDHyAjCzbROv140KrlpzZjGBEa-RuPNZAbR-u6TNiQE8h5WxerNt6gNEs_-GYE3CmoH9EE1WFFedwhHJrpiC-3QLO3V-KuWmLk6ynK2RUjvs60KqZ8UAWeh7XzZeGAKds9cq1Xq8pg/s640/Photo+13-12-2017%252C+3+06+05+PM.jpg)

**What it claims?**

This oil-free moisturizer for oily skin type leaves you with a light finish without excess shine while keeping the skin moisturized. Rosehip extracts rich in vitamins and tannins along with oil controlling powder in the moisturizer tightens enlarged pores and improves resilience, leaving the skin clean and smooth.

  

**My experience?**

This moisturizer is really lightweight and doesn't leave a sticky feeling on my skin. However, since I have dry skin, I need to add my other moisturizer when I use this. For oily skin, I totally recommend this! My husband has oily skin and he loves this moisturizer.

  

Even though you have oily skin, you still need to make sure your skin is well hydrated and in my opinion, this thing is really good to make sure your skin is well hydrated.

**What it claims?**

This powdery B.B. cream creates a clear, translucent, and natural skin tone, making it subtle and matte without the appearance of oiliness. Containing Canna from the Caribbean, which has a natural ability to control excess sebum, the cream helps leave the skin smooth and refreshed all day long.

  

**My experience?**

Again, since I have dry skin, this product is a bit drying so I need to reallyyy prep my skin and add some oil in this product. However for oily skin, I think this is really good. It has SPF to protect from the harmful UVs and the coverage is quite decent. Good for everyday use.

belif product is really worth trying and I love it! I hope you guys love it as much as I do.
