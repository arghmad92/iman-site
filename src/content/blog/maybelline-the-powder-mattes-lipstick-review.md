Sharing my thoughts on Maybelline The Powder Mattes Lipstick (Touch of Nude, Almond Pink, Toasted Brown) 

  

  

[![](https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-xQIhnkYGSMM/WxpHf4T7nuI/AAAAAAAAGmE/DWmhOgdPmJEQMjM0QdtVXWlwKNRSaggMQCLcBGAs/s640/Photo%2B07-06-2018%252C%2B11%2B13%2B15%2BPM.jpg)

  
  
  
  
  

I ada buat post about shade Touch Of Nude here: [My Current Go To Lipstick](http://www.imanabdulrahim.com/2017/11/my-current-go-to-lipsticks.html)

  

So bila Watsons ada buy 1 free 1 offer, I beli Almond Pink and Toasted Brown cause I love the formula and texture. The price is around RM26 kot for both (Sebab buy 1 free 1)

  

**Packaging**

A normal bullet lipstick with plastic brown cap. Yang sayangnya, plastic wrapping ada tulis "Maybelline Powder Mattes" tapi dah buang plastic, dah jadi biasa je nampak.

  

[![](https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-qsn5mamamE0/WxpHf5cYeTI/AAAAAAAAGmA/C5bO2HCK6IYKF2_9yQyDpyr-2CmtC9WBgCLcBGAs/s640/Photo%2B07-06-2018%252C%2B11%2B13%2B25%2BPM.jpg)

  

**Texture and formula**

It's creamy, warna pigmented bila apply dekat bibir, easy to apply and dia dries down to powderry matte. I love the finishing and it is not drying on my lips. Rasa macam tak pakai lipstick sangat. Rasa lightweight je. Takde rasa bibir tebal semacam. And it is not sticky at all. Selesa.

  

However, it doesn't last long. Lagi lagi kalau makan or minum air, dia tak tahan lama. Kena selalu touch up.

  

Not transfer-proof and kiss-proof. Kalau minum air ke, lipstick melekat dekat cawan. Which means, it is easy to remove and senang nak bersihkan. 

  

**Shades**

I have Touch of Nude, Almond Pink and Toasted Brown. 

  

[![](https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-8qob5E1Pr9U/WxpHgLBX68I/AAAAAAAAGmI/wr7b0pHUx4A-_5mfps3MxkqEFW3o1DKsQCLcBGAs/s640/Photo%2B08-06-2018%252C%2B3%2B38%2B15%2BPM.jpg)

  

  

Overall, for the price and it's performance, I totally recommend this as an everyday lipstick! That's why I ada sampai tiga hehe.
