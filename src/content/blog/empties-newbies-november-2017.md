**Empty**: Cosrx Low pH Good Morning Gel Cleanser _-_ Cleanser

Review here: [Cosrx Low pH Good Morning Gel Cleanser](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/06/cosrx-low-ph-good-morning-gel-cleanser.html)

  

I really love this cleanser, suitable for my dry skin. Tak drying, no tight feeling. Some people may experience dryness sebab dia ada tea tree oil tapi for me, okay je. I love this!

  

  

**Newbie**: Hada Labo Gokujyun Foam Cleanser

_Why I bought it_ - It's also low pH (pH5.5-6), reviews banyak yang best, not drying and suitable for dry skin even dia foam. (Sebab dry skin better avoid foam cleanser)

  

Dah pakai 3 minggu ++ and so far, memang okay for my skin.

**  
Empty**: MUJI Moisturuzing Milk _-_ Moisturizer

Review here: [MUJI MOISTURIZING MILK](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/08/muji-moisturizing-milk-review.html)

  

This moisturizer is really really my fav! Works well for my skin and entah lah, best. Rasa sedih gila bila dah habis. Product ni tahan almost 4 months (200mL) and I pakai banyak jugak sebenarnya. Nanti nanti in the future kalau dah takde moisturizer lain yang I nak try (sebab banyak lagi wishlist moisturizer yang I nak try haha), I will definitely buy this moisturizer again. Please jangan discontinue :( 

  

  

**Newbie**: St Ives Timeless Skin Collagen Elastin Moisturizer

_Why I bought it_ - Actually I got this from instagram @budgetbeauty\_\_ (do check them out!) . Ada banyak review best about this moisturizer and it contains ceramide (good for the skin) so I really want to try this out. Tapi dia ada mineral oil, certain skin might not tolerate this so better patch test dulu. Read about patch test [here](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/10/patch-test.html)

  

I patch test seminggu to see if there is any bad reaction, and dah guna dekat muka for two weeks. Setakat ni okay je.
