## Weekly skincare routine

  

This is for weekly skincare routine pulak.

  

[![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgsiIZ4XtOiwxQbLa6SXeTGCmqjbnyEuwYYIEcAXEkzOZX6Y6ozcGPJzDRpn8OMfugmlEkYYi-SKPwaE4zDwkJ56X9twgGenpcwMVsS-if01d5ZyBXXqHXGVv9n4RemIzh7hLKVijR76Gs/s640/Photo+30-08-2017%252C+8+49+11+AM.jpg)

  

**1\. Physical exfoliator** - _St. Ives Oatmeal Scrub + Mask_

Review: [ST. IVES OATMEAL SCRUB + MASK REVIEW](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/06/st-ives-oatmeal-scrub-mask-review.html)

Only once a week sebab I have dry skin and I am scared kalau exfoliate banyak sangat, nanti muka jadi lagi kering. So I keep it only once a week.

  

When to use? After cleanser.

  

**2\. Toner** \- _Cosrx AHA/BHA Clarifying Treatment Toner_

I haven't reviewed this one dekat blog sebab tak pakai setiap hari, pakai once a week je, bila rasa perlu. It's a mild exfoliator jugak tapi boleh pakai everyday (2x sehari). I is malas so I pakai seminggu sekali je....

  

When to use? After first treatment essence.

  

**3\. Clay mask** - _Innisfree Jeju Volcanic Color Clay Mask_

Review: [INNISFREE JEJU VOLCANIC COLOR CLAY MASK REVIEW (HYDRATING)](http://imanabdulrahim.blogspot.my/2017/08/innisfree-jeju-volcanic-color-clay-mask.html)

I have dry skin so tak perlu pun pakai clay mask but this one is for hydrating so macam best je. Pakai once a week je, sebab takut terlalu drying nanti shian kulit :( So, once a week sahaja.

  

When to use? After toner.
