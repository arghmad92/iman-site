Product Name: Caudalie Grape Water

Product Brand: Caudalie

Product Type: Face Mist

Size: 75mL

Price: RM40  
Where to get? Sephora Malaysia  
  

**What it claims?**   
1\. Improved hydration and diminished skin sensitivity upon use.  
2\. Intense ability to retain water.  
3\. Perfect for sensitive skin and the perfect go-to after any harsh peels or uncomfortable irritation,  
4\. Calming treatment—soothing rosacea, reducing redness and sensitivity, and calming rashes.  
5\. Strengthens the skin's barrier function, making it more resilient and less sensitive over time.  
6\. Can be used as a toner after cleansing, as a setting spray before applying makeup, or to refresh and hydrate the skin throughout the day.  
  
  
  

**How to use?**

I normally use this to damp my sponge when applying foundation/concealer. Or, to wet my brush when applying shimmering eyeshadow. Or as a face mist to refresh during the day. Depends lah kalau perlu, I spray je.

**How I feel about Caudalie Grape Water ?**

Since I have dry skin, it is good to use this to damp my sponge when applying my foundation/concealer to give the nice glow look. Kalau tak, my foundation/concealer akan nampak bercapuk sikit. Sebenarnya pakai any face mist pun takpe je. Tapi kalau guna this face mist, rasa best sebab tak pedih kulit. Best. My foundation and concealer looks better when I dampen my sponge using any face mist.

  

As a face mist itself, I like it since it does not irritates my skin, tak rasa pedih, rasa soothing, kalau ada redness tu, maybe boleh berkurang. I like the soothing effect.

  

However, as a setting spray / makeup setting spray, nope. Dia macam spray air dekat makeup and macam rosakkan makeup. 

  

Also, dia tak ada lah hydrating sangat. Maybe because I have a really dry skin, kena spray selalu kalau nak. Hydrating effect dia tahan for 30 minutes to 1 hour gitu je. Pastu rasa biasa balik. Tapi kulit jadi lagi lembut lah.

  

Anyways, I bought this from Sephora online and biasalah Sephora punya packaging camne kan. sampai sampai je, penutup dia dah pecah. That's why togel je. Lepas ni kena fikir banyak kali kalau beli dengan Sephora online.

  

  

  

  

**Overall**

I like this, but I don't really love it. Biasa biasa je lah since the price is quite pricey jugak.

  

  

**Will I repurchase?**

Yes, kalau tengah sale. And kalau tak jumpa face mist yang lagi best.
